# Achievements Guide - Darkwood

## Guide Overview

This guide provides a comprehensive reference for all achievements obtainable in *Darkwood* (1.4.2). It is organized chronologically by the parts of the game (Prologue, Chapter 1, Chapter 2, and Epilogue) and highlights decision paths (Wolfman vs. Musician, Sow vs. Villagers) to help plan a completion run.

**Game**: Darkwood by Acid Wizard Studio  
**Total Achievements**: 15  

---

## Important Playthrough Notes

* **Decision Branches**: Several achievements are mutually exclusive in a single playthrough because they require picking opposite story paths (e.g., Wolfman's Path vs. Musician's Path). Saving and reloading backups of your save file (found in `%USERPROFILE%\AppData\LocalLow\Acid Wizard Studio\Darkwood\1_4Save`) can allow you to unlock mutually exclusive achievements in a single run.
* **Dream Sequences**: Achievements like **Sleep in the grave** and **No sleep for the wicked** occur in dream sequences. If you fail or make a mistake, force-close the game immediately (e.g., `Alt + F4`) to prevent the game from saving, allowing you to retry.

## Prologue Achievements

### Finished prologue
* **Description**: Play the prologue of the game and finish it.
* **Requirements**: Completing the prologue is story-mandated. Follow the scripted tutorial sequences as the Doctor and the Unconscious Man, and escape the starting house to unlock.

## Chapter 1 Achievements

### Kill the pig
* **Description**: Kill the sow.
* **Requirements**: You must execute the giant mutated sow inside the Pig Shed in the Silent Forest.
* **Step-by-Step Instructions**:
  1. Visit Piotrek's House in the Silent Forest.
  2. Purchase a **Cable** from Piotrek. Note that you may need to deal with a dog in his garage before he will speak and trade with you.
  3. Travel to the **Pig Shed** located in the Silent Forest.
  4. Find the small building inside the Pig Shed complex containing the generator and breaker panel.
  5. Interact with the cable to repair the severed connection.
  6. Turn on the generator.
  7. Go to the breaker panel, read the posted note, and remove the lock.
  8. Pull the main power lever all the way down to electrocute and kill the sow.
  * *Alternative*: You can kill the sow using ranged firearms or Molotov cocktails, but this will make all the pigs in the area permanently hostile.

### Sleep in the grave
* **Description**: Lie in the grave during the grave dream sequence.
* **Requirements**: Must be done during the grave dream sequence, which triggers after consuming a dose of tier 2 or tier 3 skills at the Oven.
* **Step-by-Step Instructions**:
  1. Once in the dream sequence, follow the linear path until you find a dead body, a shovel, and an open grave.
  2. Interact with the grave to select the option to lie down and rest.
  3. *Pro-Tip*: Once the achievement pops, press `Alt + F4` immediately to close the game. This prevents the dream sequence from finishing and saving, allowing you to reload and go for the alternative dream achievement **No sleep for the wicked**.

### No sleep for the wicked
* **Description**: Kill the black chomper during the grave dream sequence.
* **Requirements**: Successfully defeat the fast-moving black chomper in the grave dream.
* **Step-by-Step Instructions**:
  1. Trigger the grave dream sequence (similar to **Sleep in the grave**).
  2. Instead of lying in the grave, prepare to fight. A highly aggressive black chomper will ambush you.
  3. Defeat the chomper using your combat skills. 
  4. *Warning*: The black chomper is extremely fast. If you are about to die, immediately close the game using `Alt + F4` to reload and retry.

### Wolf's path
* **Description**: Choose the Wolfman's path in Chapter 1.
* **Requirements**: Deliver the key covered in chicken feces to the Wolfman.
* **Step-by-Step Instructions**:
  1. Obtain the **Key Covered in Chicken Feces** from the Chicken Lady's house in the Silent Forest.
  2. Deliver this key to the Wolfman at his camp.
  3. The key will disappear from your inventory. The Wolfman will agree to help you and will escort you to the Doctor's House in the Old Woods to search for clues.

### Musician's path
* **Description**: Choose the Musician's path in Chapter 1.
* **Requirements**: Deliver the key covered in chicken feces to the Musician.
* **Step-by-Step Instructions**:
  1. Retrieve the **Key Covered in Chicken Feces** from the Chicken Lady's house.
  2. Give the key to the Musician.
  3. The Musician will request a new violin. You must find it in the Creepy House located in the Old Woods.
  4. *Warning*: Do not place the drawing/letter in his mother's inventory, or she will transform into a chomper and ruin the quest.
  5. Meet the Musician at the Silo in the Silent Forest to advance his storyline and find the Doctor.

### Willpower
* **Description**: Survive and escape the doctor's drug-induced hallucination.
* **Requirements**: Complete the hallucination sequence at the train wreck.
* **Step-by-Step Instructions**:
  1. Ensure you have chosen the **Musician's Path**.
  2. Travel to the **Train Wreck** in the Old Woods.
  3. Enter the open metal door of the train car. The doors will lock and a gas trap will trigger, causing the Doctor to drug you.
  4. Once the hallucination begins, navigate quickly through the changing corridors, opening doors and running from chompers.
  5. Jump down the dark abyss at the end of the pathway to successfully escape the dream and unlock the achievement.

## Chapter 2 Achievements

### Chapter 2
* **Description**: Progress to the Swamp.
* **Requirements**: Story-mandated transition.
* **Step-by-Step Instructions**:
  1. Defeat or bypass the Doctor to obtain the key to the underground door.
  2. Enter the Underground Entrance in the Old Woods and unlock the heavy metal door.
  3. Traverse the tunnel to emerge in the Swamp, starting Chapter 2.

### Entertainer
* **Description**: Kill the Wolfman in Chapter 2.
* **Requirements**: Confront and defeat the Wolfman at the Sawmill.
* **Step-by-Step Instructions**:
  1. In Chapter 1, ignore the Wolfman's requests and do not give him the Chicken Lady's key.
  2. Upon entering Chapter 2, the Wolfman will steal items from your Workbench and leave a note.
  3. Travel to the **Sawmill** in the Swamp.
  4. When the Wolfman appears, engage him in combat and kill him to retrieve your stolen inventory. Note that you only have one attempt at this fight.

### Only ashes remain
* **Description**: Burn the Talking Tree in the Swamp.
* **Requirements**: Burn down the giant Talking Tree.
* **Step-by-Step Instructions**:
  1. Visit the Mushroom Granny and complete the Elephants' quest to obtain the **Oxygen Tank**.
  2. Retrieve compressor parts from the Junkyard (requires a Shovel) to repair the compressor and refill the tank.
  3. Navigate the Swamp Village and find the unblocked basement.
  4. Use the oxygen tank to bypass the gas and set fire to the roots of the Talking Tree.

### Insatiable hunger
* **Description**: Consume the Mushroom Granny.
* **Requirements**: Eat the Mushroom Granny in Chapter 2.
* **Step-by-Step Instructions**:
  1. Travel to the **Mushroom Glade** in the Swamp.
  2. Locate the Mushroom Granny.
  3. Interact with her and choose the prompt to eat her.

### Merciful
* **Description**: Spare the Talking Tree and escape the Swamp.
* **Requirements**: Leave the Swamp without burning the Talking Tree.
* **Step-by-Step Instructions**:
  1. Do not burn the Talking Tree in the Swamp Village.
  2. Navigate to the **Radio Tower** in the Swamp.
  3. Enter the Radio Tower Bunker, search the area, and survive the final dream sequence to unlock the metal door leading to the Road Home.

### Trapped villagers
* **Description**: Trap the quarry villagers using a boulder.
* **Requirements**: Block the entrance to the quarry village with a large stone.
* **Step-by-Step Instructions**:
  1. You must have killed the sow in Chapter 1 (**Kill the pig**) for the villagers to spawn at the quarry.
  2. Locate the **Quarry** in the Swamp.
  3. Destroy the wooden log supports holding back the large boulder at the quarry entrance. This rolls the boulder forward and seals off the village entrance.

## Epilogue Achievements

### Bliss
* **Description**: Sleep in your bed during the epilogue to accept the dream.
* **Requirements**: Complete the standard epilogue ending.
* **Step-by-Step Instructions**:
  1. Follow the Road Home to return to the Protagonist's suburban apartment block.
  2. Go to your apartment unit.
  3. Interact with your bed and select the option to go to sleep, accepting the warm illusion of the dream.

### Burn them all
* **Description**: Unlock the secret ending by burning the forest.
* **Requirements**: Locate the hidden Being and burn the roots of the forest.
* **Step-by-Step Instructions**:
  1. During the epilogue in the apartment block, check the mailboxes in the lobby for a postcard.
  2. Go to the apartment basement and retrieve the jar of **Mushroom Jelly**.
  3. Use a radio in the building and scan through the static channels until the audio signal cuts out.
  4. Go to the first floor and locate the door next to the drunk man. Walk away from it, and it will click open. Go inside and look closely at the fetus.
  5. Return to your apartment, get changed, and push the TV and couch aside.
  6. Examine the exposed floor roots, then move the rug.
  7. Retrieve the tearing tool from the kitchen and rip up the floor boards leading to the bedroom.
  8. Inspect the bed and look underneath it. **Important**: When prompted, do NOT click the option to leave/sleep.
  9. Move the bed completely aside and jump down the hole.
  10. Walk through the tunnels until you reach the chamber of **The Being**. Pull your hand away from it when prompted.
  11. Walk southwest of The Being to find a sleeping man. Forcefully take his **Flamethrower**.
  12. Fire the flamethrower at the surrounding roots to burn down the forest and trigger the secret ending.
