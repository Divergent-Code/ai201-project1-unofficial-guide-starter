# Alan Wake II: Collectibles and Trophies Guide

This guide covers all the collectibles, items, documents, and trophies found throughout Alan Wake II, including the base game and its DLCs (Night Springs and The Lake House).

## Items and Upgrades

### Weapons

**Saga's Weapons (Upgraded with Manuscript Fragments):**
*   **Pistol:** Standard issue FBI sidearm. Equipped by Saga at the start.
*   **Sawed-off Shotgun:** Break-action shotgun with wide pellet spread. Found in a locked cabinet in the General Store.
*   **Crossbow:** Silent hunting weapon. Found in a Cult Stash near the Break Room on the Suomi Hiking Trail.
*   **Hunting Rifle:** Bolt-action rifle. High accuracy and range. Found in the cabinet in Blum's Workshop in the Wellness Center.
*   **Pump-Action Shotgun:** Semiautomatic shotgun. Found in the Sheriff's Station.

**Alan's Weapons (Upgraded with Words of Power):**
*   **Revolver:** Six-shot pistol. Found in the alley in the Dark Place.
*   **Flare Gun:** Single-shot weapon firing a brightly burning flare. Found on the set of the TV Musical.
*   **Double-Barrelled Shotgun:** Break-action shotgun. Briefly equipped by Alan while fleeing Elderwood Palace Lodge, acquired permanently behind the bar outside the Oceanview Hotel.

### Consumables
*   **Healing Items:** Pain Killers (restores a small amount quickly, replenishes over time), Trauma Pad (restores a good amount), First Aid Kit (restores health fully).
*   **Offensive Items:** Hand Flare (emits intense light), Propane Tank (Saga only, detonates when shot), Rocket Flare (Saga only, single-shot flare), Flashbang (emits intense burst of light).
*   **Miscellaneous:** Battery Pack (for charging the Flashlight).

### Charms (Saga)
There are 18 charms total, with 14 being unique. Equipping three at once unlocks a trophy.
1.  **Logan's Charm:** Increases maximum health. (Starts with game)
2.  **Hammer Charm:** Attacks stagger enemies more often. (Cauldron Lake previously flooded area puzzle)
3.  **Coffee Mug Charm:** Saves you from death but shatters. (Multiple puzzles)
4.  **Kalevala Knights Charm:** Increases Hand Flare duration and area of effect. (Private Cabin puzzle)
5.  **Deer Charm:** Increases resilience against being staggered. (Radio Tower puzzle)
6.  **Coffee World Token Charm:** Increases quality and quantity of resources found. (Lighthouse Trailer Park puzzle)
7.  **Lighthouse Charm:** Increases max amount of health restored in Safe Havens. (Watery Lighthouse puzzle)
8.  **Mr. Drippy Charm:** Increases damage dealt when low on health. (Latte Lagoon puzzle)
9.  **FBC Charm:** Increases damage dealt while undetected by enemies. (Ranger Cabin puzzle)
10. **Lantern Charm:** Adds one Charge to Saga's Flashlight. (Bunker Woods puzzle)
11. **Valhalla Nursing Home Charm:** Increases effectiveness of Painkillers and Trauma Pads. (Ranger Station puzzle)
12. **Anchor Charm:** Chance to stun enemy on Flashlight Boost. (Billie's Boat Yard puzzle)
13. **Mayor Setter Charm:** Marks all nearby resources and points of interest on Saga's map. (Pet Mayor Setter in Suomi Hall)
14. **Deerfest Charm:** Significantly increases time before Darkness Shields regenerate. (Lighthouse Trailer Park trailer #5 puzzle)

## Return Collectibles (Saga)

### Manuscript Pages
There are 50 Manuscript Pages found in Saga's storyline, plus 6 exclusive to Final Draft mode.
*   **Invitation:** 4 pages.
*   **The Heart:** 10 pages.
*   **Local Girl:** 10 pages.
*   **No Chance:** 4 pages.
*   **Old Gods:** 14 pages.
*   **Scratch:** 4 pages.
*   **Summoning:** 3 pages.
*   **Come Home:** 1 page.

### Cult Stashes
There are 22 Cult Stashes containing ammo and consumables. Finding all 22 rewards the Lighthouse Key.
*   **Invitation:** 3 stashes (Crime scene trailer, Elderwood Palace Lodge van, Cauldron Lake shore).
*   **The Heart:** 2 stashes (Cauldron Lake previously flooded area, Streamside).
*   **Local Girl:** 7 stashes (Suomi Hiking Path, Latte Lagoon, Lighthouse Trailer Park, Watery Lighthouse, Downtown Watery).
*   **No Chance:** 2 stashes (Bunker Woods, abandoned pickup near Valhalla Nursing Home).
*   **Old Gods:** 5 stashes (Ranger Station, Bright Falls Harbour, Oh Deer Diner park).
*   **Scratch:** 3 stashes (Sheriff's Station Evidence Room, Bright Falls park, Watery docks).

### Alex Casey Lunch Boxes
There are 21 lunch boxes containing Manuscript Fragments to upgrade Saga's weapons.
*   **Invitation:** 4 boxes (Mortar Falls, General Store path, Cauldron Lake campsite, Witch's Hut path).
*   **The Heart:** 4 boxes (Cauldron Lake FBC station, Bright Falls park, Witchfinder's Station, Streamside stream).
*   **Local Girl:** 6 boxes (Radio Tower, Coffee World tent, Coffee World ticket booth, Lighthouse Trailer Park playground, Kalevala Knights Workshop, Watery Lighthouse).
*   **No Chance:** 1 box (Bunker Woods).
*   **Old Gods:** 6 boxes (Valhalla Nursing Home Admin Office, woods southwest of Nursing Home, Ranger Station path, Ranger Station beach, Billie's Boat Yard, Rental Cabins).

### Nursery Rhyme Puzzles & Dolls
There are 17 puzzles requiring specific arrangements of 13 Nursery Rhyme Dolls.
*   **The Heart:** 4 Puzzles (Cauldron Lake flooded area, Witchfinder's Station, Private Cabin).
*   **Local Girl:** 7 Puzzles (Suomi Hiking Trail, Radio Tower, Lighthouse Trailer Park, Watery Lighthouse, Latte Lagoon, Ranger Cabin, Downtown Watery).
*   **Old Gods:** 6 Puzzles (Bunker Woods campsite, Ranger Station, Ranger Station shoreline, Billie's Boat Yard, Lighthouse Trailer Park trailer #5, Rental Cabins).
*   **Dolls:** Crow, Hero, Wolf, Moose, Deer, Wise Elder, Bear, Mother, Child, Trickster, Maiden, Monster, Father.

### Deer Heads
There are 12 Deer Heads. Petting all 12 unlocks a secret room back in the Elderwood Palace Lodge.

### Mayor Setter Signs
There are 6 election signs to examine around Bright Falls and Watery to unlock the Mayor Setter Charm.

### Pat Maine Radio Shows
There are 7 radio shows to listen to on radios found in the environment.

### Koskela Ads
There are 6 televisions that play adverts for the Koskela brothers' enterprises (Adventure Tours, Coffee World, Ahma Beer, Parade Floats, Bright Falls Blend, "Return" Review).

## Initiation Collectibles (Alan)

### Words of Power
Words of Power upgrade Alan's abilities. There are 28 in total found throughout the Dark Place.
*   **Words of Fix (5):** Healing and health regeneration.
*   **Words of Gun (4):** Weapon ammo efficiency and damage.
*   **Words of War (4):** Significant weapon damage upgrades.
*   **Words of Stuff (2):** Inventory expansion and resource marking.
*   **Words of Aid (4):** Effectiveness of healing items and flares.
*   **Words of Action (4):** Stealth and damage output in specific conditions.
*   **Words of Lamp (5):** Flashlight efficiency and health recovery on boost.

**Locations:**
*   **Casey:** 1 Word
*   **We Sing:** 1 Word
*   **Room 665:** 3 Words
*   **Zane's Film:** 4 Words

### Suitcase Stashes
Sheriff Tim Breaker leaves 13 supply stashes around the Dark Place, usually hidden and containing ammo, flares, and health items.

### Echoes
Audio clips Alan aligns from the environment to gain context.
*   **Draft One:** 4 Echoes (The Manuscript, Caldera St. Station, Bad Luck, Torchbearers).
*   **Draft Two:** 5 Echoes (Another Murder, Reception, Haunted, Room 666, Quid Pro Quo).
*   **Draft Three:** 6 Echoes (Thomas Zane, Crooked Cops, Clip of the Lost Film, Death of a Cultist, Trapped, Casey Billboards).

### Writer's Journey Videos
9 television broadcasts reflecting on Alan's writing process.

## Miscellaneous Collectibles

### Maps
There are 11 maps (6 for Saga, 5 for Alan) to find in the game to assist with navigation.

### Songs
There are 14 songs by Various Artists and 6 songs by the Old Gods of Asgard to unlock and play in the Mind Place/Writer's Room.

There are 37 documents throughout the Lake House DLC that count towards the "Floor by Floor" trophy.
*   **Ground Floor:** 10 documents (including Security Reports, Oldest House updates, and Marmont's files).
*   **Sublevel 1 (Painting Production):** 3 documents.
*   **Sublevel 3 (Page Development):** 5 documents.
*   **Sublevel 2 (Holding & Observation):** 10 documents.
*   **Sublevel 1 (2nd Visit):** 3 documents.
*   **Sublevel 4 (Research Archives):** 6 documents.
