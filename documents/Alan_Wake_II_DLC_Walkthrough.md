# Alan Wake II DLC Walkthrough

**Author**: chris-williams (Christopher Williams)  
**Associated Game**: Alan Wake II (2023) — DLC Expansions  
**Developer**: Remedy Entertainment

## 1. Night Springs Expansion

*Night Springs* is the first expansion for *Alan Wake II*. It consists of three standalone episodes penned by Alan Wake as he attempts to write his escape from the Dark Place. Access the expansions from the "Expansions" option on the game's main menu.

---

### Episode 1: Number One Fan

In this episode, you play as the **Waitress** (Everyone's Favorite Waitress, modeled after Rose Marigold), who must rescue her beloved **Writer** (Alan Wake) from his evil twin brother (the **Bad Boy**) and his army of Haters.

#### Nite's Diner
**Episode 1 Collectibles Checklist (Diner)**:
*   **Weapons**: 2 (Hunting Rifle, Automatic Shotgun)

##### Attend to Your Customers
1.  **Serve Coffee**: Grab the coffee pot from behind the counter. Walk around the diner and interact with the customers at the booths to top up their coffee cups.
2.  **Collect Plates**: Return the coffee pot. Walk around the tables again, collecting the dirty plates and dispensing advice. Place the plates in the service window.
3.  **Find the Writer's Book**: Speak to the man sitting by the jukebox. He asks for a book recommendation. Go to the rear corridor and enter the first door on the right. Grab a copy of the Writer's book from the desk.
4.  **Receive the Distress Call**: Speak to the Billy Bass plaque on the wall. The Writer speaks through the plaque, warning you that he is in trouble. (First message: progress toward the **I Could Listen to Him All Day** trophy).
5.  **Arm Yourself**: Go to the kitchen and enter the employees-only storeroom. Collect the **Hunting Rifle** and **Automatic Shotgun** from the wall mount (unlocks the **Accessorize!** trophy), along with rifle rounds, shotgun shells, two Leaky Propane Tanks, and four healing pills. Note that the shotgun fires in full auto by holding R2.

---

#### The Boatyard & Woods
##### Defeat the Haters
Leave the diner and jog down the street. Open the gate at the end of the road to enter the Boatyard:
1.  **Confront the Bad Boy**: Speak to the twin brother. He will ride off on his motorcycle, leaving his army of Haters to attack you.
2.  **Axe Thrower**: Immediately look up to spot an axe thrower on the raised platform. Shoot the red gas canister next to him to detonate it.
3.  **Group Detonation**: Kite the oncoming waves of enemies near the red propane canisters in the yard. Blow up the canisters when enemies are close; catching three in a single blast unlocks a related combat trophy. Defeat the remaining Haters.
4.  **Follow the Motorcycle Trail**: Go through the broken fence and follow the burning tire tracks left by the Bad Boy's motorcycle. Collect supplies from the emergency cabinets along the trail.
5.  **The Hiking Trail Bridge**: When you approach the wooden bridge, use the hunting rifle to take out the axe thrower on the right-hand platform before crossing. Fend off the three Haters near the hut. Grab supplies inside the hut, then defeat the next pair of enemies.
6.  **The Shrine**: When the trail forks, take the left path. Follow it down to a second fork, then go right to find a shrine dedicated to the Writer. Read all three pages of the open book to receive his second message.
7.  **The Cabin**: Follow the path to a cabin. Go up the steps and enter. Go upstairs to read the Bad Boy's plan notes and poetry.
8.  **The Beach**: Walk down to the beach to find the Bad Boy. Demand that he return the Writer. He reveals he was stalling you while his Haters stormed the Writer's mansion. Defeat the Haters that emerge from the water.
9.  **The Deer Vision**: Walk back to the cabin stairs to trigger a cutscene where a mounted deer speaks to you in the Writer's voice (unlocks the **I Could Listen to Him All Day** trophy).

---

#### The Writer's Mansion
##### Defeat the Bad Boy Boss
Climb the trail to the mansion gates. Confront the Bad Boy:
*   **Phase 1**: The twin has two main attacks: a force wave that knocks you down and floating energy hearts that track you. Dodge the force wave and shoot the hearts before they reach you. Blast him with the shotgun.
*   **Phase 2**: After taking damage, he will transform into a werewolf. Evade his lunges and shoot him.
*   **Phase 3**: The twin will summon his motorcycle, which also transforms into a wolf. Eliminate the motorcycle wolf first (it has low health), then focus your fire on the twin's wolf form until he is defeated.

Exhaust your dialogue options with the twin. Enter the mansion. Defeat the Hater that breaks through the front door. Fend off the two heavy Bruisers on the stairs. Navigate to the third-floor landing and defeat the final group of Haters. Open the door marked with a spiral to rescue the Writer (unlocks the **Love Always Wins!** trophy).

### Episode 2: North Star

In this episode, you play as the **Sibling** (modeled after Jesse Faden from *Control*), guided by a warm presence (**Polaris**) to find her missing brother (**Dylan**), who is being held by a government agency inside Coffee World.

#### Coffee World Gates
##### Gain Entry
You start outside the locked gates of Coffee World:
1.  **Logbook Clue**: Walk to the ticket booth and examine the admissions logbook. Note that the number **238** is highlighted in green.
2.  **Unlock the Gate**: Enter **238** on the gate keypad to open it.
3.  **Meet the Sheriff**: Walk toward the central gazebo. Tim Breaker (the Sheriff) is inside. Speak to him to learn that the agency is abducting people to a nearby warehouse and putting an anomalous substance in the coffee.
4.  **Equip Weapons**: Collect the service pistol and flashlight from the case on the bench, and read the notes.

---

#### Find the Six Stashes
You must locate six hidden FBC stashes around Coffee World to gather clues and keys. Each stash uses a keypad sequence lock:
1.  **Stash #1**: Look past the gazebo to find the small storage building. Search the back wall outside to find the chest. Input the flashing button sequence.
2.  **Stash #2**: Walk to the path to the right of the gazebo and head toward the Welcome Center. Defeat the two Taken (burn shields). Walk to the Percolator ride to find the stash on the left.
3.  **Stash #3**: Return to the Welcome Center. Walk along the path to the left of the stalls, turn right at the portable toilets, and open the stash inside the shed by Latte Lagoon.
4.  **Stash #4**: Return to the Welcome Center and climb the steps. Follow the signs toward the Tasting Room, then head right. Defeat the three Taken. The stash is next to the children's ride.
5.  **Stash #5**: Walk to the steps leading toward the Slow Roaster Ferris wheel. The stash is on the left next to the portable toilets.
6.  **Stash #6**: Walk down the path from the Tasting Room and turn right at the fork. Walk down to the Huotari Well. Burn away the three darkness nodes blocking the path and defeat the Taken. Go behind the red pickup truck to find the final stash (unlocks the **Percolate** trophy).
7.  **Intercom Security Question**: Go to the warehouse gate intercom. It demands a passkey or the answer to a security question: *What is the fourth word of Dark Triangle Coffee's mission statement?*

---

#### Get Inside the Warehouse
##### The Orientation Video
1.  **Ferris Wheel Key**: Walk to the gazebo and speak to the Sheriff. He reveals the tape is hidden in cup #4 of the Ferris wheel and hands you the **Ferris Wheel Control Key**.
2.  **Retrieve the Tape**: Go to the Slow Roaster controls. Use the key, start the wheel, and stop it when cup #4 reaches the bottom. Grab the **Orientation Video Tape**.
3.  **Play the Tape**: Go to the Welcome Center TV and insert the tape. The mission statement is: *To drink the truth is to become one*. The fourth word is **truth**.
4.  **Enter the Intercom Code**: Return to the warehouse intercom. Input **truth** as the answer. The system will then ask for the correct roasting temperature for the beans.

##### Calculate the Roasting Temperature
1.  **Counter Clue**: Walk to the Roasting Bar. Examine the counter notes and posters:
    *   The temperature is greater than the sum of a triangle's angles (180).
    *   The temperature is less than 500.
    *   The first digit is half of the second.
    *   The third digit is one less than the second digit (meaning it is one less than the one before it).
    *   The temperature is a prime number.
    *   Possible digits: First digit must be 2, 3, or 4 (since temperature is > 180 and < 500, and must be half of the second). 
    *   If first is 4, second is 8, third is 7 (487). 487 is prime.
    *   Enter **487** on the keypad to unlock the **Dark as the Void** trophy.
2.  **Taste Test**: Input **487** on the intercom. The system demands that you try the coffee. Go to the Tasting Room, defeat the Taken, go inside, and drink the coffee. Emerge and interact with the intercom again. Deposit your weapons in the bin to unlock the warehouse doors.

##### Navigate the Warehouse
Step inside. Avoid the patrolling Coffee Mascots; they are invulnerable and will kill you instantly if spotted:
1.  **Stealth Path**: Hide behind the coffee sack barrier as the first mascot passes, then sneak left.
2.  **Warehouse Office Key**: Go to the right side of the warehouse and collect the key from the red toolbox on the desk.
3.  **Dylan Faden**: Return past the blue lockers, use the key on the office door, and go inside without being spotted (unlocks the **Smooth Blend** trophy). 
4.  Go through the back office door, go down the ladder behind the desk, and walk down the corridor to find Dylan (unlocks the **You Are Coffee** trophy).

### Episode 3: Time Breaker

In this episode, you play as the actor **Shawn** (Shawn Ashmore, playing a multiversal version of Tim Breaker) who must travel across realities to stop **Warlin Door** (the Master of Many Worlds).

#### The Game Studio
##### Speak to the Director
You start on the live-action game set. Speak to Sam, the director, to learn about the game's premise. Walk down the corridor to the Green Room. Look on the floor to find a copy of your own dead body:
1.  **Screenplay Pages**: Read the script pages scattered around the corpse to unlock a related trophy.
2.  **Reality Shifter**: Collect the **Time Breaker Solidifier** from the body. A red-headed woman warns you of danger before you are teleported.

---

#### The Sludge Forest
Walk along the linear forest path until you reach a warning sign about cross-dimensional anomalies. Cross the bridge and follow the trail:
1.  **Find the Energy Node**: Enter the trailer at the top of the slope. Note the Lumivista TV inside. Exit, walk down the steps, and turn left into the forest. Follow the trail to the creek, climb the right-hand slope, and hold the action button to charge your **Polyhedron Reality Shifter (PRS)** at the floating energy node.
2.  **Shift Reality**: Return to the trailer. Defeat the Taken that blocks your path. Stand in front of the TV inside and hold the action button to charge it with the PRS. Step into the TV screen vortex (unlocks the **Ride the Vortex** trophy).

---

#### Oceanview Hotel Loop
You emerge in the lobby of the Oceanview Hotel:
1.  **Lobby Clock Code**: Check the reception desk clocks: the labels indicate the correct doors to enter are **12**, **3**, and **9**:
    *   Walk down the hallway and enter the door marked **12 o'clock**.
    *   Emerge in the next hallway, walk to the end, and enter the door marked **3 o'clock**.
    *   Emerge in the third hallway, defeat the two enemies, and enter the door marked **9 o'clock**.
2.  **Collect Energy Node**: Charge your PRS at the energy node in the hallway. Go through the **12 o'clock** door to return to the TV lobby. Defeat the three shadows, use the PRS on the TV, and step through the portal.

---

#### Alternative Realities
1.  **Comic Book Reality**: You find yourself in a comic strip. Click through the panels and speak to the red-headed woman. She explains that Door turns alternate versions of you into mindless "Time Breakers."
2.  **Arcade Reality**: Emerge in a 2D side-scrolling shooter. Defeat five waves of enemies. Walk right to the energy node, charge your PRS, and use it on the TV to exit (unlocks the **Retro** trophy).
3.  **The Desert**: Walk through the pink desert landscape towards the energy node. Defeat the four shadows, charge your PRS, and continue to the TV. Defeat the five shadows that attack, use your PRS, and step through.
4.  **Text Adventure Reality**: Complete the text adventure prompts in order:
    *   `>FOCUS`
    *   `>HOLD YOURSELF TOGETHER`
    *   `>FOCUS ON THE THOUGHT`
    *   `>DECIPHER THE WORDS`
    *   `>IMAGINE A CUP OF COFFEE`
    *   `>ASSESS YOUR SITUATION`
    *   `>FOCUS ON THE MASTER OF MANY WORLDS.`
    *   `>MEDITATE TO LEARN`
    *   `>FORCE YOURSELF TO CONTINUE`
    *   `>CHANNEL YOUR MIND`
    *   `>TAKE THE SEED`
    *   `>PLANT THE SEED`
    *   `>HANG YOURSELF FROM IT`
    *   `>PICK UP THE FRUIT`
    *   `>OFFER THE SEED`
    *   `>PICK UP THE BRANCH`
    *   `>USE THE BRANCH ON THE DOOR`
    *   `>STEP OVER THE THRESHOLD`
5.  Confront Warlin Door to complete the episode (unlocks the **Multiversal Agent** trophy).

## 2. The Lake House Expansion

In *The Lake House*, you play as FBC Agent **Kiran Estevez**, investigating a research facility at Cauldron Lake that has succumbed to the Dark Presence. 

### Ground Floor (Lobby)
**Lake House Document Checklist**: 37 documents (unlocks the **Floor by Floor** trophy)

##### Search the Lobby
Click on the gate and use your FBC ID card to enter. Walk down the path and cross the bridge. Use your ID card at the intercom to enter the lobby:
1.  **Document (1/37 - "URGENT REMINDER")**: Read the note on the reception desk.
2.  **Document (2/37 - "SECURITY REPORT")**: Read the report next to it.
3.  **Document (3/37 - "STATUS UPDATE")**: Read the file next to the computer.
4.  **Lake House Map**: Collect the map from the table.
5.  **Marmont's PC Passcode**: Check the desk:
    *   Read the `REPLACEMENT KEYCARD REQUEST FORM` and `RESEARCH PROFILE RE: "SUBJECT P"` (4/37 & 5/37).
    *   Read `UPDATE TO PASSWORD RESET POLICY` and `SCIENCE FACT OF THE DAY` (Hedy Lamarr age = 38).
    *   Check the Notice of Appointment: July 18 (represented as 18/07).
    *   Enter **180738** on Marmont's computer to unlock it (unlocks **Hard Work** trophy progress).
6.  **Document (6/37 - "To my new fellow Head")**: Read the plaque on the bust.
7.  **Document (7/37 - notice of appointment)**: Read the framed certificates.
8.  **Clearance Keycard**: Use the computer to unlock the security office. Go inside and collect the **Security Clearance 01 Keycard**.

Ride the lift to Sublevel 1.

---

### Sublevel 1 (Painting Production)
1.  **Document (8/37 - "THRESHOLD LINKING EXPERIMENT")**: Go into the office and read the report and research order on the desk.
2.  **Document (9/37 - "Progress Update #6")**: Read the note on the counter.
3.  **Restore Power**: Grab the **Power Core** from the table. Insert it into the Receptor in the top-left corner and pull the lever to restore backup power (unlocks the **Fault in the System** trophy).
4.  **Shift Paintings**: Go to the Panel Control Room and use the console switch to slide the paintings.
5.  **Painted Encounters**: Painted enemies will emerge from the walls. You cannot kill them yet; stun them with a **Flashbang** (unlocks a combat trophy) and run to the lift. Select Floor -2. The lift will fail and open at Sublevel 2.

---

### Sublevel 2 (Holding & Observation)
1.  **Talk to the Painting**: Walk down the corridor and interact with the large canvas to speak to the entity.
2.  **Sublevel 3**: Ride the lift to Sublevel 3. Go to the ATD Chief Technician office and read `REFINEMENT PROCESS` (10/37).
3.  **ATD Output Evaluation**: Go across to the office and read `Rudolf Lane stood back from the canvas` and `Diana and Jules Marmont` (11/37 & 12/37). Read `ORGANIZATIONAL UPDATE` (13/37).
4.  **Projector Room**: Go to the end of the corridor and read `INITIAL FINDINGS REPORT` (14/37 & 15/37) next to the projector.
5.  **ATD Archive**: Go to the Production Archive (top-right) and read `PROGRESS REPORT` (16/37).
6.  **ATD Maintenance**: Go to Maintenance and Repair (bottom-left) and read `RESEARCH NOTES` (17/37).
7.  **Get the Shotgun**: Exit the room. Collect the shotgun from the floor next to the dead guards.
8.  **ATD Chamber**: Proceed through the doors. Defeat the Taken and the Thrower. Burn the nodes.
9.  **Power Core Loop**: Walk to the door on the right. Swipe your keycard, flip the switch to clear the pipes, and grab the **Power Core**. Insert it into the receptor, restore power, and return to the ATD Chamber. Flip the gantry switch to move the panels. 
10. Talk to the painting again, then return to the lift. Select Floor -2.

---

### Sublevel 2 (Second Visit)
1.  **Jules Marmont Presentation**: Open the Subject Observation room using the Level 3 keycard. Play the projector presentation to the end (unlocks the **Shape of a Man** trophy). Defeat the Painted that spawns.
2.  **Document (31/37 - "Status of Project Arbutus")**: Read the file on the Interview Room desk.

Ride the lift to Sublevel 3.

---

### Sublevel 3 (Second Visit)
1.  Defeat the two Painted. Enter Office 3.1 and read `Water dripped from the ceiling` on the desk. Watch the presentation in the Conference Room.

Ride the lift to Sublevel 4.

---

### Sublevel 4 (Research Archives)
1.  **Rudolf Lane**: Walk to the end of the corridor to speak to the painting. He reveals he is Rudolf Lane. Collect the `Rudolf Lane was dying` document (32/37) from the floor.
2.  **Navigate the Archives**: Go to Sublevel 4. Hug the right-hand wall to navigate the shifting maze. Defeat the two Painted. Find `Diana sat in her office` (33/37) on the M725 filing cabinet.
3.  **Taken Combat**: Defeat the normal Taken, Bruiser, and Thrower that attack in sequence. Using the launcher to kill them unlocks the **Less Carrot, More Stick** trophy.

---

### Sublevel 5 (Shut Down)
1.  **Final Documents**: Read `ANNUAL REPORT` and `RESEARCH MANDATE` (34/37 & 35/37) on the cabinets. Go into the office and read `THE DEFINITION OF ART` and `FOLLOW UP REPORT` (36/37 & 37/37) (completes the document checklist; unlocks the **Floor by Floor** trophy).
2.  **Final PC Passcode**: Enter **685298** on the office computer (unlocks the **Hard Work** trophy). Flick the switch. Jules Marmont will emerge and vanish.
3.  **Oceanview Motel**: Go back to the office. Pull the ceiling cord three times to enter the Oceanview Motel. Walk to the end of the hall and enter the door to find the Panopticon. Speak to **P6** (Dylan Faden). Return to the office.
4.  **Shut Down the Experiment**: Go to the lift and select Floor -5. Approach the red light. 
5.  **Diana Marmont Boss Fight**: 
    *   Use your torch to burn away Diana's darkness shield, then blast her with the shotgun.
    *   When she teleports to the upper ledge, switch to the Black Rock Launcher to defeat the Painted that spawns.
    *   Dodge her lunges, keep firing, and activate the shut down switch to conclude the fight.
6.  Rudolf Lane protests. Pull the switch to trigger the ending cutscene. Kiran Estevez escapes the facility and joins Ed Booker outside.
    *   **Trophy Unlocked**: **Looking for Release** (automatic for completing the expansion).
    *   **Song Unlocked**: **Petri Alanko - End of an Era** plays.
