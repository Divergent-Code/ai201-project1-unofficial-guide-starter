# Alien: Isolation Blueprint and Collectibles Guide

## Guide Overview

This guide provides a complete reference for all collectible items in Alien: Isolation. It covers all 21 crafting blueprints (7 item types at 3 upgrade tiers each), all 10 Nostromo Logs, ID Tag locations, crafting material reference, and achievement requirements.

**Author**: Raycon  
**Game**: Alien: Isolation (2014) by Creative Assembly / SEGA

---

## Important: Tools Required for Full Collection

Several collectibles are locked behind access barriers that require specific tools to open. You cannot collect everything in a single pass without these tools.

| Tool | How Acquired | Opens |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Level 1 Access Tuner** | Mission 3 (found on floor) | Yellow-marked Level 1 doors |
| **Ion Torch (basic)** | Mission 5 (found in maintenance area) | Welded doors (basic seal) |
| **Level 2 Security Bypass** | Mission 10 (given by Marshal Waits) | Orange-marked Level 2 doors |
| **Ion Torch (upgraded)** | Mission 13 (found in engineering) | Welded doors (reinforced seal) |
| **Gas Mask** | Mission 14 (found in descent shaft area) | Gas-filled rooms without damage |
| **Level 3 Access Tuner** | Mission 16 (acquired at mission start) | Red-marked Level 3 doors |

> **Collection Strategy**: The most efficient approach is to collect what you can as you progress, then use **Mission 16** as a free-roam collection pass. By Mission 16 you have all tools. After completing Mission 16's main objective, backtracking becomes unavailable in many areas. Complete all collection before advancing the Mission 16 objective.

## Part 1: Blueprints

There are **21 blueprints** in Alien: Isolation — 7 craftable items, each with three upgrade tiers (V1, V2, V3). You must collect the lower tier before the higher tier will appear in its location. Collecting all 7 V1 blueprints and crafting at least one of each unlocks the **A True Engineer** achievement/trophy.

### Medkit Blueprints

#### Medkit V1
- **Mission**: Mission 2 — Welcome to Sevastopol
- **Location**: Departures Terminal area, supply room. On the shelf inside the first unlocked side room on the left side of the outer ring corridor. Door is accessible from the start.
- **Note**: This is one of the first items you can collect in the game. Do not miss it.
- **Effect**: Unlocks Medkit crafting. Restores a moderate amount of health.

#### Medkit V2
- **Mission**: Mission 10 — The Trap
- **Location**: Marshal Bureau supply room. Through the door to the left of the main reception desk.
- **Effect**: Improved Medkit — greater healing output per craft.

#### Medkit V3
- **Mission**: Mission 15 — The Message
- **Location**: Secure storage section's medical supply room. On the top shelf. Room is unlocked.
- **Effect**: Maximum Medkit — highest healing output in the game.

---

### EMP Mine Blueprints

#### EMP Mine V1
- **Mission**: Mission 4 — Seegson Communications
- **Location**: Communications control room back office. Inside the equipment locker. Access the back office from the mezzanine level.
- **Note**: Essential for android encounters. Prioritize crafting EMP Mines over all other items until Mission 7.
- **Effect**: Unlocks EMP Mine crafting. Stuns androids and disables electronics in a small radius.

#### EMP Mine V2
- **Mission**: Mission 7 — Seegson Synthetics
- **Location**: Assembly floor supervisor workstation. In the raised glass-walled office overlooking the manufacturing floor. Navigate the android patrols on the floor to reach it.
- **Effect**: Upgraded blast radius — stuns androids in a wider area.

#### EMP Mine V3
- **Mission**: Mission 12 — A Synthetic Solution
- **Location**: Lorenz Systech Spire, fourth floor IT room. On the server rack area. Requires navigating past the Spire's alert-mode Working Joes.
- **Effect**: Maximum blast radius and stun duration. The definitive android counter-tool.

---

### Noisemaker Blueprints

#### Noisemaker V1
- **Mission**: Mission 4 — Seegson Communications
- **Location**: Save station room near the relay area. On the table next to the APOLLO terminal in the pre-relay corridor.
- **Note**: Collect this immediately — Noisemakers are useful from their first availability onward.
- **Effect**: Unlocks Noisemaker crafting. Thrown device that emits sound to distract the Alien.

#### Noisemaker V2
- **Mission**: Mission 9 — Beacon
- **Location**: Gemini Exoplanet Solutions lab, first research floor supply room. On a shelf inside the supply room adjacent to the research corridor.
- **Effect**: Extended distraction duration and wider sound radius — draws the Alien from a greater distance.

#### Noisemaker V3
- **Mission**: Mission 16 — Transmission
- **Location**: Communications ring outer section. In a locker room accessible via Level 3 Access Tuner. Requires the Level 3 tuner acquired at the start of Mission 16.
- **Effect**: Maximum distraction range and duration. The Alien will investigate for a significantly longer period before returning to hunt.

---

### Smoke Bomb Blueprints

#### Smoke Bomb V1
- **Mission**: Mission 3 — Encounters
- **Location**: Tech Support area, accessible early in the mission. On a shelf in the Tech Support side room.
- **Note**: Some sources place this in Mission 5 — check both areas if it does not appear in Mission 3.
- **Effect**: Unlocks Smoke Bomb crafting. Obscures android line-of-sight and provides limited concealment from humans.

#### Smoke Bomb V2
- **Mission**: Mission 11 — Hazard Containment
- **Location**: Hazard corridor maintenance alcove. On the supply shelf, approximately two-thirds of the way through the hazard corridor section.
- **Effect**: Improved obscurement duration.

#### Smoke Bomb V3
- **Mission**: Mission 17 — Desolation
- **Location**: Reactor antechamber equipment locker. Past the debris field in the reactor access corridor.
- **Effect**: Maximum duration and smoke density.

---

### Flashbang Blueprints

#### Flashbang V1
- **Mission**: Mission 3 — Encounters
- **Location**: System Monitoring hallway, in the storage closet off the main hallway. Requires the Level 1 Access Tuner to open.
- **Effect**: Unlocks Flashbang crafting. Temporarily blinds and stuns human survivors and androids. Minimal effect on the Alien.

#### Flashbang V2
- **Mission**: Mission 14 — The Descent
- **Location**: Descent shaft, fifth platform level. On a supply shelf. Collect only if the motion tracker confirms the Alien is not on the same platform.
- **Effect**: Extended stun duration on androids.

#### Flashbang V3
- **Mission**: Mission 18 — Tomorrow, Together
- **Location**: Upper maintenance section supply cabinet. Near the APOLLO neural interface terminal where Samuels interfaces.
- **Effect**: Maximum stun duration and wider blind radius.

---

### Pipe Bomb Blueprints

#### Pipe Bomb V1
- **Mission**: Mission 5 — The Quarantine
- **Location**: Near the save station in the quarantine zone. On a table in the maintenance room adjacent to the APOLLO terminal.
- **Note**: Pipe Bombs are high-damage explosives effective against groups of humans. They have marginal effect on the Alien.
- **Effect**: Unlocks Pipe Bomb crafting.

#### Pipe Bomb V2
- **Mission**: Mission 13 — Consultation
- **Location**: Engineering section maintenance bay. On the workbench in the bay.
- **Effect**: Larger blast radius.

#### Pipe Bomb V3
- **Mission**: Mission 16 — Transmission
- **Location**: Engineering section secure locker room. Behind a **Level 3 locked door** — requires the Level 3 Access Tuner acquired at the start of Mission 16. Backtrack to the engineering section during Mission 16's free-roam window.
- **Effect**: Maximum explosive radius. Most effective against clustered human enemies.

---

### Molotov Blueprints

#### Molotov V1
- **Mission**: Mission 6 — The Outbreak
- **Location**: Security desk near the quarantine zone transit area.
- **Note**: Molotovs create fire patches that cause the Alien to rapidly retreat. They do not kill the Alien but are among the most effective deterrents available.
- **Effect**: Unlocks Molotov crafting.

#### Molotov V2
- **Mission**: Mission 11 — Hazard Containment
- **Location**: San Cristobal Medical Facility medical wing. On the supply cart next to the surgical bed in the treatment room.
- **Effect**: Larger fire area of effect.

#### Molotov V3
- **Mission**: Mission 16 — Transmission
- **Location**: Lorenz Systech Spire, behind a **Level 3 locked door** accessible during Mission 16's backtracking window.
- **Effect**: Maximum fire radius and burn duration.

## Part 2: Nostromo Logs

There are **10 Nostromo Logs** in Alien: Isolation. Collecting all 10 unlocks the **Archivist** achievement/trophy. All logs are audio recordings from the crew of the USCSS Nostromo — they tell the story of the original Alien film from multiple crew members' perspectives and provide context for Amanda's mission.

> **Important**: Many Nostromo Logs require the Ion Torch (upgraded), Gas Mask, or Level 3 Access Tuner to reach. Logs 003, 006, 007, and 008 in particular are only safely accessible in Mission 16 when you have all tools. Mission Select can be used to return for missed logs after completing the campaign.

### Nostromo Log Detailed Locations

#### Log 001 — An Initial Report
- **Area**: Seegson Communications Relay Facility
- **Mission**: Mission 4
- **Specific Location**: On the desk inside the relay facility's supervisor office. The office is unlocked and accessible during the normal mission path.
- **Notes**: One of the easiest logs to collect. Do not miss it during Mission 4.

#### Log 002 — Science Officer Reporting
- **Area**: Lorenz Systech Spire — Workplace Solutions floor
- **Mission**: Mission 10 or revisit during Mission 16
- **Specific Location**: On an executive desk on the Lorenz Systech Spire's mid-level office floor. The area is accessible from Mission 10 onward.
- **Notes**: Collectible during normal Mission 10 progression if you explore the Spire offices.

#### Log 003 — Delayed Pick-Up
- **Area**: Lorenz Systech Spire — Server Room
- **Mission**: Mission 16 (recommended)
- **Specific Location**: In the server room on the Spire's restricted floor. The server room has a gas-filled antechamber — the Gas Mask is required to enter without taking continuous damage.
- **Tool Required**: Gas Mask

#### Log 004 — Overworked and Underpaid
- **Area**: Sevastopol SciMed Tower
- **Mission**: Mission 12 or Mission 16 backtrack
- **Specific Location**: In a locked room in the SciMed Tower requiring a welded door opened with the upgraded Ion Torch.
- **Tool Required**: Ion Torch (upgraded)

#### Log 005 — System Check Went Okay
- **Area**: San Cristobal Medical Facility
- **Mission**: Mission 15 or Mission 16 backtrack
- **Specific Location**: In a restricted section of the medical facility that requires either the Ion Torch or Gas Mask to safely access.
- **Tool Required**: Ion Torch (upgraded) or Gas Mask

#### Log 006 — The Secondary Load Unit
- **Area**: Seegson Synthetics — Synthetic Storage area
- **Mission**: Mission 16 (recommended)
- **Specific Location**: In the synthetic storage area of the Seegson Synthetics facility. A gas-filled section blocks direct access — the Gas Mask is required.
- **Tool Required**: Gas Mask
- **Notes**: This is missable during Mission 7 if the player does not have the Gas Mask. Return during Mission 16's free-roam window.

#### Log 007 — Finishing Up Here
- **Area**: Engineering Section — North Maintenance Deck
- **Mission**: Mission 16 (recommended)
- **Specific Location**: On the North Maintenance Deck of the engineering section, in a room behind a Level 3 locked door.
- **Tool Required**: Level 3 Access Tuner
- **Notes**: Only accessible once the Level 3 Access Tuner is acquired at the start of Mission 16. Cannot be collected during Mission 13.

#### Log 008 — Antarctica Control Will Get Our Call
- **Area**: Gemini Exoplanet Solutions
- **Mission**: Mission 16 (recommended)
- **Specific Location**: In a restricted laboratory room in the Gemini Exoplanet Solutions wing, accessible via a welded door requiring the upgraded Ion Torch.
- **Tool Required**: Ion Torch (upgraded)

#### Log 009 — Health Checks
- **Area**: Lower station infrastructure
- **Mission**: Mission 17
- **Specific Location**: On the main console in the infrastructure section's central monitoring room. Accessible during normal Mission 17 progression.
- **Notes**: No special tool required. Collectible on the standard mission path.

#### Log 010 — Nostromo Report Packet
- **Area**: Flight recorder vault, secure storage section
- **Mission**: Mission 17
- **Specific Location**: On the vault's main storage rack, directly beside the Nostromo's flight recorder unit. Collect the log before interacting with the recorder to trigger the cutscene.
- **Notes**: No special tool required. This is the final Nostromo Log.

## Part 3: ID Tags

There are **50 ID Tags** scattered throughout Sevastopol Station. Each tag is a personal identification tag from a victim of the chaos on the station. ID Tags are found on bodies, surfaces, and environmental objects throughout all missions.

> **Collection Note**: ID Tags do not have a named achievement attached to their collection (unlike Nostromo Logs), but collecting them fills the in-game Archive and represents a complete exploration of Sevastopol. Many ID Tags are in side rooms off the critical path — thorough exploration of each mission area is required.

## Part 4: Crafting Materials Reference

All crafted items are built from four categories of materials found throughout Sevastopol Station. Material pickup notifications display in the upper left corner of the screen. Materials are shared across all recipes — managing your material pool carefully is essential to survival.

### Material Types

| Material | Description | Primary Uses |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sensors** | Electronic components; the most common material | Noisemaker, EMP Mine, Flashbang, Smoke Bomb |
| **Bonding Agent** | Chemical adhesive compound | Medkit, Smoke Bomb, Flashbang |
| **Ethanol** | Industrial solvent compound | Molotov, Smoke Bomb, Pipe Bomb |
| **Charge Pack** | Battery and power cell components | Noisemaker, EMP Mine |
| **SCJ Injector** | Medical compound cartridge | Medkit, EMP Mine, Noisemaker |
| **Blasting Cap** | Explosive primer components | Flashbang, Pipe Bomb, Molotov |
| **Compound B** | Reactive chemical compound | Medkit, Molotov, Pipe Bomb |
| **Scraps** | General mechanical components | Component in most recipes |

### Crafting Recommendations by Stage

#### Early Game (Missions 1–7)
- Prioritize **Noisemakers** and **EMP Mines**
- Noisemakers are the safest tool against the Alien — distract without provoking
- EMP Mines are essential for the android-heavy Seegson Synthetics mission
- Carry 1–2 Medkits at all times

#### Mid Game (Missions 8–13)
- Maintain a stock of **EMP Mines** and **Molotovs**
- Molotovs become available from Mission 6 — craft 2 to keep in reserve for emergencies
- Continue Noisemaker crafting for Alien distraction
- Smoke Bombs are useful in android-dense areas (Mission 7 and 12)

#### Late Game (Missions 14–19)
- **Flamethrower fuel** is prioritized over crafted devices — it is the most reliable Alien deterrent
- Continue crafting Noisemakers for the Mission 17 vent sections
- Craft 2–3 EMP Mines before Mission 18 (Samuels interface section)
- Reserve Medkits for Mission 18–19's sprint sequences

### Inventory Management Tip
When your material inventory is full, open the crafting menu and select a schematic without crafting the item. This places the required materials into a "projected" item slot, effectively freeing up inventory space for additional materials. This is the primary method for carrying more materials than your standard inventory limit allows.

## Part 5: Achievements and Trophies

### Collection Achievements

#### Archivist (Bronze/15G)
- **Requirement**: Collect all 10 Nostromo Logs
- **Strategy**: Collect logs 001, 002 during normal play. Reserve logs 003–008 for the Mission 16 free-roam pass with all tools equipped. Logs 009 and 010 are on the normal Mission 17 path.

#### A True Engineer (Bronze/15G)
- **Requirement**: Find all 7 V1 blueprints and craft at least one item of each type
- **All 7 V1 Blueprint Locations**:
  1. Medkit V1 — Mission 2
  2. Smoke Bomb V1 — Mission 3
  3. Flashbang V1 — Mission 3
  4. EMP Mine V1 — Mission 4
  5. Noisemaker V1 — Mission 4
  6. Pipe Bomb V1 — Mission 5
  7. Molotov V1 — Mission 6
- **Strategy**: All 7 V1 blueprints are collectible before Mission 7. Collect each during normal progression and craft one item of each type. Craft order does not matter.

---

### Gameplay Achievements

#### Survivor (Silver/25G)
- **Requirement**: Complete the main campaign on Hard difficulty

#### Making the Grade (Gold/50G)
- **Requirement**: Complete the main campaign on Nightmare difficulty
- **Note**: On Nightmare, the motion tracker is non-functional. All navigation must rely on sound, visual cues, and environmental knowledge.

#### True Survivor (Silver/25G)
- **Requirement**: Complete a Survivor Mode map with a score of at least 100,000 points

#### Safe Haven (Bronze/10G)
- **Requirement**: Use all 10 save stations across Sevastopol Station
- **Note**: Save stations are APOLLO emergency terminals distributed across all major mission areas. Using each at least once during a playthrough satisfies this requirement.

#### Ion-Propelled (Bronze/10G)
- **Requirement**: Use the Ion Torch to open 10 locked doors

#### One of Us (Bronze/10G)
- **Requirement**: Find a Working Joe and observe it for 60 seconds without being detected

#### Collect Everything (Platinum/Trophy)
- **Requirement**: Earn all other trophies (PlayStation only)

## Part 6: Mission Select and Cleanup

After completing the main campaign, Mission Select is available from the main menu. You can return to any mission to collect missed items. Mission Select preserves your current tool and key item loadout, meaning items like the Level 3 Access Tuner and Gas Mask are available when replaying earlier missions.

> **Recommended Cleanup Order**: Start Mission Select at Mission 16. With all tools, spend 20–30 minutes backtracking through the Lorenz Systech Spire, Seegson Synthetics, Gemini Exoplanet Solutions, and Engineering sections for the gas-locked and Level 3-locked collectibles. Then replay any earlier missions individually for any missed standard ID Tags.
