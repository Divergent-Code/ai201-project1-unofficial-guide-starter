# Amnesia: The Dark Descent Collectibles Guide

## Guide Overview

This guide provides a complete reference for all collectible items in Amnesia: The Dark Descent, organized by area. It covers Tinderboxes, Oil Containers, Laudanum, and Notes and Diary Pages, with achievement requirements for **Illuminatus** and **Master Archivist**.

**Author**: Adenosine  
**Game**: Amnesia: The Dark Descent (2010) by Frictional Games

---

## Important Collection Notes

### Tinderboxes
- There are approximately **46 Tinderboxes** in the game.
- Tinderboxes are **consumed on use** — each box lights one static source (candle, torch, fireplace). A tinderbox used for lighting does not count as "lost" for the Illuminatus achievement; only tinderboxes that are collected satisfy the achievement counter.
- **Collection vs. Use**: To earn Illuminatus, you must **pick up** all tinderboxes in a single playthrough. You may use them freely after collecting.
- Tinderboxes are found in drawers, on shelves, on floors, on tables, and on ledges. Interact with every container in every room.

### Oil Containers
- Oil containers refill the lantern's fuel supply.
- Oil is not a tracked collectible for any achievement, but running out of lantern oil creates severe sanity management problems.
- Collect all oil containers found — there is no penalty for over-collecting.

### Laudanum
- Laudanum restores Daniel's health.
- Laudanum is not a tracked collectible for any achievement.
- Consume when health is low; do not save indefinitely as it cannot be "banked" beyond inventory capacity.

### Notes and Diary Pages
- Notes and Diary Pages are the **Master Archivist** tracked collectibles.
- There are approximately **36 Notes and Diary Pages** in the game across all areas.
- Notes are automatically added to the Notes tab of the inventory when collected. They do not require reading at a save station to count.
- Notes are found on desks, floors, shelves, walls, and in locked containers.

## Part 5: Achievements and Trophies

### Illuminatus (Bronze/Trophy)
- **Requirement**: Collect all tinderboxes in a single playthrough
- **Strategy**: Use this guide's tinderbox table to check each area as you progress. The most commonly missed tinderboxes are: the one on top of the document cabinet in the Archives (requires climbing), the one under the machine floor grating in the Machine Room (requires lifting the grating), and the one on the central stepping stone in the Cistern's dark water crossing.
- **Note**: Tinderboxes that are collected and then used to light sources still count toward the achievement — only collection matters, not retention.

### Master Archivist (Bronze/Trophy)
- **Requirement**: Collect all notes and diary pages in a single playthrough
- **Strategy**: The most commonly missed notes are: the Cipher Fragment in Archives Reading Chamber B (easy to overlook the right chamber entirely), the Carved Wall Text in the Archive Tunnels (near the exit, often passed without stopping), and Daniel's Last Entry in the Inner Sanctum (on the floor, not on a surface — easy to miss while focused on the Orb).
- **Note**: Notes are auto-collected on approach — you do not need to hold an interaction button in most cases. However, notes on walls require a direct interact input.

### Agrippa's Friend
- **Requirement**: Complete the Agrippa ending
- **Step-by-Step**:
  1. Reach the Cells in Act IV and speak with Agrippa
  2. Proceed to the Inner Sanctum and collect both tonic ingredients
  3. Mix the tonic in the mixing bowl on the ritual table
  4. Return to the Cells and give the tonic to Agrippa
  5. Receive Agrippa's mouthpiece and carry it in inventory
  6. Return to the Inner Sanctum and approach the Orb to trigger the endgame
  7. In the Orb Chamber, when the portal opens, use the interaction prompt to throw the mouthpiece through the portal
  8. Weyer pulls Daniel through — achievement unlocked

### Connoisseur of Horror (Platinum/All Trophies)
- **Requirement**: Earn all other trophies (PlayStation only)
- **Notes**: Requires at minimum two playthroughs to earn both the Agrippa ending and the Redemption ending trophies if both are tracked separately. Check your trophy list for specific requirements.

## Part 6: Resource Management Summary

### Recommended Stock Levels
| Item | Recommended Minimum | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Tinderboxes (carried) | 3–5 at all times | Medium — light static sources aggressively |
| Oil (lantern charge) | Above 30% at all times | High — avoid hitting empty |
| Laudanum | 1–2 in inventory | Medium — consume freely when below half health |

### Sanity Restoration Techniques (No Items Required)
- Stand in a lit room for 30+ seconds
- Read notes and diary pages
- Complete story objectives (minor restoration)
- Look away from all monsters during encounters
- Use tinderboxes to light static sources in frequently visited rooms

### Oil Conservation Techniques
- Extinguish the lantern in well-lit rooms (lit by candles or natural light)
- Extinguish the lantern immediately when hiding — light contributes to detection
- Use tinderboxes to light rooms where significant time will be spent (puzzle rooms), then navigate by candlelight with the lantern off
