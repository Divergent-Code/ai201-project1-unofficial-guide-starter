# Dead Space (2008) Complete Walkthrough and Guide (Chapters 1-12)

## Part 1: Introduction and Core Systems

### A Brief Foreword
Dead Space is a survival horror game set aboard the USG Ishimura. Survival depends on tactical dismemberment: shooting the limbs off Necromorphs rather than aiming for their heads or torsos. 

### PS3 Controls
* **X / A**: Interact / Use / Reload (when aiming)
* **O / B**: Kinesis (grab objects) / Throw (when aiming)
* **Square / X**: Stasis / Stomping (when not aiming)
* **Triangle / Y**: Open Inventory / Jump in Zero-G (when aiming)
* **L1 / LT**: Aim Weapon
* **R1 / RT**: Melee Attack / Fire Weapon (when aiming)
* **R2 / RB**: Curbstomp / Alternate Fire (when aiming)
* **L3 / LS Click**: Locator Path (draws a guide line on the floor to the objective)
* **Right Stick (RS)**: Look around / Aim
* **Left Stick (LS)**: Move
* **D-pad**: Select Weapons
* **START**: Pause Menu
* **SELECT**: Open Map / Objectives / Database

### Starter Tips
1. **Dismemberment is Key**: Always shoot for the limbs (arms and legs). Cutting off the legs of slasher Necromorphs slows them down, allowing you to easily target their arms.
2. **Reload Management**: Always reload your weapons between combat encounters.
3. **Use the Locator (L3)**: When lost, click L3 to draw a path to your active objective.
4. **Use Kinesis (O / B)**: Use Kinesis to grab spike poles, claws, or explosive red canisters and throw them at enemies to save ammunition.
5. **Curbstomp Containers**: Use your curbstomp (R2) on green crates with lights to smash them open for supplies without wasting ammo.
6. **Watch the RIG (Spine)**: The blue glowing bar on Isaac's back is his health meter. The circular blue meter is his Stasis gauge.

## Part 2: Walkthrough Chapters 1 to 6

## Chapter 1: New Arrivals

### Objective: Walk to the Door

#### Hangar Bay and Flight Lounge
After the Kellion crash-lands in the Hangar Bay, exit the ship and follow your team. Interact with the systems panel inside the Flight Lounge to load the damage report.

---

### Objective: Reach Tram Control

#### Escape from the Lounge
After Necromorphs attack and breach the lounge, the security doors will open. Run down the hallway to the lift room elevator. Ride it down to the lower level.

#### Weapon: Plasma Cutter
Inside the Workshop, pick up the **Plasma Cutter** from the workbench. Shoot the glowing fuse next to the door lock to bypass the gate.

#### Tram Control Hallway
Walk down the corridor and pick up the credits on the floor. Grab the green health pack and play the audio log. Enter the Tram Control room to meet Hammond and Kendra behind the glass partition.

---

### Objective: Repair Damaged Tram

#### Finding the Stasis Module
Exit Tram Control to the Tram Control Hallway 2.
- **Stasis Module**: Pick up the **Stasis Module** next to the malfunctioning, rapidly closing door. Aim (L1) and fire Stasis (Square) to slow down the door, allowing you to walk through safely.
- **Stasis Recharge**: Use the wall-mounted Stasis Recharge station if your stasis gauge is depleted.

#### Tram Repair Bay
Enter the Tram Repair room and collect the **Power Node** from the wall container.
- **Centering the Tram Claw**: You must manually connect the two tram repair claws. Run to the left-side console and activate it to lock the first claw in place. Run to the right-side claw, use Stasis on the claw to slow down its rapid retraction, then quickly run back up to the main control panel and activate it to lock both claws.

---

### Objective: Find Data Board

#### Maintenance Lift
Return to the main hallway and take the Maintenance Lift down.
- **Key Item: Maintenance Bay Key**: Enter the Maintenance Bay. Defeat the Necromorphs (Slashers) in this area. Go to the corner console to pick up the **Maintenance Bay Key** and the text log.
- **Data Board Room**: Climb into the elevator, ride it to the upper walkway, and enter the locked room using the **Maintenance Bay Key**. 
- **Key Item: Data Board**: Pick up the **Data Board** from the desk. Use the Upgrade Bench in this room to spend your Power Nodes upgrading your RIG or Plasma Cutter.

---

### Objective: Call the Tram

#### Activating the Tram System
Return to the Tram Control room. Place the **Data Board** into the main computer console by the window, then activate the terminal to call the tram.

---

### Objective: Return to Kellion

#### Hangar Backtrack
Follow your locator path back through the lounge to the Hangar Bay. The Kellion will explode. Defeat the ambush of Slashers on the flight deck, then enter the elevator and follow the guide path to exit.

---

### Objective: Go to the Medical Deck

#### Tram Station Store
Go to the Tram Station. Use the Store terminal to purchase the **Pulse Rifle** or **Line Gun** if you have enough credits. Board the tram to travel to the Medical Deck, completing Chapter 1.

## Chapter 2: Intensive Care

### Objective: Retrieve the Captain's RIG

#### Medical Tram Station
Upon arriving at the Medical Tram Station, locate the corpse.
- **Stasis/Kinesis Module**: Pick up the **Kinesis Module** next to the corpse. This allows you to slide large obstacles out of your path. Move the metal crates blocking the hallway and pick up the **Flamethrower Schematic**.
- **Security Station**: Enter the Security Station (your safe haven). Grab the credits and resources, then save your game.

---

### Objective: Find the Thermite

#### Research Wing
Enter the Research Wing (Main Lab).
- **Stealth and Ranged Combat**: Walk down the research wing corridor. Use Stasis to slow down the broken mechanical door and step through.
- **Slasher Ambush**: Defeat the Slashers that drop from the vents. Use the red explosive canisters on the ground to blow them up.
- **Bio Lab**: Walk to the Bio Lab. You will encounter Lurkers (small babies that shoot three tentacles). Shoot the tentacles to defeat them.
- **Key Item: Thermite**: Collect the **Thermite** from the desk in the Bio Lab. Collect the **Power Node** from the wall container.

---

### Objective: Find the Shock Pad

#### Imaging Diagnostics
Proceed to the Imaging Diagnostics department.
- **Zero-G Therapy Hallway**: Locate the power block slot. Use Kinesis to grab a power block and slide it into the wall receiver to restore power to the lift.
- **Zero-G Therapy Room**: Enter the Zero-G room. Leap to the ceiling to navigate across the gaps. Pick up the **Shock Pad** from the terminal.

---

### Objective: Retrieve the Captain's RIG (Continued)

#### Emergency Room and Morgue
Return to the Clinic.
- **Emergency Room**: Enter the Emergency Room. Defeat the Lurkers and Slashers. Use the Stasis recharge station on the wall if needed.
- **Morgue**: Enter the Morgue. You will witness an Infector transforming the Captain's corpse into a Slasher. Immediately shoot the Infector first, then defeat the transformed Captain.
- **Key Item: Captain's RIG**: Pick up the **Captain's RIG** from the operating table.

---

### Objective: Get Back to Tram

#### Escape Route
Backtrack to the Medical Tram Station, avoid any remaining Slashers, save your game, and board the tram to complete Chapter 2.

## Chapter 3: Course Correction

### Objective: Re-fuel Engines

#### Control Room
Upon arriving at the Engineering Tram Station, proceed to the Control Room.
- **Machine Shop**: Walk down the ramp to the Machine Shop. Grab the **Flame Fuel Schematic** and the text log.
- **Refueling Chamber**: Enter the Refueling Chamber. You must fuel the engines. Go to the left side and use Kinesis to pull the refueling panel handle down. Board the moving gondola to cross to the right side, defeating the Leaper Necromorphs (crawling enemies with tails) that ambush you. Pull the second refueling handle down to complete the refueling sequence.

---

### Objective: Activate Centrifuge

#### Decontamination Room
Follow your locator to the Decontamination room.
- **Lockdown**: Once you enter, the room goes into lockdown. Defeat the three Slashers that ambush you from the vents. Once the air is recycled, exit through the opposite door.

#### Centrifuge Chamber
Enter the Centrifuge chamber.
- **Centrifuge Assembly**: You must manually lock the two centrifuge claws. Drop down to the lower walkway. Walk to the generators. Use Stasis to slow down the spinning generator shafts, then use Kinesis to slide the power block generators into the central slots. Do this for both sides.
- **Ignition Console**: Climb back to the upper console and activate the Centrifuge to restore gravity. Avoid the spinning centrifuge arm on the lower floor, as it will crush Isaac instantly. Climb to the elevator and exit.

---

### Objective: Manually Ignite the Engine

#### Fuel Storage and Engine Room
The air supply is lost, starting an oxygen countdown.
- **Fuel Storage**: Run to the lift and descend. Follow the linear path through the zero-gravity vacuum area, using stasis to slow down the spinning fans.
- **Engine Room**: Enter the Engine Room. Defeat the Slashers and Leapers. Activate the engine ignition terminal at the back of the room to restore gravity and air.

---

### Objective: Return to the Tram

#### Exiting the Engineering Deck
Backtrack through the control room to the Tram Station. Board the tram to complete Chapter 3.

## Chapter 4: Obliteration Imminent

### Objective: Meet Hammond

#### Bridge Tram Station
Arrive at the Bridge Tram Station. Walk through the Atrium Hallway to the Main Atrium.
- **Captain's Nest**: Take the elevator to the Captain's Nest and speak with Hammond.

---

### Objective: Activate Elevators

#### First Boss Fight: The Brute (Brute Boss Battle)
Upon exiting the Captain's Nest, the elevator door breaks open, and a Brute attacks.
- **Boss Strategy**: The Brute's front armor is invulnerable. Aim and shoot Stasis to freeze him, run behind him, and shoot the glowing yellow spots on his back/shoulders. Repeat this until he is defeated.

---

### Objective: Re-route ADS Power

#### Ship Systems and Mining Administration
You must reroute power from three areas to the Asteroid Defense System (ADS).
- **Rerouting Ship Systems**: Take the elevator to the Ship Systems room. Defeat the Slashers. Walk past the floor tiles with leaking electrical discharges. Use Kinesis to slide the power block into the wall slot to reroute the first power node.
- **Rerouting Mining Administration**: Take the elevator to Mining Administration. Defeat the Infector and the Slashers. Locate the power panel at the back of the room and flip the switch to reroute the second node.
- **Rerouting ADS Lower Hall**: Head to the ADS Lower Hall. Defeat the Pregnant Necromorph (bloated enemy; **do not shoot the belly** or it will release swarmers). Target the limbs, then flip the third switch.

---

### Objective: Destroy the Asteroids

#### ADS Cannon Console
Take the elevator to the ADS control room.
- **Asteroid Minigame**: Sit in the turret chair. You must shoot incoming asteroids to prevent them from destroying the ship's hull (health is shown as a percentage). 
- **Strategy**: Fire the left and right cannons in alternating bursts to prevent overheating. Target the largest asteroids first as they split into smaller pieces. Survive for 1 minute until the system auto-aligns, then return to the Tram to complete Chapter 4.

### Objective: Find the Chemicals

#### Medical Deck Search
Return to the Medical Deck. 
- **Imaging Diagnostics**: Your guide path leads through the security station to Imaging Diagnostics. Defeat the Lurkers.

---

### Objective: Find the DNA Sample

- **Key Item: Chemical Sample**: Interact with the laboratory terminal to mix the chemicals and retrieve the **Chemical Sample**.

#### Second Boss Fight: The Hunter (The Hunter Encounter)

#### Restoration of Oxygen
Dr. Mercer will poison the air.
- **Air Restoration**: Follow the locator back to the Security Station. Run to the central console and activate the emergency override to restore the O2 levels.

---

### Objective: Go to Hydroponics

#### Cryogenics Chamber
Follow the trail to the Cryogenics room.
- **Trapping the Hunter**: The Hunter will ambush you in the center of the room. Shoot his legs to crawl him into the central freezing chamber. Shoot Stasis to freeze him inside, then quickly run to the side control room and activate the cryogenic freeze console to lock him in the ice, completing the chapter.

## Chapter 6: Environmental Hazard

### Objective: Poison the Leviathan

#### Hydroponics Tram Station
Arrive at Hydroponics. 
- **Sapling Room**: Grab the schematics and follow the path to the Atmosphere Control room.
- **Trophy Check**: Buy the **Line Gun** or **Force Gun** at the store if desired.

---

### Objective: Destroy Poison Pods (8 Total)

You must locate and destroy 8 Poison Pods hidden throughout the hydroponics grow chambers.

#### Grow Chamber West (Pods 1 & 2)
- **Poison Pod 1**: Follow the path to Grow Chamber West. The first pod is on the ground floor next to the wall. Shoot it until it explodes.
- **Poison Pod 2**: Ride the lift to the second level. Look across the platform to locate and shoot the second pod.

#### Hydroponics Control (Pods 3 & 4)
- **Poison Pod 3**: Walk to the Hydroponics Control room. Shoot the third pod hidden behind the grow tanks on the left.
- **Poison Pod 4**: Shoot the fourth pod located on the ceiling above the central tanks.

#### Refrigeration West (Pods 5 & 6)
- **Poison Pod 5**: Enter Refrigeration West. Defeat the Lurkers. Locate and shoot the fifth pod in the corner of the freezer room.
- **Poison Pod 6**: Shoot the sixth pod hanging from the rafters.

#### Air Filtration (Pods 7 & 8)
- **Poison Pod 7**: Walk to the Air Filtration room. Shoot the four glowing fuses to open the safety doors. Run through the timed flame tubes, using Stasis to delay the fire. Shoot the seventh pod on the wall.
- **Poison Pod 8**: Follow the path to the upper level of the filtration room and shoot the eighth pod next to the elevator.

#### East Grow Chamber
- **Brute Battle**: Enter the East Grow Chamber. A Brute will ambush you. Use the same strategy (Stasis and shoot the back/shoulder vents) to defeat him.

---

### Objective: Recycle the Atmosphere

#### Restoring Air
Return to the Atmosphere Control room. Interact with the central terminal to recycle the air and remove the toxicity.

---

### Objective: Kill the Leviathan

#### Third Boss Fight: The Leviathan (Leviathan Boss Fight)
Enter the Food Storage chamber to face the Leviathan.
- **Phase 1 (Tentacles)**: The Leviathan will attack with three large tentacles. Stand on the side walkways, dodge its sweeps, and shoot the yellow glowing spots on the base of each tentacle when they slam down.
- **Phase 2 (Mouth)**: The central mouth will open, exposing an orange core. Shoot the core. When the Leviathan spits explosive balls at you, use Kinesis to catch the balls in mid-air and launch them back into the mouth.
- **Phase 3 (Combined)**: The tentacles and mouth will attack simultaneously. Focus fire on the mouth core while staying on the move to avoid tentacle slams. Once defeated, return to the Tram Station to complete Chapter 6.

## Chapter 7: Into the Void

### Objective: Find the Mining Access Key

#### RIG Room and Elevator
From the Mining Tram Station, follow the path to the RIG Room. Take the elevator down to Deck B (Processing). You will be attacked by four Slashers on the way down; prepare your Plasma Cutter or Force Gun.

#### Processing Antechamber
Enter the room and collect resources. Defeat the ambush of three Slashers, followed by a Leaper and a Pregnant Necromorph.

#### Mineral Processing
Enter the large zero-gravity mineral processing chamber. 
- **Deactivating the Beam**: You must throw the four glowing radioactive rocks into the processing beam using Kinesis to disintegrate them. Watch out for Leapers and Lurkers that ambush you in zero-gravity.
- **Restoring Gravity**: Return to the control panel console and restore gravity. 
- **Slasher Ambush**: Immediately upon restoring gravity, several Slashers and Exploders will drop from the ceiling vents. Defeat them, then use a Power Node to open the locked storage room for items.
- **Key Item: Mining Access Key**: Pick up the **Mining Access Key** from the main desk.

---

### Objective: Find the SOS Beacon

#### Repair Room
Head to the elevator and descend to Deck D. Watch out for infectors transforming corpses. Defeat them quickly to prevent more enemies from spawning.

#### Maintenance Chamber
Board the mechanical gondola to cross the open chasm.
- **Tentacle Hazards**: As the gondola travels, large tentacles will breach the walls. Aim and shoot the glowing yellow pods on each tentacle to destroy them before they sweep you.

#### Maintenance Annex
- **Defending the Survivor**: Protect the survivor (Elizabeth Cross) on the lower level from multiple waves of Slashers and Lurkers. 
- **Key Item: Level 4 RIG Schematic**: Retrieve the **Level 4 RIG Schematic** in the corner next to the beacon.

---

### Objective: Plant the Beacon

#### Mining Hallway
Backtrack to the elevator and ride to Deck C (Mining). 
- **Level 4 RIG Upgrade**: Go to the store and purchase the Level 4 RIG suit for 35,000 credits to increase armor and inventory capacity. 
- **Slasher Ambush**: Defeat the Slasher that bursts through the floor.

#### Mining Bay
Enter the zero-gravity Mining Bay.
- **Gravity Tethers (2 Total)**: You must manually lock the asteroid. Float to the two large rotating gravity tethers on the walls. Fire Stasis to slow their rotation, then shoot the glowing blue power modules inside the mechanisms to disable them.
- **Planting the Beacon**: Land on the outer surface of the massive asteroid. Dodge the moving mechanical slicing rings that sweep the surface. Walk to the center and interact with the console to plant the SOS Beacon on the asteroid. Leap back to the safe platform immediately.

---

### Objective: Launch the Asteroid

#### Control Room
Return to the control room. Grab the power block with Kinesis and slot it back into the wall receiver to restore elevator power. Activate the central launch panel to release the asteroid into space.

---

### Objective: Return to Tram

#### Escape
Make your way back to the Mining Tram Station. You will be ambushed by a Divider and a Pregnant Necromorph. Board the tram to complete Chapter 7.

## Chapter 8: Search and Rescue

### Objective: Contact Military Ship

#### Main Atrium
Arrive at the Bridge Tram Station. Walk to the Main Atrium.
- **Atrium Ambush**: Multiple Exploders, Leapers, and Slashers will ambush you on the ground level. Stand near the lift and use Kinesis to hurl explosive canisters at them.
- **Guardians Level 3**: Ride the lift to Level 3. Defeat the two Guardians attached to the walls by shooting their tentacles.

#### Comms Hall and Lift
Enter the Comms Hall. Defeat the Divider. Ride the Comms Lift to the upper deck.

---

### Objective: Fix the Array

#### Comms Array Chamber
Enter the large zero-gravity comms dome.
- **Dish Alignment Puzzle**: You must restore power to the primary comms dish. Use Kinesis to grab and pull out all the shattered/broken blue dishes from the mounting slots. Find the intact green dishes floating around the chamber. Use Kinesis to slide them into the active slots. You must form a complete circle of six green dishes around the central power node to restore the circuit. Defeat the Super Leapers that attack you during this process.

---

### Objective: Contact Military Ship (Continued)

#### Comms Control Room
Return to the Comms Control Room. Activate the computer terminal to transmit the distress call to the military ship USM Valor.

---

### Objective: Use ADS Cannon

#### Fourth Boss Fight: The Slug (ADS Cannon 48 (Slug Boss Fight))
Head to the lower deck to man the exterior ADS Cannon.
- **Boss Strategy**: The giant Slug creature is blocking the transmitter. Aim the dual cannons at the glowing yellow pods on its five long arms.
- **Incoming Debris**: The Slug will tear off pieces of the hull and throw them at your window. Shoot these incoming debris chunks in mid-air before they impact. Alternating between left and right fire buttons will prevent your cannons from overheating. Destroy all five arm nodes to defeat the boss.

---

### Objective: Return to Tram

#### Return Route
Backtrack through the Atrium. Defeat the Pregnant and Slashers in the corridors, then board the tram to complete Chapter 8.

## Chapter 9: Dead on Arrival

### Objective: Enter Military Ship

#### USM Valor Landing Pad
Board the crashed military ship USM Valor through the breached hull entrance. Save your game at the station.

---

### Objective: Clear Radioactive Material (6)

#### Cargo Ore Storage
Enter the zero-gravity cargo bay.
- **Disposal**: You must jettison six green radioactive cores. Use your weapon to shoot the three safety fuse locks on the large cargo bay doors to open them to space. 
- **Purging Cores**: Use Kinesis to grab each of the six glowing radioactive cylinders and hurl them out the doors into space. Defeat the Leapers and Lurkers that attempt to drag you.

---

### Objective: Find the Singularity Core

#### Cargo Bay
Move the heavy metal containers using Kinesis to open a path.
- **Twitcher Encounter**: You will encounter Twitchers—Necromorphs with high-speed movement due to stasis pack integration. 
- **Strategy**: Immediately fire Stasis to counteract their speed, then target their limbs to slow them down.

#### Armory and Shooting Gallery
Proceed to the Armory.
- **Shooting Minigame**: You can play the firing range game on the terminal. Complete all five rounds of targets to earn credits, Pulse Rifle ammo, and a Power Node.

#### Infirmary
Navigate the hallways.
- **Laser Trap**: Slow down the rapidly sweeping medical lasers using Stasis, then quickly run past them.

#### Barracks
Enter the crew barracks. You will be ambushed by four Twitchers, two Exploders, and a Pregnant. Stand in the doorway to funnel them.

#### Engine Room
Enter the main engine chamber.
- **Fire Hazards**: The engines will fire periodic blasts of fire down the runways.
- **Sliding Shields**: Stand behind the metal blast shields on tracks. Use Kinesis to drag the shield along with you to block the fire as you advance.
- **Destroying Power Boxes**: Shoot the three glowing fuses on the columns on the left, and three on the right (six total) to shut down the engines.
- **Key Item: Singularity Core**: Retrieve the **Singularity Core** from the central generator block.

---

### Objective: Return to Tram

#### USM Valor Escape
The ship's reactor is failing. Backtrack through the collapsing corridors.
- **Brute Battle**: A Brute will block your path in the Cargo Bay. Freeze him with Stasis, run behind him, and shoot the yellow weak spots on his shoulders.
- **Hangar Bay Exit**: Run past the exploding panels and exit the ship back to the Ishimura. Board the tram to complete Chapter 9.

- **Sliding Beds Puzzle**: Use Kinesis to slide the metal bunks left and right along their tracks to clear a path through the room. Defeat the Lurkers. Collect **Navigation Card 2** and **Navigation Card 3** from the tables.

### Objective: Kill the Hive Mind (Final Boss Fight: The Hive Mind)

#### Landing Pad Arena
The massive Hive Mind rises from the abyss.
- **Phase 1 (Outer Eyes)**: The Hive Mind will slam its giant tentacles down on the platform. Run left and right to dodge the sweeps. Target and shoot the five glowing yellow sensory eyes around its mouth.
- **Phase 2 (Hanging)**: The boss will grab Isaac by the leg, holding him upside down. Aim carefully and shoot the remaining yellow eyes.
- **Phase 3 (Chest Pods)**: The boss's chest plates will open, exposing five orange glowing pods. Shoot them. Use Stasis to slow its chest movements and dodge its tentacle slams. Destroy all chest pods to defeat the Hive Mind and complete the campaign.
