# Outlast 2 (2017) Collectibles and Documents Reference Guide

## Part 1: Overview

### Collectible Types
Outlast 2 has two types of collectibles:
* **Documents**: Written notes, letters, sermons, and reports found lying in the environment. Pick up by pressing R1/RB when prompted.
* **Recordings**: Events, objects, or scenes captured by aiming Blake's camcorder (L2/LT) at a highlighted point of interest and holding the button to record.

### Key Notes
- There are **36 Documents** and **20 Recordings** in the game (56 total collectibles).
- Collectibles do **not** pause enemy AI. Pick them up quickly and move on.
- After completing the game, **Chapter Select** is available from the main menu. Collectible progress is saved independently and carries over across replays.
- Open the collectibles log at any time by pressing the **Touchpad (PS4)** or **View Button (Xbox One)** to check what has been found in each chapter.

## Part 2: Chapter-by-Chapter Collectibles

## Chapter 1: Genesis — Collectibles

### Documents (4 Total)

#### Document 1 — Body in the Road
- **Location**: Along the dirt road in the outskirts of Temple Gate village, next to the body of a dead cultist lying face-down in the road.
- **How to Get**: Approach the body. The document is a crumpled note on the ground beside it. Press R1/RB to pick up.

#### Document 2 — Anna Lee's Letter
- **Location**: Inside the second house on the right side of the main village street, entered through the back door.
- **How to Get**: The letter is on the small table near the window. Pick up while the patrolling Heretic is not facing the room.

#### Document 3 — Heresy
- **Location**: At the edge of the first large cornfield, resting on a wooden post just before the church comes into view through the stalks.
- **How to Get**: Walk to the post and press R1/RB.

#### Document 4 — Scripture Note
- **Location**: In the flooded riverbed canyon section after the village escape, on a rock ledge to the left of the main path just before the wooden waterwheel structure.
- **How to Get**: Step off the main path and approach the ledge. Press R1/RB.

---

### Recordings (2 Total)

#### Recording 1 — Crash Site
- **Location**: At the burning helicopter wreckage at the start of the chapter.
- **How to Get**: Raise the camcorder (L2/LT) and aim it at the burning wreckage. Hold L2/LT until the recording completes.

#### Recording 2 — The Herd
- **Location**: Just outside the church, before entering. Heretics are gathered in a group near the church entrance.
- **How to Get**: Stay at the cornfield's edge and raise the camcorder toward the congregation of Heretics. Hold L2/LT to record.

## Chapter 2: Job — Collectibles

### Documents (7 Total)

#### Document 5 — Mining Report
- **Location**: Inside the mine, in the first large chamber. Turn right immediately upon entering the chamber.

#### Document 8 — Covenant Letter
- **Location**: Inside the first barn on the right side of the flooded farm fields, entered through the open barn doors.
- **How to Get**: The letter is pinned to the barn's interior wall. Press R1/RB.

#### Document 9 — Thomas's Confession
- **Location**: Pinned to a fence post on the far side of the second flooded field, near the wagon.
- **How to Get**: Approach the post and press R1/RB.

#### Document 10 — Jessica's Letter
- **Location**: In the upstairs bedroom of the stone farmhouse reached via the wagon trail, on the desk.
- **How to Get**: Climb the farmhouse stairs and press R1/RB at the desk.

#### Document 11 — The Crops
- **Location**: In the open countryside after exiting the mine, on the ground at the field's entrance.
- **How to Get**: Pick up as you emerge from the mine tunnel exit.

## Part 3: School Flashback Recordings

The St. Sybil Catholic School flashbacks that interrupt Blake's journey contain their own special recordings. These are captured during the flashback sequences themselves.

#### Recording 16 — St. Sybil (Hallway)
- **Chapter**: Chapter 2 (triggers after picking up Jessica's Letter)
- **How to Get**: During the first flashback, aim the camcorder at the lit classroom doorway at the end of the hallway. Hold L2/LT.

#### Recording 17 — St. Sybil (Projector Room)
- **Chapter**: Chapter 3 (triggers at the top of the radio tower)
- **How to Get**: During the second flashback, aim the camcorder at the projector light source flickering at the hallway's end. Hold L2/LT before the Father Loutermilch encounter.

#### Recording 18 — St. Sybil (Basement)
- **Chapter**: Chapter 5 (triggers after the mine collapse zone)
- **How to Get**: In the flooded school basement during the third flashback, aim the camcorder at the source of the light at the corridor's far end. Hold L2/LT.

#### Recording 19 — St. Sybil (The Truth)
- **Chapter**: Chapter 6 (triggers in the final open field sequence)
- **How to Get**: During the final flashback, aim the camcorder at the school chapel window where light is visible. Hold L2/LT.

#### Recording 20 — The Light
- **Chapter**: Chapter 6 (end of game, open field)
- **How to Get**: After Lynn collapses in the field and before the final cutscene triggers, raise the camcorder and aim at the white light in the sky. Hold L2/LT.

## Part 4: Achievements and Trophies Reference

### Collectible Achievements

| Achievement / Trophy | Requirement | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ordination** | Find all 36 Documents | Collect every written note, letter, and report across all 6 chapters. |
| **Bible Study** | Find all 20 Recordings | Capture every camcorder recording across all 6 chapters including school flashback recordings. |
| **The Apostle Paul** | Find all Documents AND Recordings | Complete both Ordination and Bible Study in a single or cumulative playthrough. |

### Difficulty Achievements

| Achievement / Trophy | Requirement | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Messiah** | Complete the game on any difficulty | Finish the full campaign. |
| **The Road to Damascus** | Complete the game on Insane difficulty | Complete all 6 chapters on Insane. Enemies are more aggressive and deal lethal damage. Collectibles are still accessible. |

### Insane Difficulty Tips
* Insane mode allows death, but progress within chapters is preserved.
* Prioritize movement and avoid engagement entirely — even a single grab from Marta is lethal.
* Pick up all batteries you see — Night Vision is essential for survival in dark areas.
* Use tall grass as your primary hiding method; it is the most reliable concealment tool in the game.
* Chapter Select can be used to replay individual chapters on Insane if you fail without losing overall progress.
