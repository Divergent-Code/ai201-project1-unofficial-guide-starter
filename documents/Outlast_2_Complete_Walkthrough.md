# Outlast 2 (2017) Complete Walkthrough and Guide (Chapters 1-6)

## Part 1: Introduction and Core Systems

### A Brief Foreword
Outlast 2 is a first-person survival horror game by Red Barrels set in the remote Sonoran Desert of rural Arizona. You play as Blake Langermann, a cameraman investigating the murder of a young pregnant woman known only as "Jane Doe." After your helicopter crashes, you are separated from your journalist wife Lynn and must survive the violent religious cult led by a preacher named Sullivan Knoth while unraveling the dark secrets connecting the cult to your own past.

Unlike most action games, Blake cannot fight back against enemies. Survival depends entirely on stealth, evasion, hiding, and knowing when to run.

---

### PS4 / Xbox One Controls
* **L2 / LT**: Record with Camcorder / Aim Camera
* **R2 / RT**: Sprint (hold to run)
* **L1 / LB**: Toggle Night Vision (drains battery)
* **R1 / RB**: Interact (pick up items, open doors, examine notes)
* **Square / X**: Crouch / Uncrouch
* **X / A**: Jump
* **Circle / B**: Hide in a hiding spot (locker, barrel, grass, under bed)
* **Triangle / Y**: Examine a point of interest with the camera
* **L3 Click**: Hold breath while hiding (reduces sound when an enemy is close)
* **D-pad Up / Down**: Cycle through batteries in inventory
* **Touchpad / View Button**: Open Documents / Recordings log

---

### Starter Tips
1. **Camera is Your Only Tool**: The camcorder is Blake's primary survival tool. Use it to record documents and events, and use Night Vision to navigate dark areas.
2. **Conserve Batteries**: Night Vision drains battery power rapidly. Do not leave it on in areas with ambient lighting. Always pick up batteries when found — they are scattered throughout the environment.
3. **Crouch to Stay Silent**: Crouching significantly reduces the noise Blake makes. Always crouch in enemy-occupied areas unless you need to sprint.
4. **Use Hiding Spots**: Crawl under beds, crouch in barrels, and hide inside lockers to break line of sight with enemies. While hiding, press and hold L3/LS to hold your breath, reducing detection noise.
5. **Enemies Cannot Be Killed**: There is no way to eliminate or permanently defeat enemies. Focus on breaking line of sight, reaching a hiding spot, and waiting for patrols to move on.
6. **Running Draws Attention**: Sprinting is loud and will alert nearby enemies. Use sprinting only when you are already detected and need to escape to a hiding spot immediately.
7. **Collect Everything**: Documents and Recordings are the only collectibles in the game. Collecting all of them unlocks specific achievements. They do not pause gameplay — grab them quickly.
8. **Tall Grass Provides Cover**: Crawling through tall grass hides Blake from sight. Enemies will lose track of you if you break line of sight and crawl into dense grass or foliage.
9. **Listen for Audio Cues**: Enemies give verbal warnings before attacking. If an enemy says something like "I can hear you," slow down and hide immediately. If they shout, sprint for the nearest cover.
10. **Chapter Select Available**: After completing the game, you can replay any chapter to collect missed documents or recordings.

## Part 2: Walkthrough Chapters 1 to 6

## Chapter 1: Genesis

### Objective: Find Lynn

#### Helicopter Crash Site
Blake regains consciousness after the helicopter crash in the dark Arizona desert. The camcorder is lying nearby. Pick it up. Walk forward along the dry riverbed toward the wreckage of the helicopter.
- **Collectible — Recording (Crash Site)**: As you approach the burning helicopter, aim your camera at the wreckage and hold L2/LT to capture the crash site recording.
- Walk through the shallow river water and follow the canyon path. Activate Night Vision (L1/LB) if the path becomes too dark.

#### Approaching the Village
Follow the linear path through the tall corn fields. The path curves left through a fence and into the outskirts of a remote village.
- **Collectible — Document (Body in the Road)**: Look for a crumpled paper note on the dirt road next to the body of a dead cultist. Press R1/RB to pick it up.
- Continue forward toward the lights. You will hear voices and cultist activity ahead.

#### The Village of Temple Gate
Enter the village through the gap in the fence. Move crouched through the open courtyard.
- **Stealth Navigation**: Multiple Heretics (female cult members led by Anna Lee) patrol the village. Stay crouched and move from building to building.
- **First Hiding Spot**: When a Heretic investigates your direction, enter the nearest open building on the left and crouch beneath the bed until she moves on.
- **Collectible — Document (Anna Lee's Letter)**: Found inside the second house on the right. Enter through the back door and examine the paper on the small table.

#### The Barn and the Well
Push through the village toward the large barn structure.
- **Encounter: First Chase Sequence**: As you pass the barn, a Heretic will spot you and scream. Sprint forward immediately, vault over the low fence ahead using X/A, and run toward the wooden fence gate on the far side of the field. Do NOT stop running until the scream audio fades.
- Follow the path down into a ditch and through an underground drainage pipe. Emerge on the other side near the well.
- Climb the ladder out of the ditch.

---

### Objective: Reach the Church

#### The Cornfield Path
Follow the path through the dense cornfield. The path is linear; follow the gaps in the stalks.
- **Collectible — Document (Heresy)**: A pamphlet resting on a wooden post at the field's edge, just before the church comes into view.
- **Collectible — Recording (The Herd)**: Raise your camera and record the group of Heretics gathered near the church entrance from a distance.

#### The Church Courtyard
The path leads to the front of a large wooden church.
- Crucified bodies line the walls. Do not stop to investigate — Heretics patrol the perimeter.
- Enter the church via the side door on the left, not the main entrance.

#### Inside the Church
Crouch and move along the church pews. Three Heretics are inside.
- Move slowly along the left wall to the far door. A fourth Heretic guards the hallway beyond — wait for her to walk away, then slip through.
- A cutscene will trigger as Blake descends into the church cellar.

---

### Objective: Escape the Heretics

#### The Underground Passage
Blake discovers a tunnel network beneath the church.
- Follow the tunnel. Collect the **battery** lying against the wall on the left to top up your camcorder.
- The tunnel opens into a larger underground chamber. A Heretic with a lantern patrols the chamber.
- **Strategy**: Wait for her patrol path to move to the right side of the chamber. Crouch and move along the left wall to the exit ladder.

#### Escape Through the Fields
Climb the ladder into the open air. You are now in a large open field on the outskirts of the village.
- Sprint across the open field toward the tree line. Heretics will pursue.
- Reach the wooden fence at the field's edge and vault over to complete the chapter's main escape sequence.

---

### Objective: Find the Mine Entrance

#### Riverbed Canyon
Blake falls down a muddy slope and must navigate a flooded riverbed.
- **Note**: This section contains no enemies. Move at your own pace.
- **Collectible — Document (Scripture Note)**: Found on a rock ledge to the left of the main path, just before the wooden waterwheel structure.
- Cross the shallow pool and climb the rope ladder up the cliff face.

#### The Mine Entrance
At the top of the cliff, follow the path to the old wooden mine entrance.
- Collect the **two batteries** inside the mine's antechamber.
- Crawl through the low tunnel to reach the mine interior and complete Chapter 1.

## Chapter 2: Job

### Objective: Navigate the Mine

#### The Dark Mine Tunnels
Activate Night Vision immediately. The mine is pitch black.
- **Collectible — Document (Mining Report)**: On an overturned cart in the first large chamber. Turn right upon entering.
- Follow the sound of running water. The path leads through a series of collapsed tunnels.
- Several sections require crouching through narrow gaps — press Square/X to crouch before approaching low openings.

#### The Mine Cart Puzzle
You will reach a blocked section where a mine cart is wedged across the track.
- Walk to the cart and hold R1/RB to push it forward down the track to clear the path. Follow it through.
- Continue forward, climbing over rubble piles and ducking under support beams.

#### The Underground Pool
The tunnel opens into a large underground chamber with a flooded floor.
- Wade through the knee-deep water. Blake cannot drown in this section.
- **Collectible — Document (Old Letter)**: On a wooden platform in the center of the chamber, raised above the water.
- Climb the rope on the far wall and follow the upper ledge to the exit passage.

---

### Objective: Find Lynn in the Village

#### Emerging from the Mine
Blake exits the mine into a new section of the countryside at dawn. The landscape opens into farmland and abandoned buildings.
- **Collectible — Recording (The Crops)**: Raise your camera and aim at the rows of dead crops in the field as Blake mutters about them. Hold L2/LT to capture the recording.
- Follow the road toward the cluster of buildings in the distance.

#### The Testament of New Ezekiel Village
This is the main settlement of Sullivan Knoth's doomsday cult. Cult members (Knoth's followers) patrol the grounds.
- Unlike the Heretics, these cultists are armed with farm tools (scythes, axes). They are slower than Heretics but can kill Blake in a few hits.
- **Strategy**: Crouch immediately and move along the fence line on the left side of the road to bypass the patrolling cultists.

#### The Village Commons Area
Move through the central area of the village.
- **Collectible — Document (Knoth's Sermon)**: On the wooden pulpit in the center of the village commons, next to a large cross.
- **Encounter: Marta Introduction**: A cutscene triggers. You meet Marta, a tall, robed woman wielding an enormous pickaxe. She is the chapter's primary antagonist and pursuer. She is extremely fast and durable.

---

### Objective: Escape Marta

#### First Marta Chase
Marta will immediately pursue Blake after the cutscene.
- Sprint forward along the main road. Do not stop.
- Vault over the fence at the road's end and continue running down the hill toward the farmland below.
- Dive into the tall grass at the bottom of the hill and immediately stop sprinting. Crouch and hold L3/LS (hold breath) as Marta descends.
- **Hiding Strategy**: Marta will patrol the area for 30–45 seconds. Crawl slowly through the grass toward the river on the far side of the field. Do not stand up or sprint until Marta leaves the area.

---

### Objective: Cross the Farmland

#### The Flooded Farm Fields
The area is a large flooded paddy field with multiple barns and structures.
- **Collectible — Document (Covenant Letter)**: Inside the first barn on the right side of the field. Enter through the open barn doors.
- **Collectible — Document (Thomas's Confession)**: Pinned to a post on the far side of the second flooded field, next to the wagon.
- Cross the farmland by moving from one dry patch or building to the next.

#### The Windmill Structure
Follow the path to a large windmill at the field's edge.
- A cultist is patrolling inside. Wait for him to face away, enter the windmill, and climb the internal ladder to the upper platform.
- Cross the connecting rope bridge to the far side and descend the ladder to the ground.

#### The Covered Bridge
Cross the narrow wooden covered bridge.
- **Encounter: Marta Bridge Chase**: Marta will break through the bridge planks from below mid-crossing. Sprint immediately to the far end of the bridge and vault over the broken section. Do not stop or look back.

---

### Objective: Find the Road

#### Farm Road and Wagon Trail
Exit the bridge area and follow the wagon trail through the trees.
- **Collectible — Recording (The Sign)**: Aim your camera at the large wooden sign at the crossroads ahead ("TEMPLE GATE — THE END IS NIGH") and record it.
- Follow the right branch of the road toward the stone farmhouse.

#### The Farmhouse
Enter the farmhouse through the front door.
- Collect the **two batteries** on the kitchen table.
- **Collectible — Document (Jessica's Letter)**: On the desk in the upstairs bedroom. (Note: This triggers a flashback sequence to St. Sybil Catholic School involving Blake's childhood friend Jessica. These sequences are interspersed throughout the game.)

#### School Flashback — St. Sybil (First)
Blake experiences a hallucinatory flashback to his days at St. Sybil Catholic School.
- Follow the school hallway. The environment shifts and distorts.
- **Objective in flashback**: Find Jessica. Move toward the lit classroom at the hall's end.
- The flashback ends and Blake wakes up in the farmhouse. Exit through the back door to complete Chapter 2.

## Chapter 3: Lamentations

### Objective: Follow the River

#### The Canyon Ravine
Blake wakes in a ravine. The path follows the river downstream through a tight canyon.
- **Collectible — Document (Heretic Symbol)**: Painted on the canyon wall in red. Aim your camera at it and examine it.
- Follow the water. A brief school flashback will interrupt.

#### The Waterfall Pool
The canyon opens into a large pool at the base of a waterfall.
- Swim across the pool — Blake can swim, but cannot dive.
- Climb the wooden docking platform on the far side and follow the path up the hillside.
- **Collectible — Recording (The Waterfall)**: Before climbing the hill, turn back and record the waterfall from the dock.

---

### Objective: Find Lynn's Signal

#### The Scalled Outpost
The path leads into the territory of the Scalled — diseased cultists who have been exiled by Knoth and left to die. They are more aggressive than standard cultists.
- **Collectible — Document (The Scalled Cry Out)**: Nailed to the door of the first abandoned shack in the Scalled territory.
- The Scalled will pursue Blake on sight. They are clustered together, so use the gaps between shacks to navigate around them.
- **Hiding Strategy**: The shacks have low crawlspaces underneath. Crawl under the nearest shack and hold your breath when pursuers run past.

#### The Collapsed Village (Scalled Sector)
The Scalled occupy the ruins of a collapsed village section near the cliff edge.
- **Collectible — Document (Dr. Trager Report)**: Inside the large tent in the center of the Scalled camp.
- Move through the tent while the Scalled leader is facing away. Collect the document and exit through the rear flap immediately.
- A school flashback interrupts at the camp's far edge.

---

### Objective: Cross the Lake

#### The Lake Shore
The path opens onto the shore of a large, dark lake.
- **Collectible — Document (Knoth on the Scalled)**: On a wooden picnic table at the lakeside camp.
- Blake must cross the lake using the wooden rowboat docked at the shore. Approach and hold R1/RB to board and row the boat.

#### Rowing the Lake
Row the boat toward the far shore. The lake is calm; however, enemies cannot follow Blake on the water.
- **Collectible — Recording (The Lake)**: While rowing, point the camera at the distant glowing light on the far shore and record it.
- **Warning**: As Blake nears the far shore, a large creature will tip the boat. Blake is thrown into the water. Swim toward the shore immediately — swim by pressing forward on the left stick. Do not let the creature pull Blake under.

#### Far Lakeshore
Blake pulls himself ashore on the far side. Collect the **battery** in the overturned boat.
- Follow the path into the forest and up the hillside.

---

### Objective: Reach the Radio Tower

#### The Radio Tower Approach
The path climbs through a dense forest toward a tall radio/communications tower.
- **Collectible — Document (Lynn's Note)**: On a broken fence post at the forest path's midpoint.
- Cultists from a new faction patrol the tower's base. Move through the treeline rather than the open road.
- Approach the tower from the right side and climb the ladder on the tower's rear face to ascend.

#### Radio Tower Top
Blake reaches the top of the tower and looks out over the landscape, locating a signal.
- A school flashback sequence triggers at the top of the tower.

#### School Flashback — St. Sybil (Second)
Navigate through the distorted school hallways. The school environment is more corrupted than before.
- **Objective**: Find the projector room. Follow the light flickering at the end of the hallway.
- A terrifying encounter with a dark figure (Father Loutermilch) occurs. Immediately sprint the opposite direction and enter the first classroom door on the left to hide.
- The flashback ends. Blake descends from the tower.

---

### Objective: Reach Lynn's Location

#### The Valley Below the Tower
Blake descends from the tower into a valley. The area is heavily patrolled by Knoth's cultists.
- **Collectible — Document (Knoth's Gospel — Part 2)**: On a barrel outside the large farmhouse at the valley floor.
- Crouch and navigate around the clustered cultist patrols using the fence line as cover.

#### The Underground Cave System
At the far end of the valley, Blake discovers an opening into a cave system.
- Activate Night Vision — the caves are completely dark.
- **Collectible — Recording (The Cave Paintings)**: Deep in the cave, point your camera at the ancient red paintings on the cave wall and record them.
- Follow the cave tunnel to the far exit.

#### Marta's Church
The cave exits behind a large stone church. This is Marta's territory.
- **Collectible — Document (Marta's Manifesto)**: Posted on the church door in large red paint.
- Approach the church door carefully.
- **Encounter: Marta Interior Chase**: Marta is inside the church. The moment Blake enters, Marta will detect him and charge.
- **Strategy**: Sprint down the center aisle, vault the altar railing, and sprint through the door at the back of the church. Do not engage or look back. Follow the path through the church's rear garden and dive into the tall grass immediately.
- **Hiding**: Crawl through the grass around the graveyard perimeter while Marta patrols. Wait until her footsteps fade, then move to the graveyard's far gate.

---

### Objective: Find Lynn

#### The Ravine Trail
Exit the graveyard through the wooden gate and follow the ravine trail downhill.
- **Collectible — Document (Village Charter)**: On a tree stump at the trail's midpoint.
- Follow the path to a camp where a cutscene triggers. Blake finds a clue to Lynn's location but is knocked unconscious by Knoth's followers. Chapter 3 ends.

---

## Chapter 4: Judges

### Objective: Escape Knoth's Camp

#### The Prisoner Cage
Blake wakes suspended upside down in a hanging cage above Knoth's main camp. Swing the cage by tilting the left stick back and forth rhythmically to build momentum. Swing far enough to grab the torch-post and knock the cage against it to break the rope.
- Blake falls to the ground. Stay crouched — Knoth's armed guards patrol directly below.

#### The Camp Perimeter
The camp is large and densely guarded. Knoth's followers carry torches and patrol in overlapping patterns.
- **Collectible — Document (Knoth's Gospel — Part 3)**: On the table inside the large central tent. Slip inside when the guard at the tent entrance walks to the right.
- **Collectible — Recording (The Camp)**: Raise your camera toward the central bonfire and the assembled cultists gathered around it.
- Move clockwise around the camp perimeter, keeping to the shadowed areas along the outer fence. The main gate exit is on the camp's north side.

#### The Gate Escape
The main gate is locked with a wooden crossbar. Wait for both gate guards to face inward, then approach and hold R1/RB to lift the crossbar and push the gate open.
- Sprint through the gate and down the hill immediately — guards will shout and pursue.
- Dive into the dense treeline at the hill's base and crawl through the underbrush until the pursuit noise fades.

---

### Objective: Reach the Underground

#### The Cornfield — Night
The path leads through a massive, dark cornfield at night. This is one of the most disorienting sections of the game.
- **Strategy**: Activate Night Vision and follow the moonlight visible above the corn stalks. Move generally forward and slightly right when the path forks.
- **Collectible — Document (The Testament of New Ezekiel)**: On a wooden stake driven into the ground at the cornfield's center clearing.
- Knoth's cultists patrol the cornfield in teams of two. When you hear voices, stop moving, crouch, and wait for them to pass.
- **Encounter: Cornfield Chase**: A patrol will spot you near the field's far edge. Sprint directly forward toward the visible fence line. Vault the fence and slide down the muddy embankment on the other side.

#### The Irrigation Tunnels
At the base of the embankment, Blake discovers a concrete irrigation drainage tunnel.
- Crawl inside. The tunnel is tight; keep crouched the entire time.
- **Collectible — Document (Engineer's Note)**: On the tunnel floor midway through, next to a broken pipe joint.
- The tunnel system splits twice. At the first fork, take the left passage. At the second fork, take the right.
- Emerge into a flooded basement beneath an abandoned farmhouse.

#### The Abandoned Farmhouse
Climb the basement ladder into the farmhouse.
- Collect the **three batteries** on the kitchen counter.
- **Collectible — Recording (The Farmhouse)**: Aim your camera out the broken front window at the night landscape and record the view.
- Exit through the farmhouse's back door and follow the muddy path into the forest.

---

### Objective: Find Marta

#### The Forest Descent
The forest path winds downhill through increasingly dense woodland.
- **Collectible — Document (Marta's Prayer)**: Carved into the bark of a large tree just off the path on the right side.
- As Blake descends, Marta's lantern light will become visible through the trees below. She is searching for Blake.

#### Marta Graveyard Encounter
The forest opens into an old, overgrown graveyard. Marta is patrolling its center.
- **Strategy**: Move along the graveyard's outer wall, keeping the stone wall between you and Marta at all times.
- Do not step on gravel patches — the crunching noise will alert Marta. Step on grass only.
- **Collectible — Document (Epitaph)**: On a large headstone in the graveyard's southeast corner.
- Exit through the wooden gate in the graveyard's north wall while Marta is at the south end of her patrol.

#### The Ravine Bridge — Marta's Final Chase
Follow the path down to a narrow rope-and-plank bridge over a deep ravine.
- As Blake crosses the midpoint, Marta will appear at the bridge's near end and begin crossing.
- **Sprint** to the far end of the bridge without stopping. At the end of the bridge, a cutscene triggers: a large cross structure falls on Marta, impaling her. Marta is defeated.
- This is the last encounter with Marta in the main campaign.

---

### Objective: Find the Underground Entrance

#### The Descent Tunnels
Past the ravine, the path leads into a system of natural caves and hand-dug tunnels.
- Activate Night Vision — the tunnels are completely dark.
- **Collectible — Document (Scripture Etching)**: Carved into the cave wall in the first large cavern.
- **Collectible — Recording (The Descent)**: Point the camera at the glowing, pulsing object visible deep in the cave floor below a shaft of light.
- Follow the tunnel to an underground structure — the entrance to the Temple Gate underground complex.
- A cutscene triggers as Blake descends into the underground. Chapter 4 ends.

## Chapter 5: Leviticus

### Objective: Find Lynn Underground

#### The Underground Entrance Hall
Blake enters the underground complex. The architecture shifts between natural caves and crude stone-block construction.
- **Collectible — Document (Knoth's Underground Manifesto)**: On a lectern inside the stone entrance hall.
- Follow the corridor lit by oil lanterns. Knoth's followers now occupy this underground space.

#### The Underground Village
The tunnel opens into an expansive underground cavern with a built village — crude structures, bridges, and walkways carved into the cavern walls.
- **Collectible — Recording (The Underground Village)**: Raise your camera and record the cavern as Blake overlooks it from the entrance ledge.
- Multiple cultists patrol the walkways. Move along the lower cave floor, below the main walkway level, to avoid detection.
- **Collectible — Document (Underground Charter)**: On a table inside the largest underground building, the central meeting hall.

---

### Objective: Navigate the Scalled Tunnels

#### The Scalled Underground Territory
A lower passage branches off the main underground route and leads into Scalled territory. The Scalled have carved their own crude tunnels deeper underground.
- The Scalled here are more aggressive and are clustered in tight groups. Use narrow side passages to navigate around them.
- **Collectible — Document (The Scalled Prayer)**: Written on cloth, draped over a wooden cross in the Scalled's underground shrine room.
- **Encounter: Scalled Leader Pursuit**: The Scalled leader, a tall, emaciated figure, will detect Blake and pursue. Sprint forward through the narrow connecting tunnel and crouch through the low opening at the end. The Scalled leader cannot follow through the small gap.

#### The Underground Stream
The Scalled tunnels emerge at an underground stream.
- Wade downstream. The current is gentle and navigable.
- **Collectible — Recording (The Stream)**: Aim the camera at the unusual glowing mineral formations on the cave ceiling above the stream.
- Follow the stream to a waterfall drop. Slide down and continue forward.

---

### Objective: Locate the Temple

#### The Ancient Caves
The path transitions from hand-dug Scalled tunnels into older, natural cave formations with ancient indigenous markings on the walls.
- **Collectible — Document (Ancient Writing — Translation)**: A paper note left by a researcher, tucked into a crack in the cave wall near the markings.
- **Collectible — Recording (Ancient Markings)**: Record the largest wall of cave markings as Blake comments on them.

#### The Mine Collapse Zone
A section of the cave system has partially collapsed, requiring Blake to climb over debris.
- Collect the **two batteries** wedged between rocks on the debris pile.
- **School Flashback — St. Sybil (Third)**: A flashback triggers in the narrow passage beyond the collapse.
  - **Objective**: Find Jessica in the school basement. Navigate through the dark, flooded basement corridors.
  - The truth about what happened to Jessica is further revealed. Follow the lit corridor to the flashback's end.

#### The Temple Antechamber
The caves open into a large man-made antechamber — the entrance to the underground temple complex.
- **Collectible — Document (Temple Records)**: On an altar stone in the antechamber's center.
- Knoth is visible through a cracked wall speaking to his remaining followers. Do not approach — stay crouched and move along the far side of the antechamber.
- Exit through the carved stone doorway at the antechamber's far end.

---

### Objective: Find Lynn in the Temple

#### The Temple Inner Sanctum
Blake moves deeper into the temple structure.
- The inner sanctum is lit by burning oil bowls. Multiple cultists guard the central passage.
- **Strategy**: Wait at the sanctum entrance. Two guards face each other at the center. When they both look toward the far wall, move crouched along the right side passage to bypass them.
- **Collectible — Document (Knoth's Final Sermon)**: On the altar at the sanctum's far end, just before the sealed inner door.

#### Sealed Door Mechanism
The inner door to Lynn's chamber is sealed by a heavy wooden bar on the far side.
- Look for the narrow side window to the right of the door. Aim your camera through the window gap. A cutscene triggers — Blake sees Lynn inside.
- Locate the small maintenance crawlspace under the right-hand wall. Crawl through to emerge inside Lynn's chamber.
- Cutscene: Blake reunites with Lynn. Lynn is gravely ill and in an advanced state of unexplained pregnancy. She insists she was not pregnant before the crash. Chapter 5 ends.

## Chapter 6: Revelation

### Objective: Escape the Temple with Lynn

#### The Temple Exit Passage
Blake must guide Lynn through the temple. Lynn walks on her own but slowly.
- **Collectible — Document (Lynn's Journal)**: On the floor near the sealed door where Lynn was kept.
- Follow the same passage used to enter, now in reverse. The cultists have largely abandoned their posts.
- **Collectible — Recording (The Temple)**: Record the temple's main chamber as you pass through it.

#### Knoth's Congregation Hall
Blake and Lynn reach Knoth's main underground hall to find a horrific scene: Knoth has instructed his followers to take their own lives. The hall is filled with the dead.
- No enemies are active in this section. Move through the hall toward the exit.
- **Collectible — Document (Knoth's Last Words)**: On Knoth's central pulpit.

#### Knoth Confrontation
Knoth himself is still alive at the hall's far end, kneeling in prayer.
- A cutscene triggers: Knoth tells Blake that the child Lynn carries is the Antichrist and that its birth will end the world. Knoth then slits his own throat.
- Blake and Lynn continue through the hall's rear exit.

---

### Objective: Reach the Surface

#### The Ascent Tunnels
Blake and Lynn must climb back up through the tunnel system to reach the surface.
- The tunnels here are linear and contain no active enemies.
- Collect the **two batteries** in the first tunnel junction.
- **Collectible — Document (Engineer's Final Report)**: On a crate near a collapsed tunnel section.

#### The Murkoff Equipment Room
Blake and Lynn pass through a hidden chamber containing modern equipment belonging to the Murkoff Corporation — the shadowy company central to the broader Outlast lore.
- **Collectible — Recording (The Equipment)**: Record the Murkoff equipment array and the corporate insignia on the wall.
- **Collectible — Document (Murkoff Directive)**: On the desk next to the main equipment console.
- The equipment reveals the truth: Murkoff has been conducting experimental microwave frequency broadcasts from this location to manipulate the population, causing mass hallucinations, hysteria, and the cultists' apocalyptic beliefs. Blake's own St. Sybil flashbacks were caused by the broadcasts.
- Interact with the console. A cutscene triggers — the equipment sparks and explodes.

---

### Objective: Get Lynn to Safety

#### The Collapse Escape
The underground structure begins to collapse after the equipment explosion. This is the game's final chase sequence.
- Sprint forward along the crumbling tunnel. Do not stop for any reason.
- Vault over the collapsing debris sections and sprint up the tunnel ramps.
- Slide under the falling ceiling section at the tunnel's final straight.
- Blake and Lynn emerge into open air at the surface. Collapse onto the ground.

#### The Open Field — Dawn
Blake and Lynn are in an open desert field at dawn, far from the village.
- **No enemies are present**. Walk forward with Lynn.
- Lynn collapses. She goes into labor.
- **School Flashback — St. Sybil (Final)**: The final, complete flashback triggers. The full truth about Jessica's fate at St. Sybil is revealed, as well as Father Loutermilch's role in her death.
- The flashback ends. Blake returns to Lynn.

---

### Objective: The Ending

#### Final Cutscene
Lynn gives birth. The infant does not cry. A blinding white light fills the sky above, emanating from the direction of the now-destroyed Murkoff equipment.
- Lynn dies from the trauma of the birth.
- Blake holds the silent, motionless child. The white light intensifies and washes over everything.
- Blake's fate is left ambiguous as the screen goes white.
- Credits roll. The game is complete.

### Collectibles Completion Note
> After completing the game, use the Chapter Select feature from the main menu to replay any chapter and collect missed Documents or Recordings. Your collectible progress is saved separately from chapter progress and carries over across chapter replays.
