# Resident Evil 4 Remake (2023) Collectibles Guide and Trophy Roadmap

This reference guide provides structured lists of all collectibles (Weapons, Weapon Parts, Crafting Recipes, Small Keys, Merchant Requests, and Clockwork Castellans) and the optimal Trophy/Achievement Roadmap, formatted for RAG recursive chunking.

---

## Part 1: Collectibles Overview

Resident Evil 4 Remake features 180 total collectible locations for 100% completion. Collectibles include:
- **16 Clockwork Castellans** (unlocks "Revolution Wind-Up" trophy)
- **104 Treasures** (39 in the Village, 41 in the Castle, 24 on the Island; unlocks "Bandit", "Raider", and "Burglar" trophies)
- **19 Merchant Requests** (unlocks "Jack of All Trades" trophy)
- **29 Weapons** (unlocks "Gun Fanatic" trophy)
- **7 Weapon Parts** (completes "Parts Enthusiast" challenge)
- **5 Crafting Recipes** (completes "Crafty Crafter" challenge)
- **8 Small Keys** (needed to open locked drawers containing treasures)

---

## Part 2: Clockwork Castellans Locations (16 Total)

There is exactly 1 Castellan figurine hidden in each of the 16 story chapters. They emit a distinct ticking/clanking sound when nearby.

- **Castellan 1 (Chapter 1)**: Lakeside Settlement. Go to the central house behind the dynamite traps, enter the back window, and look up in the roof rafters.
- **Castellan 2 (Chapter 2)**: Abandoned Factory gate trail. Hug the left wall past the gate, enter the small wooden hut, and find it in the corner behind the door.
- **Castellan 3 (Chapter 3)**: Quarry Merchant area. Drop down the ladder to the lower dock level, walk east toward the water, and look through the gap between the overhang and the boxes.
- **Castellan 4 (Chapter 4)**: Forest Altar. Use the Insignia Key to open the settlement gate, climb the ladder to the altar, and look through the northern fence slats to see it on the ground.
- **Castellan 5 (Chapter 5)**: Village Chief's Manor. Go upstairs to the bedroom, pull the lever behind the crooked frame, climb the ladder to the attic, and find it behind the yellow crate on the left.
- **Castellan 6 (Chapter 6)**: Checkpoint path. Before entering the gate to the Checkpoint area, look to the right of the trail next to the bonfire.
- **Castellan 7 (Chapter 7)**: Dungeon storage room. Solve the Garrador fight, climb the ladder to the storage room, and look on top of the shelves in the middle of the room.
- **Castellan 8 (Chapter 8)**: Castle Battlements. Drop down the right side of the tower while avoiding the giant's rocks, enter the second tower, and find it on the rooftop behind the junk.
- **Castellan 9 (Chapter 9)**: Courtyard Maze. Tucked in the northwest corner of the maze behind wooden boards.
- **Castellan 10 (Chapter 10)**: Sewer waterway. Directly outside the Merchant room, look through the iron bars of the sewer grate to find it hanging upside down.
- **Castellan 11 (Chapter 11)**: Stopover building. After the first minecart ride, stand inside the stopover room and look at the top of the outer wall structure.
- **Castellan 12 (Chapter 12)**: Clocktower. Enter the ground floor, walk to the northwest corner before going upstairs, and locate it sitting on a box.
- **Castellan 13 (Chapter 13)**: Wharf/Cargo Depot path. Move past the first rocket launcher Ganado, go to the southeast corner next to the forklift, and locate it on the ground to the right of the forklift.
- **Castellan 14 (Chapter 14)**: Campsite tunnel. Go through the gate past the Amber Storeroom, walk south in the tunnel, enter the small concrete building, and find it sitting on top of the green lockers.
- **Castellan 15 (Chapter 15)**: Specimen Storage. Go past the request letter box to the northern room containing body bags. It is sitting on the wooden beams above the treasure chest.
- **Castellan 16 (Chapter 16)**: Timed escape route. During the self-destruct run, enter the room with the green forklift and find it sitting on top of the boxes in the back right corner.

## Part 3: Merchant Requests List (19 Total)

Complete all requests and talk to the Merchant to receive Spinels.

### Village Region (Requests 1 to 8)
- **Request 1: Destroy the Blue Medallions 1 (Chapter 1)**: Farm area. Shoot the 5 Blue Medallions. (Rewards: 3 Spinels).
- **Request 2: Pest Control (Chapter 2)**: Abandoned Factory. Kill 3 rats. (Rewards: 3 Spinels).
- **Request 3: Viper Hunter (Chapter 3)**: Town Hall Merchant. Collect and sell 3 Vipers. (Rewards: 4 Spinels).
- **Request 4: Grave Robber (Chapter 3)**: Graveyard. Deface the matching emblems on the double graves. (Rewards: 2 Spinels).
- **Request 5: Destroy the Blue Medallions 2 (Chapter 3)**: Quarry entrance. Shoot 5 Blue Medallions. (Rewards: 4 Spinels).
- **Request 6: Egg Hunt (Chapter 4)**: Boat dock. Find and sell a Gold Chicken Egg. (Rewards: 3 Spinels).
- **Request 7: Catch Me A Big Fish (Chapter 4)**: Lakeside Settlement. Harpoon the Lunker Bass near the Boat House and sell it. (Rewards: 4 Spinels).
- **Request 8: A Savage Mutt (Chapter 5)**: Town Hall Merchant. Defeat the unique wolf in the Village Square. (Rewards: 8 Spinels).

### Castle Region (Requests 9 to 15)
- **Request 9: Destroy the Blue Medallions 3 (Chapter 7)**: Chapel gate. Shoot 6 Blue Medallions near the cannon walls. (Rewards: 5 Spinels).
- **Request 10: Destroy the Blue Medallions 4 (Chapter 9)**: Grand Hall. Shoot 6 Blue Medallions in the Grand Hall, Dining Hall, Armory, and Gallery. (Rewards: 5 Spinels).
- **Request 11: More Pest Control (Chapter 9/10)**: Grand Hall. Kill 3 rats in the Dining Hall, Armory corridor, and library corridor (accessible in Ch. 10). (Rewards: 3 Spinels).
- **Request 12: Merciless Knight (Chapter 10)**: Mausoleum. Defeat the unique Gold Knight and his guards. (Rewards: 8 Spinels).
- **Request 13: Insect Hive (Chapter 11)**: Hive area. Destroy the 4 yellow glowing sacks on the ceiling. (Rewards: 4 Spinels).
- **Request 14: Jewel Thief (Chapter 12)**: Clocktower Merchant. Shoot the crow's nest outside the Courtyard gate, retrieve the Scratched Emerald, and sell it. (Rewards: 3 Spinels).
- **Request 15: The Disgrace of the Salazar Family (Chapter 12)**: Clocktower Merchant. Throw a chicken egg at Ramon Salazar's portrait in the Throne Room. (Rewards: 4 Spinels).

### Island Region (Requests 16 to 19)
- **Request 16: Destroy the Blue Medallions 5 (Chapter 14)**: Cargo Depot entrance. Shoot 5 Blue Medallions. (Rewards: 5 Spinels).
- **Request 17: Even More Pest Control (Chapter 14)**: Waste Disposal Merchant. Defeat 4 rats in Waste Disposal. (Rewards: 3 Spinels).
- **Request 18: The Wandering Dead (Chapter 14)**: Cargo Depot exit. Defeat the unique upgraded Regenerador in the Incubation Lab. (Rewards: 8 Spinels).
- **Request 19: Destroy the Blue Medallions 6 (Chapter 15)**: Cliffside Ruins exit. Shoot 5 Blue Medallions. (Rewards: 5 Spinels).

## Part 8: Trophy Roadmap

To achieve the Platinum Trophy ("Cuz Boredom Kills Me") in the most efficient manner, follow this 6-step roadmap:

### Step 1: Mercenaries Mode (Unlock Handcannon)
Download the free Mercenaries DLC. Beat the 3 maps with an S-rank (using Leon is recommended, build up a 100-kill combo). This unlocks the **Handcannon** bonus weapon, making subsequent Professional runs much easier.

### Step 2: 100% assisted Run (Collectibles and Saves Setup)
Play on Assisted difficulty. Collect all 16 Castellans, 104 Treasures, and complete all 19 Merchant Requests. Max out Leon's health with green/yellow/red herb combinations. Fully upgrade the Blacktail pistol.
- **Point of No Return Gate**: Keep a manual save at the Chapter 16 Merchant before entering the Saddler boss fight.
- **Knife Setup**: Finish the game, purchase the **Primal Knife** in the Bonus Menu, reload your Chapter 16 save, and upgrade the Primal Knife to make it unbreakable. Finish the game again to save the NG+ file.

### Step 3: Minimalist and Silent Run (NG+ Assisted)
Start New Game+ on Assisted using your completed save. Complete the game under two strict conditions:
1. **Minimalist**: Use ONLY handguns and knives (no grenades, eggs, or special weapons).
2. **Silent Stranger**: Never interact with the Merchant even once. (Rely on your fully upgraded pistol and unbreakable Primal Knife from Step 2).

### Step 4: Professional A-Rank Run (NG+ Professional)
Start New Game+ on Professional difficulty. Buy the **Infinite Rocket Launcher** from the first Merchant for 2 Million ptas (sell upgraded weapons if short on funds). Complete the game in under 7:00 hours to unlock the **Chicago Sweeper** bonus weapon and the **Knight Armor** for Ashley (makes her invulnerable and immune to abduction).

### Step 5: Hardcore S+ Run (New Game Hardcore)
Start a completely fresh **New Game** (not New Game+) on Hardcore difficulty. 
- **Setup**: Equip Ashley with the Knight Armor. Retrieve the **Chicago Sweeper** from storage. Complete all Merchant Requests in the Village area to gather 30 Spinels, then buy the **Exclusive Upgrade Ticket** in Chapter 7 to give the Chicago Sweeper infinite ammo. Complete the game in under 5:30 hours. (Unlocks the **Chicken Hat** which reduces incoming damage by 50%).

### Step 6: Frugalist Run (NG+ Assisted)
Start New Game+ on Assisted difficulty. Equip the Chicken Hat. Complete the game without using a single healing item (Frugalist trophy). Use the Infinite Rocket Launcher to defeat all bosses in one hit and run past standard enemies.
