# Resident Evil 4 Remake (2023) Complete Walkthrough and Collectibles Guide

## Introduction and General Overview

This walkthrough covers all story objectives, key items, puzzles, and collectible locations (Treasures, Castellans, and Merchant Requests) in a structured format designed for optimal information retrieval.

---

## Chapter 1 Walkthrough

### Objective: See What's Taking So Long

#### Entering the Hunter's Lodge
After the initial cutscenes, you gain control of Leon. Follow the dirt path forward until you reach a house and enter through the front door. Look around the hallway and rooms if desired, then head to the far end of the hallway and open the door at the end to trigger a cutscene. 

#### Key Item: Hunter's Lodge Key
After the cutscene, pick up the **Hunter's Lodge Key** from the ground next to you.

#### Entering the Basement
Return to the hallway and use the **Hunter's Lodge Key** on the door to the right marked with red paint. Head down the stairs and follow the linear path to the end of the basement to trigger another cutscene.

---

### Objective: Escape from the Hunter's Lodge

#### Fighting Your Way Out
After the scene, backtrack out of the basement. Upon returning to the main hallway, an enemy will walk out of the room that was previously blocked on the right. Neutralize the threat, enter the open room, and go up the stairs to trigger the next cutscene.

---

### Objective: Head for the Lake

#### Path to the Village Gate
Following the cutscene, you will find yourself outside the lodge. Follow the trail forward toward another building. 
- **Destructible Barrels**: Break the destructible barrel outside the house either by interacting with it or by using a knife attack (pressing the slash button without aiming) to get supplies.
- **Typewriter (Save Point)**: Inside this building, you will find the first Typewriter of the game. Use it to save your progress. Note that manual saves at Typewriters are the only way to save on Professional difficulty.

#### First Combat Encounter
Continue down the path. You will encounter normal Ganado (human-looking) enemies. Stun them by shooting them in the head or legs, then run in and perform a physical melee attack to finish them off and conserve ammunition. Check the small hut on the left side of the path for extra resources, then follow the path to the large wooden gate at the end and push it open to enter the Village Square.

#### Village Square Siege
Entering the Village Square triggers a large, arena-style combat encounter. You must defend yourself against a mob of villagers and a Chainsaw Villager. 
- **Survival Trigger**: You do not need to kill every enemy; the siege ends after you survive long enough for the church bell to ring.
- **Chainsaw Villager Drop**: If you manage to defeat the Chainsaw Villager, he drops an **Emerald**. Note that this does not count towards the 39 official Village Treasures.
- **W-870 Shotgun (Weapon)**: Run to the second building on the left (the two-story house). Enter and block the ground-floor window with the nearby bookshelf to delay enemies. Head upstairs and grab the **W-870 Shotgun** hanging on the wall. 
- **Treasure: Velvet Blue**: From the upper floor of the shotgun house, break the window to the right of where the shotgun was hanging. Hop out onto the rooftop and run along the ledge to the right to find a **Velvet Blue** sitting on the roof peak.

---

### Farm Area Exploration

#### Village Exit
Once the church bell rings, the villagers will depart. Search the area for leftover supplies and proceed through the northeastern path.
- **Treasure: Ruby**: In the last shed on the left before reaching the iron gate, open the chest immediately to the right of the door to collect a **Ruby**.

#### Entering the Farm
Go through the gate to reach the Farm. The first building on your right contains a Typewriter. Clear the normal Ganados patrolling the farm area.
- **Treasure: Pearl Pendant**: To the left of the shed, find the windmill with a silver cage hanging from its side. Wait until the windmill rotates the cage to the right (away from the dirty well), then shoot the cage down.
- **Merchant Request: Destroy the Blue Medallions**: Interact with the blue leaflet stuck to the wall near the northern gate. This request requires destroying 5 Blue Medallions hidden around the farm.
  1. *Blue Medallion 1*: Hanging on the northern corner of the building containing the typewriter.
  2. *Blue Medallion 2*: Climb the fence next to the barn and look through the open window of the barn wall.
  3. *Blue Medallion 3*: Inside the small wooden hut located to the right of the windmill, tucked up in the rafters.
  4. *Blue Medallion 4*: Stand near the northern gate and look up at the second-story window of the large barn.
  5. *Blue Medallion 5*: Walk to the left side of the large barn, look over the fence toward the windmill balcony, and spot it hanging underneath the balcony structures.

---

### Windmill Gate and Lakeside Path

#### Heading Inside the Windmill
Head to the large northern gate and enter the wooden door. Watch for a tripwire bomb inside. You can crawl underneath it, disarm it manually, or lure enemies into it.
- **Finding the Cog**: Climb the ladder to the second level. Exit through the southeastern door to reach a balcony, then drop down to the fenced-in area below.
- **Treasure: Flagon**: Open the red chest in this lower area to retrieve the **Flagon** (a socketed treasure).
- **Treasure: Ruby**: Break the padlock on the nearby door to enter the lower level of the barn. In the left corner, open the drawer to find a **Ruby**.
- **Key Item: Wooden Cog & Weapon: Kitchen Knife**: On the table to the right in the same room, pick up the **Wooden Cog** and a disposable **Kitchen Knife**.

#### Opening the Gate
Exit the room, climb back up the windmill ladder to the second floor, and place the **Wooden Cog** into the gear mechanism on the wall. Pull the lever next to it to open the large gate below.

#### Path to Lakeside Settlement
Pass through the newly opened gate. Proceed forward past the large house and go to the right of it. Push the wooden cart out of the way. Proceed along the path, avoiding falling rubble and Ganados to reach the Lakeside Settlement.

#### Lakeside Settlement Exploration
Be cautious of enemies throwing dynamite in this area. You can shoot the dynamite out of their hands.
- **Castellan 1**: Walk to the far end of the settlement, past the dynamite traps. Find the central house, go around to the rear, and climb in through the window. In the second room, look up at the ceiling rafters to find and shoot **Castellan 1**. Destroying this unlocks the "Revolt Against the Revolting" achievement.
- **Finishing the Chapter**: Approach the main house. Break the padlock on the door, enter the hallway, and disarm the tripwire. Push the bookshelf out of the way, defeat the Ganado in the next room, and open the trapdoor. Drop down and examine the body at the end of the tunnel to complete Chapter 1.

## Chapter 2 Walkthrough

### Objective: Head for the Church

#### Retrieving Your Gear
Follow the path out of the room.
- **Weapon: Kitchen Knife**: Take the **Kitchen Knife** from the corpse directly ahead.
- **Stealth and Parry Tutorials**: Crouch and approach the first enemy from behind for a stealth kill. Proceed to the next hallway and perform a timed parry against the next enemy.
- **Treasure: Sapphire**: In the same room, open the second oven on the left wall to find a **Sapphire**.
- **Reclaiming Equipment**: Continue to the final room of the factory, interact with the switch to crank open the door on the other side, and run back around to retrieve your gear.

#### Meeting the Merchant
Exit the room and proceed forward to find the Merchant.
- **Merchant Request: Pest Control**: Grab the request letter from the wall on the right. You must kill 3 rats in the factory area:
  1. *Rat 1*: In the destroyed hallway with the two windows where the final enemies were walking.
  2. *Rat 2*: In the central room where you had the Parry tutorial.
  3. *Rat 3*: In the adjacent main room near the entrance.
- **Treasure Map: Village**: Trade 1 Spinel with the Merchant to acquire the **Treasure Map: Village**.

#### Navigating the Valley
Follow the path out the southeast of the merchant area to reach the Valley.
- **Key Item: Small Key**: Enter the large building on the right side of the valley. Vault through the southeastern window, enter the small room on the left, and retrieve a **Small Key** from the box.
- **Key Item: Hexagonal Emblem**: Climb the ladder to the roof of the building and pick up the **Hexagonal Emblem** from the corner structure.
- **Treasure: Ruby**: Go to the path on the northeast side of the valley. At the top, open the chest under the small structure to collect a **Ruby**.
- **Valley Gate Crank**: Interact with the crank to the left of the chest to open the gate shortcut.

#### Backtracking for Treasures
- **Treasure: Elegant Mask**: Return to the factory room where you reclaimed your gear. Use the **Small Key** on the locked drawer to obtain the **Elegant Mask**.

#### Unlocking the Gate
Go to the wall opposite the Merchant. Place the **Hexagonal Emblem** into the pedestal. Rotate it so it mirrors the slot (the phoenix symbol must face the wall), then press it in to crank the gate open.

#### Path to the Village Chief's Manor
Pass through the gate and deal with the three Ganados. 
- **Treasure: Sapphire**: Before going up the stairs, crouch and slide through the small opening on the left side of the wall. Open the chest to collect a **Sapphire**.
- **Castellan 2**: Proceed up the stairs. Enter the first wooden hut on the left side. Look in the corner of the hut behind the door to find and destroy **Castellan 2**.
- **Treasure: Sapphire**: Exit the hut, look up at the nearby tree, and shoot down the hanging metal cage to collect a **Sapphire**.
- **Treasure: Pearl Pendant**: Walk to the left to find a metal cage hanging over a well. Shoot the wooden board holding the well's lid up first, then shoot the cage down to safely retrieve the clean **Pearl Pendant**.
- **Defeating the Chainsaw Villager**: Proceed up the trail. A Chainsaw Villager will attack. You can lead him into the bear traps scattered on the path or run up the stairs and use the tripwire to damage him.
- **Treasure: Ruby**: Enter the Village Chief's Manor through the back door. In the entry hallway on the ground floor, open the drawer on the right to find a **Ruby**.
- **Key Item: Small Key**: Enter the main dining room on the ground floor. Pick up the **Small Key** from the corner table.
- **Key Item: Crystal Marble**: Interact with the lock on the large wardrobe downstairs. Set the dials to: **Crops – Pig – Baby**. Retrieve the **Crystal Marble**.
- **Key Item: Insignia Key**: Go upstairs, insert the **Crystal Marble** into the bedroom door, and rotate it until the bubbles align with the pattern behind it. Enter the room, interact with the desk drawer in the corner, and collect the **Insignia Key** to complete Chapter 2.

## Chapter 3 Walkthrough

### Objective: Head for the Church

#### Returning to the Village Square
Head downstairs and exit the front door. 
- **Freeing the Dog**: Go around the side of the house and interact with the bear trap to free the dog.
- **Village Square Shortcut**: Head to the gate opposite the house. Use the **Insignia Key** to unlock it.
- **Treasure: Vintage Compass**: Enter the second house on the left in the Village Square. Use the **Small Key** on the locked drawer to obtain the **Vintage Compass**.
- **Exiting the Square**: Navigate past the collapsed tower by going through the last house on the right, then use the **Insignia Key** on the iron door to proceed.

#### Town Hall and Graveyard
- **Treasure: Velvet Blue**: Inside the Town Hall, look up at the ceiling and shoot down the hanging metal cage to collect a **Velvet Blue**.
- **Merchant Request: Viper Hunter**: Exit the Town Hall to find the next Merchant. Collect the request letter on the wall to sell 3 Vipers.
- **Merchant Request: Grave Robber**: In the center of the graveyard, locate the twin graves with matching emblems and destroy both emblems to complete the request.
- **Treasure: Elegant Headdress**: Follow the path around the right side of the Church to the back. Open the chest at the dead end to collect the **Elegant Headdress**.
- **Save Room**: Enter the side room next to the Church, examine the map, then drop down the hatch.

---

### Objective: Cross the Lake

#### Path to the Quarry
Follow the path past the Church.
- **Viper 1**: Inside the building, break the crate on the table to collect **Viper 1**.
- **Merchant Request: Destroy the Blue Medallions 2**: Upon entering the Quarry, grab the request letter from the wall.
- **Trophy: Shield Your Eyes**: Throw a flash grenade at the crows on the ground in the Quarry to kill at least 3 with one explosion.
- **Treasure: Ruby**: Shoot down the hanging lantern from the overhead wires to collect a **Ruby**.
- **Blue Medallion 1**: Look up at the wooden scaffolding near the lantern to shoot **Blue Medallion 1**.

#### Quarry Merchant and Fish Farm
- **Shooting Gallery**: Enter the elevator next to the Merchant to access the Shooting Gallery.
- **Castellan 3**: Drop down the ladder to the lower level. Walk toward the water and look through the gap between the overhang and the boxes to destroy **Castellan 3**.
- **Key Item: Hexagon Piece A**: Open the chest directly opposite the Merchant to collect **Hexagon Piece A**.
- **Blue Medallion 2**: Drop down to the lower level by the lake dock and look under the dock to shoot **Blue Medallion 2**.
- **Blue Medallion 3**: Enter the Fish Farm. Climb up to the first raised platform on the right and look inside the wooden shack to shoot **Blue Medallion 3**.
- **Viper 2 & 3**: Shoot and collect two Vipers in the pathways in front of this shack.
- **Blue Medallion 4**: Look through the gap in the wooden wall on the left side of the main water path to shoot **Blue Medallion 4**.
- **Treasure: Antique Pipe**: Shoot down the silver container hanging from the scaffold over the northern water to collect the **Antique Pipe**.
- **Blue Medallion 5 & Key Item: Hexagon Piece B**: Follow the platform to the end, drop down under the wooden walkway, and shoot **Blue Medallion 5**. Open the chest next to it to collect **Hexagon Piece B**.
- **Key Item: Small Key**: Enter the small wooden building on the left and open the drawer to find a **Small Key**.
- **Key Item: Boat Fuel**: Break the padlock on the next building to collect the **Boat Fuel**.

#### Backtracking for Treasures
- **Treasure: Brass Pocket Watch**: Return to the Quarry Merchant's building. Drop down the ladder next to him and use the **Small Key** to open the locked drawer to obtain the **Brass Pocket Watch**.
- **Completing requests**: Speak to the Merchant and sell the 3 Vipers to complete the request.

---

### Objective: Defeat the Lake Monster

#### First Boss Fight: Del Lago
Board the boat and use the **Boat Fuel** to launch.
- **Strategy**: Steer with the stick to avoid logs. Throw harpoons at the monster's back. When it charges the boat, throw a harpoon directly into its open mouth to stop it. Continue until Del Lago is defeated.

---

## Chapter 4 Walkthrough

### Objective: Find the Key to the Church

#### Lakeside Cave
Follow the path out of the boathouse.
- **Plaga Guadaña Encounter**: Shoot the parasite that sprouts from the enemy's neck.
- **Mural Cave**: Interact with the stone mural on the wall to mark the location of the two heads on your map.

---

### Objective: Investigate the Two Locations

#### Key Item: Old Wayshrine Key
Follow the path to the river exit. Turn right to find a small altar. Pick up the **Old Wayshrine Key** from the chest.

#### Merchant Request: Egg Hunt
Grab the request letter from the broken stone wall near the boat dock.

#### Lake Exploration
Board the boat to navigate the lake.
- **Treasure: Alexandrite**: Open the chest in the center of the shipwreck located in the middle of the lake.
- **Treasure and Weapon: Red9 Pistol**: Open the ornate chest at the bow of the shipwreck to collect the **Red9 Pistol**.
- **Treasure: Velvet Blue**: Go to the chicken farm island on the eastern side of the lake. Collect the **Velvet Blue** sitting on a barrel.
- **Gold Chicken Egg**: Pick up the **Gold Chicken Egg** from the ground at the back of the chicken farm.

#### Large Cave Shrine (First Location)
Dock at the northeastern cave.
- **Key Item: Blasphemer’s Head**: Solve the button puzzle by pressing the three buttons matching the yellow symbols painted on the cave walls. Enter the room and collect the **Blasphemer's Head**.
- **Key Item: Hexagon Piece C**: Open the chest to the right of the statue to collect **Hexagon Piece C**.
- **Treasure: Ruby**: Climb the ladder and shoot down the hanging cage to collect a **Ruby**.

#### Lakeside Settlement Cleanup
Dock at the northwest settlement.
- **Treasure: Pearl Bangle**: Use the **Old Wayshrine Key** on the wayshrine behind the house to retrieve the **Pearl Bangle**.
- **Request: Catch Me A Big Fish**: Use the **Insignia Key** on the locked door in the settlement. Enter the tunnel and grab the request letter from the pillar on the left.
- **Castellan 4**: Exit the tunnel, enter the house, climb the ladder to the Forest Altar, and shoot **Castellan 4** through the fence.
- **Key Item: Small Key**: Pick up the **Small Key** from the wooden cart next to the altar.
- **Treasure: Gold Bar**: Enter the main settlement house and use the **Small Key** on the drawer on the left to obtain the **Gold Bar**.
- **Treasure: Red Gemstone Ring**: Follow the path into the basement and retrieve the **Red Gemstone Ring** from the box.

#### Small Cave Shrine (Second Location)
Dock at the southern cave shrine.
- **Treasure: Depraved Idol**: Place the 3 Hexagon Pieces into the altar.
  - *Standard Solution*: Spin left once, spin top once.
  - *Hardcore Solution*: Spin right twice, spin left twice, spin top once.
  - *Reward*: Open the altar to collect the **Depraved Idol**.
- **Lunker Bass (Catch Me A Big Fish)**: Board the boat and drive to the waters in front of the Boat House. Harpoon the largest fish swimming near the boathouse and collect the **Lunker Bass**.
- **Merchant Detour**: Sell the **Gold Chicken Egg** and **Lunker Bass** to the Merchant to complete the requests.
- **Treasure: Splendid Bangle**: Drive the boat into the western tunnel under the Quarry. Open the wayshrine with the **Old Wayshrine Key** to retrieve the **Splendid Bangle**.
- **Treasure: Alexandrite**: Shoot down the hanging lantern above the wayshrine to collect an **Alexandrite**.
- **Key Item: Apostate’s Head**: Follow the cave path to the end. Climb the ladder and solve the button puzzle using the yellow cave paintings to unlock the door. Retrieve the **Apostate's Head**.
- **Treasure: Velvet Blue**: Shoot down the hanging metal cage under the stone bridge to collect a **Velvet Blue**.

---

### Objective: Get the Key to the Church

#### Key Item: Church Insignia
Return to the Mural Cave. Place the **Blasphemer's Head** and **Apostate's Head** onto the pedestals to retrieve the **Church Insignia**.

---

### Objective: Head for the Church

#### Church Gate
Return to the Quarry Merchant's dock and take the path toward the Church.
- **Treasure: Butterfly Lamp**: Use the **Old Wayshrine Key** on the wayshrine on the path to collect the **Butterfly Lamp**.

---

### Objective: Defeat the Giant

#### Second Boss Fight: El Gigante
- **Strategy**: Shoot the giant's head until the parasite sprouts from his back. Shoot the parasite. Use the dog ally's distraction to stagger the giant, then climb onto his back to perform a knife slash on the parasite. Repeat until defeated.
- **Treasure: Yellow Diamond**: Collect the **Yellow Diamond** dropped by the giant.

---

### Objective: Look for Ashley in the Church

#### Stained Glass Puzzle
- **Key Item: Small Key**: Enter the Church and pick up the **Small Key** from the table on the right side of the altar.
- **Treasure: Yellow Diamond**: Go to the side save room outside the Church and use the **Small Key** on the drawer to collect the **Yellow Diamond**.
- **Key Item: Blue Dial**: Go down the right aisle of the Church and retrieve the **Blue Dial** from the cabinet.
- **Puzzle**: Pull the lever left of the altar, insert the **Blue Dial**, and rotate the Red, Green, and Blue glass dials until the stained glass patterns align with the central symbol. Climb the ladder on the left and enter the room to rescue Ashley and complete Chapter 4.

## Chapter 5 Walkthrough

### Objective: Escape from the Church

#### Leaving the Church
- **Treasure: Yellow Diamond**: Shoot down the metal cage hanging from the ceiling in the attic room where you start the chapter to obtain a **Yellow Diamond**.
- **Attic Ladder**: Boost Ashley up to drop the ladder. Climb up and jump out of the window to exit the Church.

---

### Objective: Head for the Extraction Point

#### Village Square and Chief's Manor
- **Trophy: Talk About Near-Death Experience!**: Allow a Ganado to grab Ashley, then shoot the enemy while they walk away to rescue her.
- **Merchant Request: A Savage Mutt**: Pick up the request letter from the wall outside the Town Hall.
- **Treasure: Elegant Bangle**: Proceed to the Village Square. Go to the locked house in the northwest corner, boost Ashley through the window to unlock the door, and open the chest inside to collect the **Elegant Bangle**.
- **Castellan 5**: Go to the Village Chief's Manor. Go upstairs to the bedroom, pull the lever behind the crooked painting, and boost Ashley into the attic. In the attic, shoot **Castellan 5** located behind the yellow crate on the left.
- **Treasure: Antique Camera**: Pick up the **Antique Camera** from the cabinet at the end of the attic.
- **Defeating the Savage Mutt**: Return to the Village Square. Defeat the Savage Mutt wolf to complete the request (rewards 8 Spinels).

---

### Objective: Stand Your Ground

#### The Windmill Villa
Take the alternate route past the farm to reach the windmill.
- **Treasure: Antique Pipe**: Open the wayshrine next to the second barn with the **Old Wayshrine Key** to collect the **Antique Pipe**.
- **Villa Siege**: Enter the villa. Block the windows with wooden planks. Defend the ground floor with Luis, then head upstairs to push the ladders down. Defeat the Hammer Brute to complete Chapter 5.

---

## Chapter 6 Walkthrough

### Objective: Escape from the Village

#### Border Canyon
Proceed forward to find the Merchant.
- **Treasure: Yellow Diamond**: Defeat the Ganados and the brute in the canyon. Climb the stairs on the left to find a **Yellow Diamond** in the chest.
- **Treasure: Chalice of Atonement**: Enter the first house on the left on the path ahead, defeat the enemy, and open the chest to collect the **Chalice of Atonement**.
- **Castellan 6**: Look to the right of the path near the bonfire to find and shoot **Castellan 6**.

#### Checkpoint Area
- **Treasure: Elegant Headdress**: Go upstairs in the Checkpoint building, exit the left door, and open the wayshrine on the platform to collect the **Elegant Headdress**.
- **Key Item: Checkpoint Crank**: Drop down to the lower level. Interact with the crank to spawn the Chainsaw Sisters. Defeat them and collect the **Checkpoint Crank** from their bodies.

---

### Objective: Flee from the Villagers

#### Chase Sequence
Call Ashley out of the locker and open the southern gate with the **Checkpoint Crank**. 
- **Strategy**: Run down the path without stopping. Throw stun grenades to bypass Ganados. Shoot the red explosive barrel to clear the path. Turn left past the cart at the end to trigger a cutscene.

---

### Objective: Defeat Mendez

#### Third Boss Fight: Chief Mendez (Slaughterhouse Boss Fight)
Follow the path to the final Merchant room, then go upstairs to start the fight.
- **First Phase**: Climb the ladders to the upper platform, dodge his tentacle attacks, and shoot the eye on his back. Perform the knife prompt when he falls.
- **Second Phase**: Dodge the burning wooden beams and shoot the explosive barrels in his hands. Shoot his body until he falls, then perform the final knife prompt.
- **Key Item: Mendez’s False Eye**: Pick up **Mendez's False Eye** from his body.

---

### Objective: Escape from the Slaughterhouse

#### Castle Gate
- **Treasure: Emerald**: Exit the building, follow the path, and shoot down the silver cage hanging from the tree branches on the left to collect the **Emerald**. Proceed across the bridge to enter the Castle and complete Chapter 6.

---

## Chapter 7 Walkthrough

### Objective: Head for the Courtyard

#### Meeting the Castle Merchant
At the start of the Castle, enter the first door on the right to find the Merchant. 
- **Treasure Map: Castle**: Buy the Castle Treasure Map from the trade section for 1 Spinel.
- **Weapon Upgrades**: The Blacktail handgun and Stingray rifle are now available for purchase.
- **Treasure: Vintage Compass**: Go behind the Merchant building. Circle around to the back and open the chest outside to find a **Vintage Compass**.

#### Chapel and Castle Walls
- **Weapon: Boot Knife**: Defeat the first Plaga Mandibula enemy in the Chapel. It drops a **Boot Knife** upon defeat.
- **Treasure: Elegant Bangle**: Proceed past the crossbow enemies. Look through the window on the stairs to shoot the yellow-painted counterweight cage. This raises the cannon. Climb the ladder and open the chest next to the cannon to collect the **Elegant Bangle**.
- **Trophy: Overkill**: Use the cannon to shoot one of the walking zealot enemies.
- **Opening the Gate**: Shoot the castle gate with the cannon to open the path.

#### Gate Blue Medallions
- **Merchant Request: Destroy the Blue Medallions 3**: Interact with the blue leaflet behind the gate you shot open. This request requires destroying 6 Blue Medallions.
  1. *Blue Medallion 1*: Go back to where the cannon was raised. Locate it hanging in the pavilion structure behind the cannon.
  2. *Blue Medallion 2*: Stand at the cannon and look north toward the ruined window across the gap.
  3. *Blue Medallion 3*: Hanging on the wall directly above the second medallion.
  4. *Blue Medallion 4*: Drop down from the cannon to the room below (where the counterweight was located). Look up to find it hanging from the ceiling.
  5. *Blue Medallion 5*: Climb the next ladder from the counterweight room and look left and down.
  6. *Blue Medallion 6*: Located just before the door leading back inside, to the left of the treasure chest.
- **Treasure: Elegant Bangle**: Open the chest next to Medallion 6 to collect an **Elegant Bangle**.

#### Audience Chamber and Dungeon
- **Treasure: Elegant Perfume Bottle**: Enter the main hall. Defeat the shield and helmet zealot enemies. Boost Ashley up the wall on the left side of the room. She will open the gate. Go through the gate and open the chest at the end of the corridor to collect the **Elegant Perfume Bottle**.
- **Treasure: Ruby**: Squeeze through the gap in the wall, turn left, and shoot down the hanging metal container to collect a **Ruby**.

---

### Objective: Look for Another Way Around

#### Retrieving the Dungeon Key
- **Key Item: Dungeon Key**: Enter the dungeon area. Follow the path to the door on the left, walk to the end of the passage, and pick up the **Dungeon Key** from the corpse.
- **Trophy: Never Heard It Coming**: You will fall through the floor and face a Garrador. Defeat this blind enemy using only knives. Crouch and sneak behind him, avoiding the hanging chains, and perform backstabs.
- **Dungeon Escape**: Use the crank handle to open the gate. Walk back to Ashley and use the **Dungeon Key** to open the middle gate.
- **Treasure: Spinel**: Climb the ladder to the storage room. Pick up the **Spinel** sitting on the cabinet.
- **Castellan 7**: Look immediately to the right of the Spinel to find **Castellan 7** sitting on top of the shelves in the middle of the room.

#### Treasury Sword Puzzle
Enter the Treasury.
- **Key Item: Golden Sword, Rusted Sword, Iron Sword**: Grab the three swords from the slots on the wall.
- **Key Item: Bloodied Sword**: Pull the string left of the gated wall, then shoot the three matching animal icons (Viper, Stag, Eagle) to open the gate. Pick up the **Bloodied Sword** from the pedestal.
- **Puzzle Solution**: Place the swords into the four wall frames in the following order from left to right: **Iron Sword, Golden Sword, Bloodied Sword, Rusted Sword**.
- **Treasure: Gold Bangle**: Go through the door and open the chest in the next corridor to collect the **Gold Bangle**.
- **Treasure: Golden Hourglass**: Proceed to the upper level of the Audience Chamber. Jump across the chandelier on the right, turn right, and drop through the hole in the floor. Open the chest to find the **Golden Hourglass**.

---

### Objective: Head for the Courtyard (Continued)

#### Water Hall Escape
Enter the Water Hall.
- **Key Item: Halo Wheel**: Drop down to the lower level. Walk to the center platform and retrieve the **Halo Wheel** from the statue.
- **Cranking the Stairs**: Place the **Halo Wheel** into the pedestal on the right side of the room. Turn it to lower the stairs, then retrieve the wheel.
- **Cranking the Bridge**: Return to the upper level. Place the **Halo Wheel** in the central pedestal to lower the next set of stairs. Proceed to the far end of the hall, use the wheel to raise the first part of the bridge, then boost Ashley up on the left platform. Protect her from zealots while she cranks the remaining two wheels. Once the bridge is raised, cross it and exit the hall. Boost Ashley over the ledge in the next room to complete Chapter 7.

## Chapter 8 Walkthrough

### Objective: Find Ashley

#### Start of the Chapter
- **Scratched Emerald (Jewel Thief request)**: At the start of the chapter, hang a left toward the Merchant flame. Shoot down the bird's nest on the roof of the entry building to retrieve the **Scratched Emerald**.
- **First Summoner Fight**: Follow the path inside the castle. You will encounter a Summoner. Shoot him from a distance to prevent him from turning other zealots into Plagas.
- **Key Item: Crimson Lantern**: Pick up the **Crimson Lantern** from the Summoner's body.
- **Treasure: Mirror with Pearls & Rubies**: Open the chest located behind where the Summoner was standing to collect the **Mirror with Pearls & Rubies**.
- **Opening the Gate**: Place the **Crimson Lantern** into the left hand of the door statue to open the passage.
- **Treasure: Extravagant Clock**: Proceed into the Bindery. Open the chest next to the fireplace to collect the **Extravagant Clock**.
- **Lithographic Stone Puzzle**: Pick up the three Lithographic Stones in the room. Place them on the door console and arrange them as follows:
  - *Left*: Orange Helmet in Hexagon
  - *Top*: Orange Shield in Square
  - *Right*: Blue Sword in Square
  - *Bottom*: Blue Armor in Hexagon
- **Key Item: Small Key**: Go through the door. Pick up the **Small Key** from the cabinet in the hallway.
- **Treasure: Brass Pocket Watch**: Backtrack to the Bindery. Use the **Small Key** on the locked drawer to collect the **Brass Pocket Watch**.

#### Castle Battlements
