# Resident Evil 4 Remake: Ada Separate Ways DLC Complete Walkthrough

This guide covers all objectives, key items, puzzles, and collectible locations for the *Separate Ways* DLC campaign, formatted in a clear hierarchy optimized for RAG recursive chunking.

---

## Chapter 1 Walkthrough

### Objective: Escape from the Castle

#### Upper Level Exploration
At the start of the chapter, exit the entrance hall of the castle.
- **Treasure: Elegant Headdress**: Go forward and around the small building on the upper level corner. Open the chest to collect the **Elegant Headdress**.

#### Lower Level Exploration
- **Treasure: Splendid Bangle**: Drop down to the lower level. Near the small stone structure, open the chest to collect the **Splendid Bangle**.
- **Weapon: SR M1903 Rifle & Scope**: Drop down to the platform where you must shoot the counterweight to raise the cannon. Pick up the **SR M1903** and its attached **Scope** from the table.
- **Treasure: Ruby**: Disable the cannons. Before grappling over to the next area, walk down the stairs and open the box to retrieve a **Ruby**.

---

## Chapter 2 Walkthrough

### Objective: Head for the Church

#### Retrieving Gear and Bypassing Gates
Grapple across the gaps to reach ground level.
- **Treasure: Red Gemstone Ring**: Locate the gate where the pig-headed Brute enemy spawns. Before the gate, walk up the stairs and open the chest to collect the **Red Gemstone Ring**.
- **Treasure: Flagon**: Go through the gate past the Villa. Follow the path leading around the back of the building and open the chest to find the **Flagon**.
- **Merchant Request: Eradicate the Blue Medallions**: Proceed along the cliffside to the puzzle door. Turn around and grapple to the high post to find the blue request letter. You must destroy 4 Blue Medallions:
  1. *Blue Medallion 1*: Located directly ahead, just past the request letter.
  2. *Blue Medallion 2*: Grapple back across to the puzzle door and turn around. It is hanging directly under the wooden platforms where the request letter was posted.
  3. *Blue Medallion 3*: Go to the end of the platform where the request letter was, facing the puzzle door. Look down to find it hanging in a tree.
  4. *Blue Medallion 4*: From the same position, look directly up to spot it hanging from the cliff face.

---

### Objective: Head for the Village Chief's Manor

#### Back to the Village
- **Treasure: Ruby**: Walk to the Church rooftop. Turn around and shoot the bird's nest in the corner of the roof to collect a **Ruby**.
- **Small Keys (Merchant)**: Meet the Merchant. Trade Spinels to buy **Small Keys** (5 keys are available, costing 2, 3, 4, 5, and 6 Spinels progressively).
- **Treasure: Velvet Blue**: Enter the first room of the building next to the Merchant. Shoot down the hanging silver cage to collect a **Velvet Blue**.
- **Treasure: Gold Bangle**: Go to the Village Square. Enter the house and use a **Small Key** to open the locked drawer, retrieving the **Gold Bangle**.
- **Treasure: Elegant Perfume Bottle**: Go to the barn in the northwestern corner of the Village Square. Grapple through the western window to enter the upper level, then open the chest to collect the **Elegant Perfume Bottle**.

---

## Chapter 3 Walkthrough

### Objective: Find a Clue on Luis's Whereabouts

#### Chief's Manor Investigation
- **Treasure: Extravagant Clock**: Enter the Village Chief's Manor. On the ground floor, open the chest above the fireplace to collect the **Extravagant Clock**.

---

### Objective: Flee from the Villagers

#### Escape Path
Exit the Manor. You will be pursued by Mendez.
- **Treasure: Red Beryl**: Quickly open the chest located just outside the Manor to retrieve the **Red Beryl**. (You can also backtrack for this later in the chapter).

---

### Objective: Enter the Abandoned Factory

#### Factory Entrance
- **Treasure: Emerald**: Grapple over the gate to escape Mendez. Follow the path to the left, look up, and shoot down the hanging lantern to collect the **Emerald**.
- **Merchant Request: Jacket of All Trades**: Retrieve the request letter next to the Abandoned Factory entrance.
  - *Objective*: Defeat the enemy wearing Leon's jacket inside the factory and sell the **Jacket** to the Merchant.
- **Weapon: Blast Crossbow**: Enter the factory. Pick up the **Blast Crossbow** sitting on the table.
- **Treasure: Gold Bar**: Use a **Small Key** on the locked drawer next to the crossbow to collect the **Gold Bar**.

---

### Objective: Head for the Villa

#### Clearing the Farm
- **Treasure: Antique Pipe**: Go to the northeastern exit of the Village Square. Enter the small building and open the box to collect the **Antique Pipe**.
- **Treasure: Pearl Pendant**: Go to the Farm. Shoot down the silver container hanging from the small windmill. Ensure it does not fall into the dirty water.
- **Treasure: Yellow Diamond**: Shoot down the lantern hanging from the side of the large northern windmill.
- **Treasure: Sapphire**: Go inside the large windmill and open the box on the opposite side to collect a **Sapphire**.

---

## Chapter 4 Walkthrough

### Objective: Go Back Upstairs

#### Under the Floorboards
- **Treasure: Gold Bar (L)**: Rescue Luis from the fire. Proceed up the stairs. Before climbing the ladder, turn around and grapple to the high platform. Open the chest to retrieve the **Gold Bar (L)**.

---

### Objective: Search for the Three Ingredients

#### Castle Halls and Maze
- **Treasure: Velvet Blue**: Enter the shield puzzle room. Shoot down the container hanging from the ceiling to collect a **Velvet Blue**.
- **Treasure: Vintage Compass**: Escape the large drill mechanism. Enter the next room on the right and open the chest to collect the **Vintage Compass**.
- **Treasure: Ornate Necklace**: Walk to the corridor before the stairs. Use a **Small Key** to open the locked drawer and retrieve the **Ornate Necklace**.
- **Treasure: Alexandrite**: Open the chest behind the wooden boards where the two knights break through.
- **Treasure: Elegant Chessboard**: Place the gold bottle into the puzzle pedestal. This opens the side gate; open the chest behind it to collect the **Elegant Chessboard**.
- **Merchant Request: Eradicate the Blue Medallions 2**: Retrieve the request letter next to the Merchant save room. You must destroy 5 Blue Medallions:
  1. *Blue Medallion 1*: Hanging underneath the overhead walkway near the maze entrance.
  2. *Blue Medallion 2*: Go east from the first medallion, walk up the stairs, and shoot it behind the locked gate.
  3. *Blue Medallion 3*: Located behind the iron bars in the southwest corner of the maze.
  4. *Blue Medallion 4*: Climb the ladder in the southeast corner of the maze. The medallion is at the top of the tower.
  5. *Blue Medallion 5*: Go to the center-north of the maze and look up at the tower wall.
- **Treasure: Blue Velvet**: Walk behind the stone fountain past the Merchant to find a **Blue Velvet**.
- **Treasure: Red Beryl**: Follow the path past the fountain to the second fountain. Open the box to collect a **Red Beryl**.
- **Treasure: Butterfly Lamp**: Go to the alcove where you shot the fifth medallion. Open the chest to collect the **Butterfly Lamp**.
- **Treasure: Sapphire**: Exit the maze. Pick up the **Sapphire** sitting in plain sight on the table.
- **Treasure: Ruby**: Go to the first tower of the Castle Battlements. Drop down two floors and open the small box in the corner to find a **Ruby**.
- **Treasure: Golden Hourglass**: Cross to the building opposite the first tower. Pull the lever to open the lower gate, drop down, and open the chest to collect the **Golden Hourglass**.
- **Treasure: Yellow Diamond**: Drop down from the second tower. Open the chest outside the collection room to retrieve the **Yellow Diamond**.

---

## Chapter 5 Walkthrough

### Objective: Go After Luis

#### The Battlements
- **Treasure: Pearl Bangle**: At the start of the chapter, turn around, walk outside, and climb the ladder on the left. Open the chest on the platform to collect the **Pearl Bangle**.
- **Treasure: Elegant Crown**: Go to the bottom level of the next tower. Open the chest behind the yellow switch lever to collect the **Elegant Crown**.

---

### Objective: Go Through the Waterway

#### Waterway Navigation
- **Treasure: Velvet Blue**: Enter the waterway. Turn around and walk to the top of the broken stone stairs to find a **Velvet Blue**.
- **Treasure: Ruby**: Shoot down the silver container hanging from the ceiling in the middle of the waterway channel to collect a **Ruby**.
- **Merchant Request: Lord of the Waterway**: Grab the request letter on the wall outside the Merchant room.
- **Treasure: Gold Bar (L)**: In the valve room, use a **Small Key** to open the locked drawer and collect the **Gold Bar (L)**.
- **Treasure: Mirror with Pearls and Rubies**: Drain the water. Follow the path past the valve room and open the chest around the corner to collect the **Mirror**.
- **Treasure: Alexandrite**: Drop down into the drained channel. Walk to the far north and climb the ladder to open the chest, collecting an **Alexandrite**.
- **Treasure: Yellow Diamond**: Shoot down the silver container hanging from the left wall of the drained waterway to collect the **Yellow Diamond**.
- **Lord of the Waterway**: Walk through the tunnel past the yellow diamond. Crouch under the fungus growth to enter the flooded passage. Shoot and collect the large mutated fish, then sell it to the Merchant to complete the request.

---

### Objective: Go After Krauser

#### Cavern Path
- **Merchant Request: Bug Buster**: Pick up the request letter from the crate next to the Merchant before entering the caverns.
- **Novistador 1**: Pull the lever to start the moving stone pillars. Look at the ceiling directly past the first pillar to shoot the Novistador.
- **Treasure: Depraved Idol**: Pull the lever and enter the northern passage. Open the chest to collect the **Depraved Idol**.
- **Treasure: Emerald**: Shoot down the silver cage hanging from the ceiling between the two moving stone pillars to collect the **Emerald**.
- **Novistador 2**: Climb the ladder to the cavern exit. Turn around and look up at the ceiling to shoot the Novistador.
- **Novistador 3**: Walk outside the cave. Look up at the corner of the building on the left to shoot the third Novistador.
- **Treasure: Brass Pocket Watch**: Enter the docks. Shoot down the silver container hanging from the scaffold on the right to collect the **Brass Pocket Watch**.

---

## Chapter 6 Walkthrough

### Objective: Head for the Comms Facility

#### Storeroom and Construction Yard
- **Treasure: Crystal Ore**: Enter the Amber Storeroom. Open the chest in the northeast corner to collect the **Crystal Ore**.
- **Treasure: Alexandrite**: Go to the construction yard elevator. Look up at the ceiling and shoot down the hanging cage to collect an **Alexandrite**.
- **Treasure: Red Beryl**: Shoot down the silver cage hanging from the yellow crane structure in the middle of the construction yard to collect a **Red Beryl**.
- **Merchant Request: Beautiful Beetles**: Grab the request letter to the left of the Merchant in the Facility 1 Loft.

---

### Objective: Find a Way Out of Facility 1

#### Lab Retrieval
- **Ornate Beetle 1**: Go to the Incubation Lab. Locate the single Regenerador standing inside the glass tank. Shoot the tank to release it, destroy all its parasites to kill it, and collect **Ornate Beetle 1**.
- **Treasure: Velvet Blue**: Enter the Dissection room. Open the glass cabinet on the northern wall to find a **Velvet Blue**.
- **Ornate Beetle 2**: In the right room of the Dissection area, open the container in the back-left corner to collect **Ornate Beetle 2**. Sell both beetles to the Merchant to complete the request.
- **Treasure: Golden Lynx**: Turn off the power and squeeze through the gap into the kitchen. Open the chest in the small alcove on the right to collect the **Golden Lynx**.
- **Treasure: Gold Ingot**: Go to the end of the Utilities area. Enter the side room on the right and use a **Small Key** on the locked drawer to collect the **Gold Ingot**.

---

### Objective: Head for the Comms Facility (Continued)

#### Defensive Line
- **Merchant Request: Bug Buster 2**: Grab the request letter from the wall next to the laser turrets.
- **Novistador 1**: Jump down into the defensive line. Take cover behind the first barrier, look left, and shoot the Novistador on the rock wall.
- **Treasure: Gold Bar (L)**: Defeat the laser turrets. Open the green dumpster in the middle of the area to collect the **Gold Bar (L)**.
- **Novistador 2**: Climb the scaffolding behind the right turret bunker. Look at the high watchtower on the cliff using a scoped weapon to shoot the Novistador.
- **Novistador 3**: Go behind the left turret bunker. Look at the far wall near the last turret to shoot the third Novistador.

---

### Objective: Connect the Device

#### Comms Facility
- **Treasure: Sapphire**: Enter the facility. Look up at the ceiling to the left of the green corridor and shoot down the container to collect a **Sapphire**.
- **Treasure: Ornate Necklace**: Escape the giant creature. Enter the monitoring room on the left and open the silver case to collect the **Ornate Necklace**.

---

## Chapter 7 Walkthrough

### Objective: Take the Amber Back from Saddler

#### Final Ascent
Ride the elevator to the sanctuary path.
- **Treasure: Velvet Blue**: Look up at the elevator shaft ceiling and shoot down the hanging cage to collect a **Velvet Blue**.
- **Treasure: Staff of Royalty**: Walk down the stairs from the elevator. Open the chest on the left side of the path to collect the **Staff of Royalty**.
- **Treasure: Justitia Statue**: Ride the second elevator down. Open the chest on the right wall just before the Merchant room to collect the **Justitia Statue**.
- **Treasure: Ruby**: Walk through the next tunnel. Eliminate the two Novistador bugs that ambush you. Shoot down the hanging silver container from the ceiling to collect the final treasure, a **Ruby**.
- **Ending**: Follow the path forward to complete Chapter 7 and the Separate Ways campaign.
