# Silent Hill 2 Remake: Complete Walkthrough

**Author's Note:** This walkthrough is based on the **Standard** puzzle difficulty. For light or hard puzzle solutions, or for a comprehensive list of all collectibles and easter eggs, please refer to the dedicated guides. 

## Controls
The default controls can be customized at any point throughout the game.

**PlayStation 5 / Xbox / PC**
*   **Left Analog / Left Thumbstick:** Movement
*   **Right Analog / Right Thumbstick:** Move camera
*   **D-Pad Up:** Map
*   **D-Pad Right:** Weapon Change: Rifle
*   **D-Pad Down:** Weapon Change: Shotgun
*   **D-Pad Left:** Weapon Change: Handgun
*   **X / A:** Interact / Extended Movement / Flip (Investigation) / Struggling
*   **Square / X:** Reload
*   **Triangle / Y:** Quick Heal (Tap) / Syringe Heal (Hold) / Read (Investigation)
*   **Circle / B:** Back (In gameplay) / Dodge / Skip
*   **L1 / LB:** Previous Item (Investigation) / Sprint
*   **L2 / LT:** Row Left / Zoom (Investigation) / Ranged Aim
*   **R1 / RB:** Next Item (Investigation) / Quick Turn
*   **R2 / RT:** Row Right / Shoot / Melee Attack
*   **R3 / Click Right Thumbstick:** Flashlight
*   **Touchpad / Windows:** Inventory
*   **Options / Menu:** Pause

## Road to Silent Hill
After the opening cutscene and in control of **James**, look to the left toward the car. Walk to the driver's seat, as we should see a **Map of South Vale** on the driver's seat. 

Take the map, as we'll open it up using **D-Pad Up**. Throughout the game, we can take a look at the map when needed to know where we need to go. From here, we need to take the path toward the graveyard. Take the path and go until we see a sign that reads **Toluca Lake**. We'll follow the path until we reach a well. Inside the well is a **red square**, and you will be able to use that to save progress in the game. 

Once we've taken a look at that, we'll want to continue on the path until we reach the gates to the graveyard. Enter the graveyard as a cutscene will trigger. When in control of **James** again, and if we look at the map, we'll need to head to the wall to the left. There'll be a gate that we can use to advance further to **Silent Hill**. Follow the path until we get to **Silent Hill Ranch** according to the map. When reaching an old truck, we'll enter the ranch area. Making our way further, we'll have a **sawmill** that we'll bypass. Not far after that, we'll come to a building to our left that's an **auto parts** place. Enter through the gate door. There should be a note not far from the door.

> *Went to town to get the window fixed. Spare key's in the drawer.*

After reading the note, head to the left of where we entered and around the corner. There should be a window we can enter through by pressing **X/A**. Inside the building, head to the desk that's in front of us on the other side. The key will be in the drawer on the right near the lamp. Interact with the drawer, then grab the **Auto Parts Key**. Head to the door to the right, unlock it, and head back outside. Go through the door gate and to the red gate. Use the key that we just acquired to unlock the door in the red gate. On the other side, until we reach a tunnel that's blocked off and a path to the right. 

If we go to the tunnel before making a turn to the right, interact with it, as we'll get what's called a **Glimpse of the Past**. These will also be covered in the Glimpse of the Past section. Take the path to the right until we reach another tunnel-like area. Go under and through the gate with a **"Restricted Area"** sign. We'll go until we reach what looks like a path that veers off to the left. Stay on the path that heads to the right. Stay on the path until we reach **Sanders St.** according to the map and the game takes over briefly.

## East South Vale
When in control, we'll be in **East South Vale** and will have the map for the area. Taking a look at the map, we'll head straight from **Wiltse Rd.**, as there's a flower shop across the street. Inside the flower shop, head to the desk, as there's a note there.

> **Ms. Cassandra**
> *a customer came in today. Ordered a mixed bouquet, red and white. I tried to explain it's bad luck, but he couldn't decide on just one. He seemed a bit off, looked at me like I wasn't even there.*
> *Then he just left, said he'd be back to pick'em up later. I just wanted to let you know, since I might be gone by then.*

After reading the message, we'll exit the flower shop via the way we came. Upon exiting, head left toward **Lindsey St.**, as we'll see some weird substance on the road. Get close and examine it. As we look at it, we'll take a right. Making our way on **Lindsey St.**, we'll get halfway to **Katz St.** when we'll see more of that substance again on the road. We'll be able to take a look at it like before. Keep going until a brief cutscene triggers. As soon as we gain control again, we'll head until we see more of that substance to the left of the road near a sidewalk. 

Follow it toward the space between the two buildings. On the other side, we should be in the backyard area of the building to the right. There should be a fence to the right with an opening near a tree trunk. Reaching the opening, look to the slight right. There should be another fence as there should be a gate. Should be easy to spot as it's the only portion that has that weird substance on it. Upon going through it, we'll get another glimpse of it before it takes off. We'll need to head to where it was. As we do, we'll go until we reach a gate fence with an opened doorway to go through. After we get to the next area, we should see a garage with a hole in the door. Enter it by using **X/A** to crawl through the opening. 

Inside the garage area, there should be a small opening on the far end on the right. Walk over and interact to squeeze through. Inside we should see a **red square** like we did at the well, as we can save progress if needed. In the room we're currently in, head to the right toward the door. On the other side, we should reach a kitchen. Head to the counter near the window. Interact with the top drawer near the window to reveal a **health drink**. 

As we take it, it can be used by pressing **Triangle/Y** if we're using the default controls. Keep in mind that it can be used even at full health, so be sure you don't accidentally take it when we don't need to. Upon getting the health drink, head out through the door to the left of the counter. In the hallway, take a left until we reach the last door on the right. Enter the room as we should hear a noise coming from inside it. To the left of the door should be a chair that has a **Radio**, thus where the sound is coming from. Interact with it as the game will take over, and eventually, we'll have our first encounter with an enemy known as **Lying Figure**.

When in control again, we'll have a weapon of sorts to take it out with a **Wooden Plank**. We'll want to do what we can to dodge its attacks, as it can spray a liquid at you. Timing will be needed to know when it'll do that and dodge by pressing **Circle/B**. When we dodge, we can attack by pressing **R2/RT**. Just dodge and attack when we won't get sprayed by it. As soon as it's down for the count, the radio we found will go silent. Once it's down, head through the way that it came. 

In the next room, there should be a door to the left with a clear near it. Go through the door and you should see a hole in the wall. It should be similar to that when we entered the garage. Go through the hole into the next room. On the desk to the right should be a box. Interact with it to open it to find a **syringe**. This will help you recover your health more compared to the health drink, but these will be scarce to find. Go toward the nearby window and use the **wooden plank** to smash it. As we do that, go through the window back outside. The game will take over briefly, and when back in control, follow the path until we reach an opened gate in the fence. Use it to get out to the road. 

Head to the vehicle on the left that looks like a Jeep Cherokee. We should see something in the back of it. Use the Wooden Plank to smash the back window and grab the **health drink** inside. When we grab that, we'll want to head toward where **Martin St.** and **Katz St.** intersect. Now, head in the direction we started down the road until we reach a dead body. Take a look at the dead body and inspect the marked areas. The first one that we inspect should be a piece of a map. The other should be a memo.

> **Front**
> *I'll write down what I've learned so far.*
> *Maybe I'll help you somehow.*

> **Front**

When we've read that, go toward the camper, but as we do, there'll likely be another Lying Figure that'll be coming toward us. Take it out while avoiding taking any damage from it as much as possible. Taking it out, if we take a look at the camper, there should be another memo on the ground on the right side facing it.

> *They can be killed just make sure they stay down*

Now, go inside the camper, as there'll be a memo on the table to the right between the small couches.

> *This place all it's ever brought me is pain and misery*

We can leave the camper after that, but before we do, look to the right of the door as we're about to leave. There should be a **health drink** on the counter to the right. Take it before leaving, as we'll now head to **Saul St. Apartments**. The entrance to the apartment will have 16 to the right of the door. Upon entering, the radio may go off as we'll have a Creeper that'll be crawling around the other side of the current hallway. Likely near the window at the end. Make your way past the front desk on the left, as we'll find a map on a bulletin board. As we look at it, we'll need to go to Room #1, which will be to the left of our current position. Reaching the door, the door will be off the hinges, but there'll be what looks like a clothes closet. Get near it and push it until we aren't able to anymore. Inside the room, go to the right through the open doorway, as we'll be in the front desk area for the apartments. On the desk should be a memo.

Taking the key, as we'll see it's for the apartment mentioned in the note (**Apt 5 Key**). Exit the front desk area by the door near the desk. Unlock it and back into the main hallway. Back in the main hallway, head to the right and then to the hallway opposite of where the map is. Bypass the stairs for now until we reach the end of the area. There'll be an apartment to the right, which should be **Apt. 3**. Go to the door opposite it and enter the room. We'll be in a pharmacy that's next door to the apartments. Go in Room 217 and on the bed is a suitcase with a box of ammo. Step onto the balcony, as we should see a glass roof.

In the secret room, head to the Vanity Mirror and place the new bulb in there. When the new light is placed, we'll get a code on the Vanity Mirror.

**Explore Employee Section / Locate Snow White Figurine**
Interact with it and put all of our items in each of the compartments. We'll need to do this, or we can't use the elevator. Once all items are in there, head to the elevator and interact with the buttons to the right. We'll only be able to choose one floor. When reaching the floor, we'll exit out, and we'll be in an area where we'll have **Mandarins** in the area. We aren't going to have any way to fight back with them while here. Upon exiting the elevator, head right for the window and to the other side. To the right side of the room is a door with a dartboard on it. To the left of it is a map we can get of the area.
To the right of the door with the dartboard is a safe, but we cannot unlock it at this time. The first area we want to attempt to go is the **Cafeteria** but we'll have to make sure we'll be in the clear to do so. And by in the clear, not be in the line of sight of any of the Mandarins. Exit the window to the left of the refrigerator when we're clear to do so. Before we can exit through there, go to the desk with the coffee pot, as there'll be a **health drink** in the top right drawer. Exit through the window and to the left toward the cafeteria. The safe bet is to go into the **Pantry** to reduce being attacked. Inside the Pantry head to the door to the left. Entering the **Freezer Room**, we should have a freezer to the left upon entry. Go to the front of it for an item.

Behind it, we should reach an item of interest on the counter to the right.
This one is the **Green Gemstone**. Once we grab that one, head back to the Freezer Room and to the Pantry. Head back to the **Break Room** and to catch a breather. When in the clear again, exit and head toward the **Manager's Office**. Inside the office, there's a set of lockers, and there's a box of **shotgun shells** to the right of them on a small cabinet. Head to the **TV Room** from the Manager's Office. There's a cabinet we'll have to go to the left and push it. Go through the hole in the wall and to the desk to the left.
Interact with the box and get the **Red Gemstone**. Now, head back to the Manager's Office and to the desk. On the desk should be a puzzle. Before interacting with the desk take a look at a nearby painting.
Head to the desk and interact with a puzzle. We'll need to place the gemstones we found in the correct slots. The gemstones go in the following order:
- **Top Left:** Green
We should get a piece of paper with a code of **7414** on it. Head back to the Break Room and to the safe.

We should pick up the **Pump Valve**. Once we pick it up, exit out when in the clear and to the **Liquor Storage**. Inside of the Liquor Storage, take a left and squeeze through the hole in the wall. There'll be a door to the **Boiler Room** but we'll use the Pump Valve to close out the steam coming out. Once we do that, enter the Boiler Room. Inside the Boiler Room there's a box with a **syringe** in it. Taking a look at the pump, there's an item attached to it.
Interacting with it as if it's the **Venus Tears Bar Key**. Exit the Boiler Room and grab the **Pump Valve**. Head to the door to the left upon exiting the Boiler Room and use the recently acquired key to unlock it. Inside, go to the right and use the Pump Valve again to shut off the steam coming down. On the other side, go toward the jukebox, and there should be an item on the floor near it.
It'll be **The Little Mermaid Figurine**. Go to the right of where we found it, as there's a save point and a set of double doors. Exit through the doors, as we'll be in the main portion of the hotel again. Head up the stairs, and
- **Center Door:** Little Mermaid
- **Right Door:** Cinderella

- **Left Disc:** Move right once
- **Top Disc:** Move left once
- **Right Disc (Outer):** Move right once
- **Right Disc (Inner):** Move left three times

## Lakeview Hotel (Otherworld)

**Finding Mary**
When back in control, we'll be in the hotel but in the **Otherworld** version of it. The first thing we'll have to do is exit the room that we're in and go into the hallway. Once in the hallway, head to **Room 314**. Inside Room 314, go around the corner and through the wall that leads to **Room 315**. In the nightstand to the right of the bed in **313**, has a box of ammo to get. Once we get that, go into the bathroom and squeeze through the gap in the wall. If we go toward **Room 311**, we can enter and use it to get to **Room 310**. From 310, use it to get to **Room 309**. Inside of 309 there'll be a nightstand with a box of **shotgun shells**.
Unlock the door for **309**, exit into the hallway, and take a left toward **Room 302**. Eventually, we'll cross a portion of the floor that's weak when closer to Room 302.

Enter **Room 304** and through the wall near **Room 306**. Go through the gap to Room 306. There's a box on the bed that has a **syringe** in it to take. Exit Room 306 and head between 301 and 302. Go around behind 302, as we should see a cart, as well as a crawl space. Use the crawl space to enter. We'll go to where we'll be in **Room 303**, and there's an end table near the blocked door. Inside of it is a box of ammo. To the right of that is a message on the wall.

> *There is no healing*
> *of they bruise;*
> *they wound is grievous*

Exit back out through the crawl space. Grab the cart and move it to the weak portion of the floor. With the newly formed hole, jump when prompted. We'll be on the **2F** of the hotel. Head forward until we have a gap we'll have to squeeze through. Enter **Room 207** and go to the nightstand to the right of the bed. Inside is a **health drink**. Head out of the room, but there's a book with a brief passage we can read.

> *Wash yourselves, make yourselves clean;*
> *Remove the evil of your deeds from my sight*

Exit Room 207 and go to the **Restroom**. Go through the wall into the **Reading Room**. Head around the sofa as we'll head past it toward the double doors into the hallway. We'll then go into the **Cloakroom** and through the Hall to the **Nurse's Station**. To get to the Nurse's Station, we'll see a shelf below a window.
Climb onto the shelf and through the window. Go around to the desk, as there's a tape recorder we can interact with that has a message on it. Now, exit through the gap in the wall opposite the desk with the recorder. Before going further, there's a desk between **Room 213** and **Room 214** that has a box of ammo in it. Take it and then head toward the stairs. Go until we reach the elevator to the right. Use it to head to the **Basement**. When we reach the bottom, interact with the elevator doors to open them. Head to the right, as we should see a set of open double doors to enter.

Inside, go to the right and around to the other side of the counter. There'll be a save point to use if needed. Go through the door near the save point. In the kitchen, go left through the door until we reach a hallway. There should be a gap in the wall to the left.

Inside should be the **Liquor Storage** area. Going between the first two shelves is a box of **rifle rounds** we can take. At the end, take a right as there'll be a box of **shotgun shells** on the shelf to the left before reaching the door. Although we aren't able to go through the door, so we'll need to use the desk to the right to go through the gap above it. On the other side we'll have a door with a bright light on the other side. Walk over to the door and interact with it for a cutscene. When the cutscene is done, we'll use the stairs to head up to the **1F Employee Section**. Head outside through the door at the top of the stairs.

Around the corner to the left is a **Lying Figure**, we can bypass it by going to the right. There isn't much of a path except going toward the **Manager's Office**. If going around the corner near the Manger's Office there'll be a **Bubble Head**. Just go inside of the office. There's a note on the desk.
> *312 to be cleared out*

To the left of the desk, we'll go toward the **TV Room**, but watch out for the **Spider Mannequin**. Head to the door to the left of it. Follow the path until we reach a gap that we have to squeeze through. Go to the right and through the door. We should see nine save points on the wall. That is the last save point of the entire game. This would be the best time to save for what's coming up. Go to the right until we reach a door to interact with, and a cutscene will trigger.

**Boss Information**
**Boss:** Pyramid Head Duel
**HP:** ??
Similar to the fight at Bluecreek Apartments, but this time it'll be double the trouble. During the fight, we'll need to keep our distance from them. Remember to aim anywhere except for the head area. You'll need to do this from a distance at their lower part or from behind. If either of themgetst closeenough,h we'll need to dodge when they're about to attack. This will be a repeat process during this fight. The fight will conclude when a cutscene triggers. When the fight concludes, we'll need to inspect both of them with one of their hands. Both will have an egg. One will have a **rust-colored egg** and the other has a **Scarlett Egg**. We'll need to insert them at the doors shown below:

Depending on which door you enter, it may determine which ending you get and what you have done up to this point. Inside the hallway we'll have a monologue from **Mary**. Listening to it to the end or going through the end before it finishes will also count which ending we may get. On the other side of the door, head to the right, as we should find a box of ammo along with a **syringe**. To the left of those, you will find a strange photo.
This one is titled **SHAPE FORCES THE MIND**. Turn around and go to the other side of where we came out. Be sure to pick up the boxes of **shotgun shells**, **rifle rounds**, and **handgun** ammo. After collecting those, head up the stairs until we reach a ladder we'll climb. At the top, we'll see someone looking out the window. Walk over until a cutscene triggers.

**Boss Information**
**Boss:** Mary
**HP:** ??

**Phase One**
The first phase we'll need to watch for the moths that'll come at us. Watch for when they'll come near us and dodge them. Also, watch for when she'll go underground and be careful of where she'll come up at. We don't want her to get too close, or she'll use a lash-like attack against us. Similar to the **Pyramid Head**, we'll need to dodge if she gets too close, and we'll need to plan out an attack against her. Be sure to have a good amount of distance to take a shot against her. We'll need to stick to this plan until we do enough damage to where the game will take over.

**Phase Two**
The next portion of the fight will be in a corridor-like area and will be like a maze. This will make the fight a little bit trickier. Especially trying to dodge her attacks. Her attacks will include doing a whip-like attack, so don't be too close if possible. Although it'll be easy to know where she is due to the sparks above us. Be sure to be one step ahead for when she drops down for any attacks. Using a weapon like the **Rifle** will help in this portion of the fight when she drops down and keep our distance. When enough damage has been dealt, we'll prepare for the third and final stage.

**Phase Three**
We'll be back in an open area like with the first phase of the fight. This will be the most challenging portion of the fight, as she'll have the ability to go where she pleases without having to disappear. At least for a while. She'll disappear and reappear like the first stage. For this portion, we will require knowing how to keep our distance and planning our attacks like during the **Pyramid Head Duel** fight. When she disappears, it'll be good enough to strategize when she reappears. As soon as she does, we'll need to start going for any attacks against her. As in the previous stages, we'll know when we do enough damage as the game will take over. When in control again, we'll walk over to her and deliver the final shot. Upon doing that, the game will be beat, and we'll be able to enjoy the ending that we'll get.

## Endings

### Classic

These are the endings that don't require **New Game+** to achieve.

#### Leave
**Requirements**
- Always keep James' health above danger/critical
- Examine Mary's photo and letter at a minimum of two times
- Listen to James and Mary's conversation
- Minimize fights against enemies
- Blame **Snake** at the end of the Coin Cabinet puzzle
- Use **Rotten Apple** for mirror puzzle at Lakeview Hotel
- Do NOT check on Maria
- Do NOT inspect Angela's knife

#### Maria
**Requirements**
- Do NOT inspect Mary's letter, photo, or Angela's knife
- Do NOT allow Maria to take damage in the following areas:
    - West South Vale chase sequence
    - Hospital Basement
    - Mannequin ambush at Jack's Inn
- Spend at least a minimum of 10 minutes with Maria
- Inspect every interaction at **Heaven's Night** before leaving
- Take Maria with you while exploring **Nathan Ave**
- Visit Maria while she's resting at **Brookhaven Hospital**
- Visit Maria in the cell a minimum of two times at **Labyrinth**
- Visit Maria after clearing the Labyrinth before leaving
- Use **Scarlett Egg** at the door after the Pyramid Head Duel fight
- Do NOT listen to the entire conversation with Mary during the long hallway at the end

#### In Water
**Requirements**
- Inspect Angela's knife a minimum of two times
- Do NOT inspect Mary's letter or photo
- Spend a minimum of 10 minutes in critical health
- Kill a minimum of 350 enemies
- Stomp on an enemy that is already dead a minimum of 30 times
- Inspect graffiti on the wall above the jukebox in **Neely's Bar** in East South Vale (Otherworld)
- Minimize the time spent with Maria
    - Do NOT bring her to explore Nathan Ave in West South Vale
    - Do NOT protect her against any enemy attack
- Use **Rotten Apple** for the mirror puzzle at Lakeview Hotel
- Kill the enemies in Lakeview Hotel (Otherworld) before they die on their own
- Enter the door with the **Rust-colored Egg** after the Pyramid Head Duel fight
- Listen to the entire conversation in the hallway after the Pyramid Head Duel fight
