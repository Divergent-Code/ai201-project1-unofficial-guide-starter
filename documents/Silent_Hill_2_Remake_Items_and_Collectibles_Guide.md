# Silent Hill 2 Remake: Items and Collectibles Guide

This guide covers all weapons, personal items, equipment, medical supplies, and key items found throughout Silent Hill 2 Remake.

---

## Weapons

### Melee Weapons

| Weapon | Location | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Wooden Plank** | East South Vale - Apartment on Martin Street (first encounter with Lying Figure) | Wooden Plank studded with nails. An improvised weapon. (Range: Low, Strength: Low) |
| **Steel Pipe** | West South Vale - Katz St. (during cutscene) | A 3-foot long steel pipe. Cold and smooth to the touch. (Range: Low-Medium, Strength: Medium) |
| **Chainsaw** | Near Silent Hill Ranch (New Game+ only) | Gas-powered chainsaw. Extremely high attack power. (Range: Medium, Strength: High) |

### Firearms

| Weapon | Location | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Handgun** | Wood Side Apartment - Apartment 213 (In Shopping Cart) | A light, semi-automatic pistol. Holds 10 rounds per magazine. Not much stopping power but easy to use. (Range: Medium, Strength: Low) |
| **Shotgun** | Brookhaven Hospital - Women's Locker Room (2F) | A pump-action shotgun with a wide blast radius. The magazine holds 6 shells. Not very handy but powerful. (Range: Low, Strength: Medium-High) |
| **Hunting Rifle** | Toluca Prison - Armory (Requires Armory Key near Wellness Checkpoint) | A long range, high-caliber hunting rifle. Holds 4 rounds per clip. Takes a long time to reload, but each shot is very powerful. (Range: High, Strength: High) |
