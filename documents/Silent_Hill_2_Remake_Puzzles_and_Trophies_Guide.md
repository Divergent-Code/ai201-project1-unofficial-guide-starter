# Silent Hill 2 Remake: Puzzles, Trophies, and Achievements Guide

This guide covers the solutions for all major puzzles across Light, Standard, and Hard difficulties, as well as a comprehensive list of Trophies and Achievements.

---

## Puzzle Solutions

### Grand Market Keypad
* **Location:** East South Vale
* **Light:** 4442
* **Standard:** 4444
* **Hard:** 4446

### Jukebox Puzzle
* **Location:** Neely's Bar
* **Light:** 
  * Riddle 1: Man - blank - Woman - blank - Flower
  * Riddle 2: Sword - blank - Woman - blank - Snake
  * Riddle 3: Snake - blank - Grave - blank - Man
* **Standard / Hard:**
  * Riddle 1: Man - Empty - Empty - Grave - Snake
  * Riddle 2: Grave - Sword - Flower - Empty - Empty
  * Riddle 3: Empty - Man - Woman - Empty - Snake

### Apartment 206 Safe
* **Location:** Wood Side Apartments
* **Light:** Right 15, Left 11, Right 13
* **Standard / Hard:** Right 13, Left 7, Right 11

### Clock Puzzle
* **Location:** Bluecreek Apartments, Room 212
* **All Difficulties:** Hour Hand 9, Minute Hand 2, Second Hand 3

### Moth Puzzle (Room 202 Safe)
* **Location:** Bluecreek Apartments
* **Light:** 582
* **Standard:** 373
* **Hard:** 522

### Motel Safe Code
* **Location:** Jack's Inn, Room 108
* **Light / Standard:** 0451
* **Hard:** 0533

### Pete's Bowl-O-Rama Safe
* **Location:** West South Vale (Otherworld)
* **All Difficulties:** 1887

### Hospital Padlock Code
* **Location:** Brookhaven Hospital, 1F Reception
* **Light:** 287
* **Standard:** 724
* **Hard:** 152

### X-Ray Puzzle (Hospital Door Code)
* **Location:** Brookhaven Hospital, 3F X-Ray Room
* **All Difficulties:** Right 4, Left 37, Right 12

### Bracelet Puzzle
* **Location:** Brookhaven Hospital, Director's Office
* **All Difficulties:** Bloodstained (Top), Marked (Middle), Filthy (Bottom). Code is **92-45-71**.

### Hospital Safe Code (Director's Books)
* **Location:** Brookhaven Hospital, Director's Office
* **All Difficulties:** Rearrange the books so the spine symbols form a Firebreathing Lion.

### Trick or Treat Elevator / Decorative Box
* **Location:** Brookhaven Hospital (Otherworld)
* **All Difficulties:** Question 1: 2 (67) | Question 2: 3 (Wiltse Road) | Question 3: 1 (Miriam)

### Chained Box
* **Location:** Brookhaven Hospital (Otherworld), 2F
* **All Difficulties:** Combination code is **1622**.

### Bug Room Keypad
* **Location:** Toluca Prison
* **All Difficulties:** 9659

### Weights and Scale
* **Location:** Toluca Prison, Yard
* **All Difficulties:** 
  * Headless Serpent: Left scale (Initial, Heaviest)
  * Hornless Ox: Left scale (Initial, Heaviest), Right scale (Medium)
  * Eyeless Boar: Left scale (Initial, Heaviest), Right scale (Medium, Heavy)
  * Wingless Dove: Left scale (Initial, Light, Heavy), Right scale (Medium)
  * Execution Lever: Left scale (Lightest, Light, Heavy), Right scale (Initial, Medium, Heaviest)

### Witness Room Generator
* **Location:** Toluca Prison
* **Light:** 4 3 5 1 2
* **Standard:** 4 6 5 2 3 1
* **Hard:** 4 3 5 1 2 6 7

### Hangman Puzzle
* **Location:** Toluca Prison, Yard
* **All Difficulties:** The correct noose is randomized per playthrough based on the generated poems.

### Rotating Cube
* **Location:** Labyrinth Central
* **All Difficulties:** 
  * Rotten Area: Left x2, Down x1
  * Desolate Area: Left x2, Up x1
  * Ruined Area: Left x2, Down x2

### Music Box Puzzle
* **Location:** Lakeview Hotel, Lobby
* **Light / Standard:** Left Disc (Right 1), Top Disc (Left 1), Right Disc Outer (Right 1), Right Disc Inner (Left 3). Code: **7-5-1**.
* **Hard:** Turn Left key 7 times, Middle key 2 times, Right key 1 time. Code: **7-2-1**.
