# Silent Hill 2 Remake: Puzzles, Trophies, and Achievements Guide

This guide covers the solutions for all major puzzles across Light, Standard, and Hard difficulties, as well as a comprehensive list of Trophies and Achievements.

---

## Puzzle Solutions

### Grand Market Keypad
* **Location:** East South Vale
* **Light:** 4442
* **Standard:** 4444
* **Hard:** 4446

### Jukebox Puzzle
* **Location:** Neely's Bar
* **Steps (all difficulties):** Collect Jukebox Button (Saul St. Apartments), Broken Vinyl Record halves (Neely's Bar jukebox + Groovy Music lounge), Vinyl Glue (Groovy Music lounge), Neely's Bar Coin (Cafe Texan register). Combine the two record halves with the glue. Insert button in slot 2, interact with **C** and **2**, then insert the coin.

### Coin Cabinet
* **Location:** Wood Side Apartments - Lounge
* **Light:**
  * Riddle 1: Man → blank → Woman → blank → Flower
  * Riddle 2: Sword → blank → Woman → blank → Snake
  * Riddle 3: Snake → blank → Grave → blank → Man
  * Final: Any coin in the top slot to their original side.
* **Standard:**
  * Riddle 1: Man → blank → blank → Grave → Snake
  * Riddle 2: blank → Sword → blank → Woman → Flower
  * Riddle 3: blank → blank → Man → Grave → Snake
  * Final: Any coin in the top slot to their original side.
* **Hard:**
  * Riddle 1: Man → Empty → Empty → Grave → Snake
  * Riddle 2: Grave → Sword → Flower → Empty → Empty
  * Riddle 3: Empty → Man → Woman → Empty → Snake
  * Final: Any coin in the top slot to their original side if needed.

### Apartment 206 Safe
* **Location:** Wood Side Apartments
* **Light:** Right 15, Left 11, Right 13
* **Standard / Hard:** Right 13, Left 7, Right 11

### Clock Puzzle
* **Location:** Bluecreek Apartments, Room 212
* **All Difficulties:** Hour Hand 9, Minute Hand 2, Second Hand 3

### Moth Puzzle (Room 202 Safe)
* **Location:** Bluecreek Apartments - Apartment 202
* **Light:** Count moths of each type (Eye: 5, Moon Crescent: 8, Skull: 2). Code: **582**.
* **Standard:** Skulls(2) + Circles(5) = 7; Moon − Skull = 3; Circle − Skull = 3. Code: **373**.
* **Hard:** Moon + Skull − Eye = 5; Eye × Skull − Moon = 2; (Skull + Moon) ÷ Eye = 2. Code: **522**.

### Motel Safe Code
* **Location:** Jack's Inn, Room 108
* **Light / Standard:** 0451
* **Hard:** 0533

### Pete's Bowl-O-Rama Safe
* **Location:** West South Vale (Otherworld)
* **All Difficulties:** 1887

### Hospital Padlock Code
* **Location:** Brookhaven Hospital, 1F Reception
* **Light:** 287
* **Standard:** 724
* **Hard:** 152

### X-Ray Puzzle (Hospital Door Code)
* **Location:** Brookhaven Hospital, 3F X-Ray Room
* **All Difficulties:** Right 4, Left 37, Right 12

### Bracelet Puzzle
* **Location:** Brookhaven Hospital, Director's Office
* **All Difficulties:** Bloodstained (Top), Marked (Middle), Filthy (Bottom). Code is **92-45-71**.

### Nurse's Station Keypad
* **Location:** Brookhaven Hospital - 2F Nurse's Station
* **Light / Standard:** **3578**
* **Hard:** **7456**

### Hospital Safe Code (Director's Books)
* **Location:** Brookhaven Hospital, Director's Office
* **All Difficulties:** Rearrange the books so the spine symbols form a Firebreathing Lion.

### Trick or Treat Elevator / Decorative Box
* **Location:** Brookhaven Hospital (Otherworld)
* **All Difficulties:** Question 1: 2 (67) | Question 2: 3 (Wiltse Road) | Question 3: 1 (Miriam)

### Chained Box
* **Location:** Brookhaven Hospital (Otherworld), 2F near Nurse's Station
* **Part 1:** Obtain the Lapis Eye Key.
* **Part 2 (Combination Code):** Go to Room M2 and read Calendar Note → Check sink for pills → Turn flashlight off and count flashes → Check hole right of sink for clock. Solution: 1 year (1) + 6 pills + 2 flashes + 2 (clock) = **1622**.
* **Part 3 (Keypad Code):** Laundry Room (break wall) → Cart to L3 → Director's Office Key → Ladder in Director's Office → Restroom near Room 17 → Activate generator → Activate machine in Treatment Stretcher room. Code: **Light/Hard: 9659 | Standard: 9695**.

### Bug Room Keypad
* **Location:** Toluca Prison
* **All Difficulties:** The code is **randomized** each playthrough, using digits 2, 3, and 9. Possible codes: 239, 293, 329, 392, 923, 932. Trial and error required.

### Weights and Scale
* **Location:** Toluca Prison, Yard
* **All Difficulties:** 
  * Headless Serpent: Left scale (Initial, Heaviest)
  * Hornless Ox: Left scale (Initial, Heaviest), Right scale (Medium)
  * Eyeless Boar: Left scale (Initial, Heaviest), Right scale (Medium, Heavy)
  * Wingless Dove: Left scale (Initial, Light, Heavy), Right scale (Medium)
  * Execution Lever: Left scale (Lightest, Light, Heavy), Right scale (Initial, Medium, Heaviest)

### Seesaw (Figurine Balance) Puzzle
* **Location:** Bluecreek Apartments - Room 210
* **All Difficulties:** Collect Pigeon Figurine (Apt. 210), Wooden Swan Head (Apt. 211), and Malformed Figurine Part (Apt. 209 bathroom). Combine the latter two to make the Swan Figurine, then use them on the seesaw.

### Witness Room Generator
* **Location:** Toluca Prison - Witness Room (Headless Serpent path)
* **Purpose:** Unlocks the Death Chamber to obtain the Medium Weight.
* **Light:** 4 → 3 → 5 → 1 → 2
* **Standard:** 4 → 6 → 5 → 2 → 3 → 1
* **Hard:** 4 → 3 → 5 → 1 → 2 → 6 → 7

### Hangman Puzzle
* **Location:** Toluca Prison, Yard
* **All Difficulties:** The correct noose is randomized per playthrough based on the generated poems.

### Rotating Cube
* **Location:** Labyrinth Central
* **All Difficulties:** 
  * Rotten Area: Left x2, Down x1
  * Desolate Area: Left x2, Up x1
  * Ruined Area: Left x2, Down x2

### Hotel Safe (Gemstone Puzzle)
* **Location:** Lakeview Hotel - 1F Employee Section; Break Room
* **All Difficulties:** Collect three gemstones from Kitchen (Green), Pantry (Blue), and Manager's Room (Red). Solve the gem arrangement puzzle in the Manager's Office to match the painting. The safe combination is **7414**.

### Music Box Puzzle
* **Location:** Lakeview Hotel, Lobby
* **Figurine locations:** Snow White → solve Mirror puzzle in Room 202; Little Mermaid → Basement/Venus Tears Bar; Cinderella → 3F Conference Room (Fireplace Key + Room 107 Key required).
* **Disc positions (all difficulties):** Left Disc → Right 1; Top Disc → Left 1; Right Disc (Outer) → Right 1; Right Disc (Inner) → Left 3. Press the square button in the lower right to trigger riddles.
* **Light:** Riddles reference "seven kindly souls" (7), "two" (2), "one behind" (1). Turn keys: **7-2-1**.
* **Standard:** Riddles reference "seven kindly souls" (7), "love of five" (5), "one behind" (1). Turn keys: **7-5-1**.
* **Hard:** Riddles reference "alone" (1) × 3 scenarios. Turn keys: **1-1-1**.

## Endings

### Classic Endings (no New Game+ required)

#### Leave
* Always keep James' health above critical/danger
* Examine Mary's Photo and Letter at least **2 times** each
* Listen to the James and Mary conversation
* Minimize enemy kills
* **Blame Snake** at the end of the Coin Cabinet puzzle
* Use the **Rotten Apple** for the mirror puzzle at Lakeview Hotel
* Do **NOT** check on Maria
* Do **NOT** inspect Angela's knife

#### Maria
* Do **NOT** inspect Mary's letter, photo, or Angela's knife
* Do **NOT** allow Maria to take damage in: West South Vale chase, Hospital Basement, or Mannequin ambush at Jack's Inn
* Spend at least **10 minutes** with Maria
* Inspect every interaction at Heaven's Night before leaving
* Take Maria while exploring Nathan Ave
* Visit Maria while resting at Brookhaven Hospital
* Visit Maria in her cell at least **2 times** in Labyrinth
* Visit Maria after clearing the Labyrinth before leaving
* Use the **Scarlet Egg** at the door after the Pyramid Head Duel
* Do **NOT** listen to the entire conversation with Mary in the long hallway at the end

#### In Water
* Inspect Angela's knife at least **2 times**
* Do **NOT** inspect Mary's letter or photo
* Spend at least **10 minutes** in critical health
* Kill at least **350 enemies**
* Stomp on already-dead enemies at least **30 times**
* Inspect the graffiti on the wall above the jukebox in Neely's Bar (East South Vale Otherworld)
* Minimize time with Maria; do **NOT** bring her to Nathan Ave and do **NOT** protect her
* Use the **Rotten Apple** for the mirror puzzle at Lakeview Hotel
* Kill enemies in Lakeview Hotel (Otherworld) before they die on their own
* Use the **Rust-Colored Egg** after the Pyramid Head Duel
* Listen to the **entire** Mary conversation in the hallway after the Pyramid Head Duel

### New Game+ Endings

#### Rebirth
* Collect **Crimson Ceremony** (Cemetery where you meet Angela)
* Collect **White Chrism** (Table at Baldwin Mansion in West South Vale)
* Collect **Obsidian Goblet** (Historical Society, alcove on the wall after Brookhaven Hospital)
* Collect **Lost Memories** (Cafe inside Lakeview Hotel - 1F, break a wall)

#### Dog
* Find **Broken Key Part 1** at Pet Center near Big Jay's
* Find **Broken Key Part 2** in West South Vale on Katz St., near a wooden gate with a dog bone on the door
* **Combine** both key parts
* Use the combined key at Lakeview Hotel - 3F Observation Room

#### UFO
* Obtain **Blue Gem** at the jewelry store near Big Jay's
* Investigate the Blue Gem at the following 4 locations:
  1. Saul Apartments Rooftop (while finding jukebox parts)
  2. Rosewater Park pier after meeting Maria
  3. The dock at Lakeview Gardens - South
  4. Lakeview Hotel - Room 312 (before watching the videotape)

#### Bliss
* Obtain the **Rusted Key** from Pete's Bowl-O-Rama safe (code: 1887) in New Game+
* Find the **Small Chest** at Brookhaven Hospital - Garden Gazebo
* Combine Rusted Key + Small Chest → **White Claudia**
* Reach Lakeview Hotel - Room 312, drink White Claudia, then watch the videotape

#### Stillness
* Obtain the **Chainsaw** after starting New Game+ (near Silent Hill Ranch)
* Find a green car after leaving Silent Hill Ranch and smash the window to get **Key of Sorrow**
* Watch the videotape in Lakeview Hotel - Room 312
* Go to Lakeview Hotel - 1F Manager's Office, use the Key of Sorrow to open the safe
* Collect **Toluca Postcard** and investigate it at least once
* Finish the game
