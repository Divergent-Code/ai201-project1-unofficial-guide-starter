# The Evil Within (2014) Complete Walkthrough and Guide (Chapters 1-15)

## Part 1: Introduction and Core Systems

### A Brief Foreword
The Evil Within is a third-person survival horror game developed by Tango Gameworks and directed by Shinji Mikami, the creator of Resident Evil. You play as Detective Sebastian Castellanos, who is called to a disturbance at Beacon Mental Hospital only to witness a mass slaughter perpetrated by a pale, hooded figure named Ruvik. Sebastian is knocked unconscious and awakens in a nightmarish, shifting world of fractured reality — a construct created by STEM, an experimental brain-linking technology that has trapped Sebastian and his colleagues inside a shared consciousness.

Survival requires careful resource management, stealth where possible, and strategic use of limited weapons and tools. The world is hostile and ammunition is scarce — every encounter demands a decision between fighting, sneaking, or running.

* **R2 / RT**: Fire Weapon
* **L1 / LB**: Sprint (hold)
* **R1 / RB**: Interact / Pick Up Items
* **Square / X**: Reload
* **X / A**: Dodge / Roll (while aiming) / Jump low obstacles
* **Circle / B**: Crouch
* **Triangle / Y**: Switch Weapon (cycle through equipped weapons)
* **L3 Click**: Walk Slowly / Deep Stealth Mode
* **R3 Click**: Look Behind (while running)
* **D-pad Up / Down**: Cycle Inventory Items (bottles, matches, med kits)
* **D-pad Left / Right**: Switch Crossbow Bolt Type
* **Options / Menu**: Pause / Map / Archives

### Starter Tips
1. **Stealth Kill to Save Ammo**: Approach an unaware Haunted (basic enemy) slowly while crouched or walking, and press R1/RB from behind to perform a stealth kill. This uses no ammo and is silent.
2. **Throw Bottles to Distract**: Bottles found in the environment can be thrown to create a noise distraction. Haunted will move toward the sound, turning their backs to you. Immediately follow up with a stealth kill.
3. **Burn Bodies with Matches**: Downed enemies will reanimate unless set on fire. Walk over a downed body and press R1/RB to ignite it with a match. This permanently eliminates the enemy and may drop gel or ammo. Matches are limited — conserve them.
4. **Green Gel is the Upgrade Currency**: Collect all green gel you find. It is used at the Upgrade Chair in the Safe Room to permanently upgrade Sebastian's health, stamina, stealth, and weapon efficiency. Prioritize Health and Stamina upgrades early.
5. **The Safe Room**: Progress frequently checkpoints the game into the Safe Room — Beacon Mental Hospital's basement bathroom. Use the Upgrade Chair here to spend gel. The nurse's locker room contains lockers that can be opened with Locker Keys.
6. **Smash White Statues for Locker Keys**: Small white porcelain statues are hidden throughout each chapter. Smash them by kicking or shooting to reveal a Locker Key inside. Keys open lockers in the Safe Room which contain resources, ammo, and gel.
7. **The Agony Crossbow**: The crossbow is Sebastian's most versatile weapon. It fires multiple bolt types (Shock, Freeze, Harpoon, Flash, Explosive, Smoke). Crossbow bolts can be crafted at the Workbench using parts found in the world.
8. **Craft Bolts at Workbenches**: Workbenches appear throughout the world. Collect bolt parts (syringes, coils, canisters) and combine them at a workbench to craft additional crossbow bolts of any unlocked type.
9. **Spike Traps**: Wire traps are scattered throughout the game. They will kill Sebastian instantly if he runs through them. Crouch and approach slowly to disarm them, yielding a snare trap item you can reuse.
10. **Chapter Select**: After completing the game, you can replay any chapter via Chapter Select to collect missed items, Locker Keys, or collectibles.

## Part 2: Walkthrough Chapters 1 to 15

## Chapter 1: An Emergency Call

### Objective: Investigate Beacon Mental Hospital

#### Arrival at the Hospital
Detective Sebastian Castellanos arrives at Beacon Mental Hospital with partners Kidman and Connelly following reports of multiple casualties. Walk with Kidman through the front entrance.
- The hospital has suffered a catastrophic incident. Bodies litter the corridors.
- Follow Kidman to the security room. Interact with the bank of security monitors to observe the footage.
- A cutscene triggers: Sebastian witnesses a pale, hooded figure (Ruvik) slaughter an entire room of police officers in a single motion. Before Sebastian can react, Ruvik appears directly behind him.

#### Waking in the Dungeon
Sebastian regains consciousness hanging upside down from a meat hook on a rotating rack in a dark, industrial slaughterhouse basement.
- A large man with a chainsaw enters and begins butchering a hanging body nearby.
- **Objective**: Escape the hook. Swing Sebastian back and forth using the left stick until he reaches the hanging cord on the rack's right side. Press R1/RB to grab the cord and cut yourself free.
- Sebastian falls to the floor. Move immediately — the chainsaw man will investigate.

#### Escaping the Slaughterhouse
Crouch and move through the hanging carcasses. The butcher (chainsaw enemy) patrols the slaughterhouse. He cannot be killed at this stage.
- **Strategy**: Stay crouched and move slowly between the rows of hanging carcasses. Use them as visual cover.
- Navigate through the door on the far side of the slaughterhouse into the adjacent corridor.
- **Collectible — Map Fragment**: On the floor of the first side room off the main slaughterhouse corridor.
- Collect the **matches** from the shelf in the supply room.

#### The Drain Tunnel
Follow the path into a flooded drainage tunnel. Wade through the shallow water.
- Laura (a spider-legged creature) will appear and pursue Sebastian through the tunnel. She cannot be defeated here.
- **Sprint** down the flooded tunnel without stopping. Turn left at the tunnel fork, then push through the door at the end.
- Climb the ladder at the tunnel's end to emerge outside.

---

### Objective: Survive and Escape

#### The Rural Road
Sebastian emerges into a rain-soaked countryside road at night. This is the first exterior area.
- A police cruiser is nearby. Collect the **handgun ammo** from the car's front seat.
- **Collectible — Newspaper**: On the ground next to the crashed police car.
- Follow the road forward. A truck will crash nearby — move past the wreckage.

#### The Ruined Village (First Enemy Encounter)
The road leads into a ruined village. A single Haunted (reanimated corpse-like enemy) is feeding near the first building.
- **Stealth Kill Practice**: Move crouched slowly behind the Haunted. When the prompt appears, press R1/RB for a stealth kill.
- Collect the **handgun ammo** and **green gel** dropped.
- Two more Haunted are ahead in the village center. Use the same stealth approach.
- **Collectible — Document**: Inside the first stone building on the right. The document is on a table.

#### The Burning Village
The village ahead is on fire. Haunted patrol the burning streets.
- Move along the left side of the road using parked wagons and fences for cover.
- A Haunted is crouching near a burning barrel. Stealth kill from behind.
- **Key Item — First Locker Key**: A white statue is on the wooden fence to the left of the burning barn. Kick or shoot it to collect the Locker Key.
- Enter the barn through the side door. Collect the **shotgun shells** from the barrel inside.
- Exit the barn's rear and follow the path up the hill as the chapter ends.

## Chapter 2: Remnants

### Objective: Find Survivors

#### Waking in the Field
Sebastian wakes in an open field. He is injured. The environment has shifted — the nightmare world is now fully apparent in its distorted geography.
- Walk forward along the dirt path. The path is linear.
- **Collectible — Missing Person Poster**: Nailed to the fence post at the field's edge.

#### The Farm Outbuildings
The path leads to a cluster of farm outbuildings. Two Haunted patrol the area.
- **Strategy**: Crouch in the tall grass on the left side of the farmyard. The first Haunted walks a loop near the barn. Wait for him to face away, step behind him, and stealth kill.
- The second Haunted stands near the water trough. Throw a bottle from your inventory (D-pad item) to the right side of the yard to lure him away, then stealth kill from behind.
- **Collectible — Green Gel**: In the wooden crate near the water trough. Kick the crate open.
- Enter the farmhouse through the front door.

#### The Farmhouse Interior
The farmhouse is empty but contains resources.
- Collect the **handgun ammo** from the kitchen table.
- Collect the **matches** from the fireplace mantle.
- **Collectible — Map Fragment**: Pinned to the wall of the upstairs bedroom.
- A **spike wire trap** is stretched across the top of the stairs. Crouch and disarm it by pressing R1/RB when next to the wire. Yields 1 snare trap.
- Exit through the back door.

---

### Objective: Reach the Safe Room

#### The Safe Room Introduction
Sebastian encounters the hospital basement Safe Room for the first time via a transition cutscene.
- **The Upgrade Chair**: Interact with the chair in the center of the room. A nurse appears and straps Sebastian in. A menu appears allowing you to spend accumulated **Green Gel** on upgrades.
  - **Recommended Early Upgrades**: Health (Level 2), Stamina (Level 2), Stealth Kill (Level 2 — increases range and reduces detection risk).
- **The Locker Room**: Use your collected Locker Keys at the bank of numbered lockers. Each opened locker contains resources (ammo, gel, items).
- Examine the map board on the wall to track collectible counts by chapter.
- Exit the Safe Room through the door when ready.

---

### Objective: Find Joseph and Leslie

#### The Church Road
Exit the Safe Room transition back to the exterior world. A new path leads toward a distant church.
- Three Haunted patrol the church road. Two are clustered together near the gate — do not attempt a double stealth kill; throw a bottle beyond the gate to split them up.
- **Collectible — Newspaper**: On the stone bench to the left of the church gate.
- Enter the church through the main doors.

#### The Church
The church is occupied by three Haunted wandering between the pews.
- Navigate along the right-side pew aisle. Stealth kill the Haunted nearest to you, then move forward.
- **Collectible — Document (Personal Document)**: On the altar at the front of the church.
- Exit through the vestry door on the left of the altar.

#### The Cemetery
The vestry exits into a cemetery.
- **Spike Wire Trap**: A trap is stretched between two headstones on the main path. Disarm it by crouching.
- Joseph and Leslie are visible ahead. A cutscene triggers. Chapter 2 ends.

## Chapter 3: Claws of the Horde

### Objective: Get Through the Village

#### The Open Village Area
This chapter features the first major open combat area. Multiple Haunted are scattered through the village, and reinforcements will appear.
- **Resource Priority**: Check every building — most contain ammo, gel, or bolt parts on shelves and counters.
- **Key Item — Agony Crossbow**: Joseph gives Sebastian the Agony Crossbow at the chapter's start. Equip it using Triangle/Y. This is now the game's primary tool for environmental kills.
- **Collectible — Map Fragment**: On the wooden counter inside the first house on the left.
- **Collectible — Audio Tape**: On the table in the second house on the right.

#### Horde Encounter — Village Square
A large group of Haunted will converge on the village square when triggered.
- **Strategy**: Before triggering the horde, check the square for traps — two spike wire traps are already deployed. Note their positions.
- Use a **Shock Bolt** (crossbow) on the first Haunted to stun and down them, then approach for a stealth finish if ammo allows.
- Use the **Explosive Bolt** on a cluster of three or more Haunted to take multiple out simultaneously.
- **Environmental Kills**: Multiple explosive red barrels are scattered around the square. Lure Haunted near them and shoot the barrel.
- **Collectible — Missing Person Poster**: On the bulletin board on the north side of the square.

---

### Objective: Reach the Church Bell

#### The Village Outskirts
Push through the village toward the hill beyond.
- **Key Item — First White Statue (Locker Key)**: On the small wall to the right of the wooden bridge over the stream. Smash it.
- Cross the bridge and follow the path up the hillside.

#### The Ruined Farmhouse — Barn Fight
A cutscene shows the barn doors slam shut, trapping Sebastian inside. Waves of Haunted pour in.
- **Strategy**: This is a forced combat encounter. Use Handgun ammo for single targets. Deploy your disarmed snare traps near the barn doors before they open to catch incoming Haunted.
- A **Haunted with a torch** will appear — priority target. If the torch enemy ignites other Haunted, they become flaming and move faster.
- Once the wave is defeated, the barn doors open. Exit immediately.

#### Sniper Alley
The path through the village continues past open ground. A Haunted sniper is positioned in a tower at the far end.
- **Strategy**: Move from cover to cover (hay bales, walls) in a crouch. Do not run in the open — the sniper has long range.
- Use a **Harpoon Bolt** (crossbow) to pin the sniper to the wall of the tower from a safe distance, eliminating him.
- Collect the **sniper rifle** dropped by the sniper if approached.

---

### Objective: Ring the Church Bell

#### The Church Approach
Navigate through the churchyard. Three spike wire traps are set on the main path — disarm them all.
- **Collectible — Newspaper**: On the wooden stump just outside the church's north door.
- **Collectible — Green Gel (Large)**: Inside the small chapel annex attached to the church's west wall.
- Enter the church through the main entrance.

#### The Church Bell Tower
Climb the bell tower stairs to the top. A large Haunted with a giant axe guards the bell platform.
- **Strategy**: Use the Freeze Bolt (crossbow) to immobilize the axe Haunted, then approach and perform a stealth kill while frozen.
- Pull the bell rope. Chapter 3 ends with a cutscene.

## Chapter 4: The Patient

### Objective: Navigate the Hospital

#### Hospital Corridors (Interior Shift)
The world shifts back to a hospital-like environment — distorted, blood-soaked corridors resembling Beacon Mental Hospital but wrong in their proportions and layout.
- **Collectible — Document**: On the nurses' station counter in the first corridor.
- Two Haunted wander the hallway. Stealth kill the first from behind. Use a bottle distraction on the second.
- Try every door — unlocked rooms contain ammo, gel, and bolt parts on shelves.

#### The Patient Ward
A large ward with multiple beds. Haunted are lying on beds and may or may not be active.
- **Warning**: Some Haunted in beds are dormant and will not activate unless you run or make noise. Walk slowly through the ward.
- **Collectible — Map Fragment**: On the windowsill at the ward's far end.
- A Haunted with a head lamp is active and patrolling. She will detect Sebastian in the dark with her beam — stay outside the light cone.
- Exit through the double doors at the ward's end.

---

### Objective: Find the Way Through

#### The Operating Theater
A large operating theater with multiple Haunted inside.
- **Environmental Hazard**: Electrified water on the operating theater floor. Do not walk into it.
- Use the **Shock Bolt** (crossbow) aimed at the water to create an area shock that downs all Haunted standing in the water simultaneously.
- Collect the **green gel** from the supply cabinet and **handgun ammo** from the surgical tray.
- **Collectible — Personal Document**: Pinned to the corkboard near the exit door.

#### The Basement Corridors
Follow the stairs down into the basement.
- A single, powerful enemy (a Haunted with a blade arm) patrols the basement.
- **Strategy**: This enemy cannot be stealth killed from behind like standard Haunted. Use a Freeze Bolt to immobilize, then use 2–3 shotgun shells to the head, or use a Flash Bolt to stun and then stomp.
- **Key Item — Locker Key (White Statue)**: On the basement shelf, left of the generator room door.
- Interact with the generator panel to restore power to the elevator.
- Take the elevator up to complete the chapter transition.

---

### Objective: Escape Ruvik

#### The Ruvik Chase
Ruvik appears directly and pursues Sebastian through a shifting corridor sequence.
- Ruvik is indestructible at this stage. Do not engage — only run.
- Sprint forward and follow the branching path left at every fork.
- Slide under the collapsing ceiling segment by pressing X/A.
- The chase ends when Sebastian dives through a door. A cutscene triggers. Chapter 4 ends.

## Chapter 5: Inner Recesses

### Objective: Navigate the Underground

#### The Underground Passage
Sebastian descends into a deep underground area beneath the hospital — cave-like tunnels reinforced with old stone.
- Activate careful movement — the tunnels are narrow and echo.
- **Collectible — Map Fragment**: On a rock ledge just to the left of the first lantern in the tunnel.
- **Collectible — Document**: On a wooden crate in the second tunnel junction.

#### The Flooded Cavern
The tunnel opens into a large partially-flooded cavern.
- Several Haunted wade through the water.
- **Strategy**: Use the Shock Bolt on the water surface to stun all enemies in the flooded area simultaneously. Follow up with stealth kills on downed enemies.
- **Collectible — Newspaper**: On a wooden platform raised above the waterline at the cavern's north end.
- Collect the **shotgun shells** and **green gel** from the platform.
- Climb the rope on the far wall to the upper level.

---

### Objective: Find a Way Out

#### The Underground Village Ruins
The upper level opens into ancient ruins — stone buildings partially collapsed, with burning torches.
- Three Haunted patrol the ruins in loose patterns. Stealth kill each individually.
- **Collectible — Missing Person Poster**: Nailed to the stone wall between the second and third ruin buildings.
- **Key Item — Locker Key (White Statue)**: On the stone altar in the central ruin building. Smash it.
- Collect the **crossbow bolt parts** from the workbench inside the largest ruin.
- **Workbench**: This is the first workbench encounter. Interact with it to craft additional crossbow bolts using collected parts. Recommended: craft 2 Shock Bolts and 1 Freeze Bolt.

#### The Spike Pit Trap Area
The path leads through a series of rooms with elaborate spike-pit floor traps.
- **First Pit**: The pit is triggered by a pressure plate in the center of the room. Walk along the left-side wall edge to bypass the pressure plate entirely.
- **Second Pit**: The plate here is unavoidable. Use your disarmed snare trap to prop the mechanism before crossing, then retrieve it.
- Collect the **handgun ammo** from the room's far shelf.

---

### Objective: Defeat the Chain-Spike Trap Enemy

#### The Armored Haunted Encounter
A heavily armored Haunted blocks the exit passage. Standard weapons deal minimal damage to his front.
- **Strategy**:
  - Use a Freeze Bolt to immobilize him.
  - While frozen, sprint around behind him and use a stored snare trap on the floor behind his feet.
  - When he thaws and steps into the snare, he will be briefly stuck. Shoot him in the back of the head with the handgun (3–4 shots) or one shotgun blast.
  - Alternatively, lure him into the spike pit trap room and trigger the pressure plate while he is walking over it.
- Collect the **green gel** and **shotgun shells** he drops.

---

### Objective: Reach the Surface

#### The Ascent Path
Follow the ascending path through the stone tunnels back toward the surface.
- **Collectible — Audio Tape**: On a crate in the tunnel's last side chamber before the exit.
- A final spike wire trap is stretched across the exit passage. Disarm it.
- Emerge through the cellar door into the open air. A cutscene triggers as Sebastian sees the scale of the nightmare world around him. Chapter 5 ends.

## Chapter 6: Losing Grip on Ourselves

### Objective: Survive the Asylum

#### The Distorted Asylum Corridors
Sebastian finds himself inside a twisted version of Beacon Mental Hospital. The corridors have shifted — walls are at wrong angles, floors slope, and rooms repeat incorrectly.
- Walk forward along the only accessible corridor. A loud banging comes from behind a sealed door on the left — do not investigate.
- **Collectible — Document**: On the floor of the first accessible room on the right, next to an overturned gurney.
- Three Haunted patrol the main corridor ahead. Two are facing away; one patrols toward you.
  - **Strategy**: Wait for the patrol Haunted to turn, then stealth kill the first two in sequence before he returns. Hide in the alcove recess on the right when the third turns to face the corridor.

#### The Patient Cells Block
A long block of padded cells, some open, some sealed.
- Haunted occupy several open cells. They emerge when Sebastian passes the door threshold.
- **Strategy**: Do not run. Walk slowly past each open cell doorway. Haunted inside will not react unless they hear sprint noise or see Sebastian in the corridor.
- **Collectible — Missing Person Poster**: Pinned to the cell block guard station at the corridor's midpoint.
- **Key Item — Locker Key (White Statue)**: On the nurse's cart at the far end of the cell block. Smash it.
- At the corridor's end, a large gate is locked. Pull the lever on the right wall to open it.

---

### Objective: Find a Way to the Courtyard

#### The Library
Sebastian enters a large multi-story library — rows of shelves filled with distorted books, with a dark gap where some of the upper balconies have collapsed.
- **Collectible — Newspaper**: On the reading table in the library's center section.
- **Collectible — Map Fragment**: On the upper balcony, reached by climbing the spiral staircase on the library's left side.
- Four Haunted are in the library. Two are on the ground floor, two on the upper balcony.
  - Stealth kill both ground-floor Haunted before ascending the staircase.
  - Use a Smoke Bolt on the upper balcony to obscure vision, then stealth kill both upper Haunted in the smoke.

#### The Library Collapse
As Sebastian descends back to the ground floor, the library structure begins to collapse.
- Sprint immediately toward the main exit door on the far ground floor wall. Do not stop.
- Bookshelves will topple across the path — vault them using X/A.
- Reach the door and interact with the handle to force it open. Exit into the exterior courtyard.

---

### Objective: Cross the Courtyard

#### The Asylum Exterior Courtyard
An open courtyard between wings of the asylum. Guards (Haunted) patrol the courtyard in structured formations.
- **Collectible — Audio Tape**: On the stone bench in the courtyard's southeast corner.
- **Strategy**: Use the courtyard's central hedgerow as a dividing cover line. Move along the hedgerow's near side to bypass the first patrol cluster. At the hedgerow gap, wait for the two-guard patrol to separate, then slip through.
- A locked gate on the courtyard's north wall blocks the exit. Locate the guard carrying the key — the large Haunted near the fountain.
  - Stealth kill the large Haunted from behind or use a Freeze Bolt followed by two shotgun shots to the head.
  - Collect the **courtyard gate key** from the body.
- Unlock the gate and proceed.

#### Laura's Return
Beyond the gate, Laura (the spider-creature) descends from the ceiling. She cannot be killed conventionally here.
- **Strategy**: Sprint past Laura toward the door at the end of the hallway. Do not engage.
- Sebastian slips through the door as Laura reaches him. A cutscene triggers. Chapter 6 ends.

## Chapter 7: The Keeper

### Objective: Navigate the Trap Maze

#### The Underground Trap Corridor
Sebastian enters an elaborate trap-filled underground dungeon — the domain of The Keeper, an enemy who uses the environment itself as a weapon.
- **Warning**: This section contains the highest density of spike wire traps in the game. Move at a crouch continuously. Check every doorway threshold before stepping through.
- **Collectible — Document**: On a wooden table in the first open room off the main corridor.
- Disarm all spike wire traps you encounter. Each yields a reusable snare trap — you will need them for The Keeper fight.
- **Collectible — Map Fragment**: In the second side room on the right, on a stone shelf.

#### The Safe Storage Room
A room with a large iron safe in the center is encountered mid-dungeon.
- Interacting with the safe triggers a brief Safe Room visit. Use this opportunity to upgrade at the chair.
  - **Recommended Upgrades for Chapter 7**: Bottle Throw (upgrade to Level 2 for wider throw arc), Handgun (Firepower Level 2).
- Exit the safe room and return to the dungeon.

---

### Objective: Defeat The Keeper (Boss 1)

#### The Keeper — Introduction
The Keeper is a massive enemy wearing a large metal safe as a head, armed with a barbed wire and hook weapon. He appears at the end of the trap corridor.
- **Phase 1 Strategy**:
  - The Keeper moves slowly but has extremely high health. Direct weapon fire is largely ineffective.
  - Use the trap corridor's own spike wire traps against him. Lead The Keeper through the corridor and trigger traps by luring him into them (he will blunder into activated traps).
  - Alternatively, use planted snare traps from your inventory on the floor in his patrol path. When he steps into a snare, rapidly shoot the back of his head with the handgun (6–8 shots) or the shotgun (2 blasts).
- **Phase 2 (After First Health Bar)**:
  - The Keeper becomes faster and more aggressive. He will teleport to a new position after taking heavy damage.
  - Use Freeze Bolts to immobilize him momentarily. While frozen, plant a snare and position behind him for a burst of shots.
  - **Final Phase**: When The Keeper's health is nearly depleted, he retreats to a pressure plate. Do not follow him onto the plate. Instead, use a Harpoon Bolt from distance to pin him to the wall, then shoot his head.
- The Keeper is defeated (temporarily). Collect the **green gel** and **Keeper's key** he drops.

---

### Objective: Reach the Surface

#### Post-Boss Corridor
Use The Keeper's key on the large iron door at the dungeon's far end.
- **Collectible — Missing Person Poster**: On the wall just past the iron door.
- **Collectible — Newspaper**: On a crate in the final room before the exit shaft.
- Climb the iron ladder to the surface. A cutscene plays as Sebastian emerges. Chapter 7 ends.

## Chapter 8: A Planted Seed Will Grow

### Objective: Escape the Burning Village

#### The Village in Flames
Sebastian finds himself in a burning rural village. The environment is actively on fire — burning buildings collapse, and flaming debris blocks paths.
- **Environmental Hazard**: Flaming debris and fire patches on the ground will damage Sebastian if walked through. Watch your footing carefully.
- **Collectible — Document**: On the ground outside the first intact building on the left, where it was dropped by a fleeing cultist.
- Two Haunted are burning (already on fire from the environment). They move faster than standard Haunted — do not attempt stealth. Use the handgun to knock them down, then sprint past.
- Navigate through the burning alley toward the village outskirts. Follow the visible clear path through the smoke.

#### The Farm Road
Emerge from the burning village onto a farm road. The fires are behind you.
- **Collectible — Map Fragment**: On the road's left shoulder, pinned under a fallen sign post.
- A group of four Haunted are gathered in the farmyard ahead. They are alert but have not spotted Sebastian.
  - **Strategy**: Move to the far right side of the road and approach via the tree line. Two of the four Haunted can be picked off with stealth kills. Use a bottle distraction to handle the remaining two.
- Collect the **handgun ammo** and **green gel** from the farmyard shed.

---

### Objective: Find the Lighthouse

#### The Cliff Path
A path winds along a seaside cliff. The world has shifted to a coastal environment.
- **Collectible — Audio Tape**: On the rocks at the cliff path's midpoint, overlooking the water.
- **Collectible — Newspaper**: On a wooden bench near the cliff's edge above the beach.
- Three Haunted patrol the cliff path. The ledge is narrow — do not allow an enemy to push Sebastian off the edge.
  - **Strategy**: Stealth kill each Haunted from behind and toward the inland side of the path.

#### The Beach
The path descends to a rocky beach at the base of the cliffs.
- Laura's arms emerge from the sand, reaching for Sebastian as he walks across the beach.
- **Strategy**: Walk quickly but do not run or stop. Walking at a moderate pace keeps the arms from triggering. If arms grab Sebastian's leg, rapidly mash X/A to pull free.
- Reach the beach's far end and climb the rope ladder up the cliff face.

---

### Objective: Enter the Lighthouse

#### The Lighthouse Approach
A tall lighthouse stands on the cliff promontory. Multiple Haunted guard the lighthouse road.
- **Key Item — Locker Key (White Statue)**: On the stone wall to the right of the lighthouse gate. Smash it.
- **Collectible — Personal Document**: On the lighthouse keeper's desk in the keeper's cottage adjacent to the lighthouse.
- Haunted (4 total) patrol the lighthouse perimeter. Two guard the entrance. Use Smoke Bolts to blind the entrance guards, then stealth kill each in the smoke.
- Enter the lighthouse through the main door.

#### Inside the Lighthouse
Climb the spiral staircase to the top of the lighthouse.
- Each landing has one Haunted. Stealth kill at each landing before ascending.
- At the top, interact with the lighthouse beacon. A cutscene triggers — the beam sweeps the water, revealing something massive in the ocean. Chapter 8 ends.

## Chapter 9: The Cruelest Intentions

### Objective: Find the Survivors

#### The Distorted Manor
Sebastian finds himself in a large, upscale manor — ornate furniture and paintings distorted by the STEM nightmare. The manor is modeled on Ruvik's childhood home.
- **Collectible — Document (Personal Document)**: On the writing desk in the manor's entrance foyer.
- Walk through the foyer into the main hallway. Doors to the left and right are sealed. The path leads straight.
- Two Haunted are in the dining room. A chandelier hangs above the dining table — shoot the chandelier chain with the handgun to drop it on both Haunted simultaneously.
- Collect the **handgun ammo** and **green gel** from the dining room sideboard.

#### The Manor's Upper Floor
Climb the grand staircase to the upper floor.
- **Collectible — Map Fragment**: On the small table in the upper hallway, left of the staircase landing.
- **Collectible — Newspaper**: In the library room on the upper floor's right side.
- A Haunted with a crossbow patrols the upper hallway. He can shoot at range.
  - Use a Flash Bolt to blind him, then close the distance for a stealth kill while blinded.
- **Key Item — Locker Key (White Statue)**: On the windowsill of the upper-floor bedroom. Smash it.

---

### Objective: Find Ruvik's Laboratory

#### The Hidden Laboratory Entrance
A bookcase in the manor's study conceals a hidden door. Examine the bookcase and interact with the red book on the third shelf to trigger the mechanism.
- Descend the hidden staircase into Ruvik's personal laboratory beneath the manor.
- **Collectible — Document**: On the laboratory's main desk — Ruvik's research notes.
- Cutscene: Sebastian discovers the truth behind Ruvik's origin. As a child, Ruvik was trapped in a burning barn by his family. The trauma and his extraordinary mind allowed him to develop STEM. Sebastian sees a vision of Ruvik's past.

#### The Laboratory Escape
After the cutscene, the laboratory begins to destabilize. Haunted materialize throughout.
- **Strategy**: Do not stop to fight. Sprint from the lab through the staircase and back into the manor.
- The manor's front doors are now accessible. Burst through them to exit.
- A long chase sequence begins in the exterior grounds. Sprint forward and do not look back.
- Vault the iron fence at the grounds' edge using X/A. Sebastian tumbles into a ditch. Chapter 9 ends.

## Chapter 10: The Craftsman's Tools

### Objective: Navigate the Underground Facility

#### The Industrial Underground
Sebastian descends into a vast underground industrial facility — pipes, machinery, and walkways constructed from metal and stone. This is a Murkoff/Ruvik research installation from the real world, distorted inside STEM.
- **Collectible — Document**: On a metal desk just inside the facility's main entrance corridor.
- **Collectible — Map Fragment**: On the catwalk above the first machinery room, reached by climbing the metal ladder on the room's east wall.
- Multiple Haunted patrol the facility floor in wide, sweeping patterns.
  - **Strategy**: Use the machinery and pipe structures as cover. Move along the room's outer walls to bypass the central patrol cluster.

#### The Flooded Lower Level
A section of the facility is flooded with dark water. Wading slows Sebastian significantly.
- Use the Shock Bolt on the water ahead before entering to stun any Haunted wading in it.
- **Collectible — Newspaper**: On a submerged platform at the flooded section's center — only partially above water. Collect it while wading.
- Climb the ladder on the far wall to reach the dry upper walkway.

---

### Objective: Escape The Keeper

#### The Keeper Returns — Pursuit Mode
The Keeper reappears in the industrial facility and pursues Sebastian through the zone. Unlike the boss fight, he is not stoppable here.
- **Strategy**: Do not engage The Keeper. Sprint forward along the catwalk.
- When the catwalk section collapses, drop immediately (X/A) and run along the lower facility floor.
- **Collectible — Audio Tape**: On a crate just past the catwalk collapse section — grab it only if you have enough distance from The Keeper.
- Push through the heavy bulkhead door at the facility's far end and seal it behind you by interacting with the wheel valve.

#### The Storage Vault Puzzle
Beyond the bulkhead, a large vault door is sealed with a combination mechanism.
- Examine the three numbered plaques on the walls of the storage room: each plaque has a symbol corresponding to a number on the vault dial.
- **Vault Solution**: Rotate the dial to each number in the sequence shown on the plaques (left to right). Press R1/RB to confirm each position.
- The vault opens. Inside:
  - **Collectible — Personal Document**: On the vault's interior shelf.
  - **Key Item — Facility Keycard**: On the metal hook on the vault's interior right wall.
  - Collect the **green gel (large)** and **shotgun shells** from the shelving.

### Objective: Shut Down the Facility

#### The Control Room
Use the facility keycard on the reader beside the control room door.
- The control room has two Haunted technicians and one Haunted guard.
  - Stealth kill the guard first — he has his back to the door.
  - Use a bottle distraction to separate the two technicians, then stealth kill each.
- **Collectible — Missing Person Poster**: On the corkboard next to the main control console.
- Interact with the main control console and pull the emergency shutdown lever.
- A facility-wide alarm triggers. All remaining Haunted in the area become alerted.

#### The Alarm Escape Sequence
Multiple alerted Haunted pour into the facility. Sprint toward the exit marked by the flashing green emergency sign.
- Use the Explosive Bolt on any clustered Haunted blocking the corridor — do not stop for individual kills.
- Reach the emergency exit and push through. Sebastian climbs the external shaft ladder.
- Emerge at the surface. A cutscene triggers as Sebastian sees Ruvik in the distance. Chapter 10 ends.

## Chapter 11: Reunion

### Objective: Find the Survivors

#### The Ruined City Streets
Sebastian emerges into a ruined city environment — rubble-strewn streets, collapsed buildings, and overturned vehicles. This is the most expansive outdoor area in the game.
- **Collectible — Newspaper**: On the hood of an overturned police car at the street's first intersection.
- **Collectible — Map Fragment**: On the fire escape landing of the partially-collapsed building on the left side of the street, reached by climbing the fire escape ladder.
- Multiple Haunted patrol the ruined streets in loose groups. Three are gathered around a burning car at the second intersection.
  - **Environmental Kill**: Shoot the burning car's gas tank to trigger an explosion, eliminating all three Haunted at once.
  - Collect the **green gel** scattered near the car wreckage.

#### The Collapsed Building Interior
A path leads through a building that has partially fallen into the street. The interior is structurally unstable.
- **Warning**: Sections of the floor are weakened. Cracked floor panels will collapse if Sebastian runs over them. Walk across all cracked sections.
- **Collectible — Document**: On a reception desk in the building's ground floor lobby, visible through the rubble.
- **Key Item — Locker Key (White Statue)**: On the second floor of the collapsed building, on the window ledge of the northeast room. Smash it.
- Exit through the building's rear emergency exit onto the alley behind.

---

### Objective: Meet Joseph at the Rendezvous

#### The Underground Passage
The alley leads to a manhole cover. Interact with it to descend into the underground drainage network.
- The drainage tunnels are dark — multiple branching paths exist.
- Navigate by sound: follow the dripping water sounds toward the right-side passage at the first fork.
- **Collectible — Audio Tape**: On a ledge in the drainage tunnel's second chamber, above the waterline.
- Climb the ladder at the tunnel's end to emerge in the city's lower district.

#### Reunion with Joseph
A cutscene triggers in the lower district plaza. Sebastian finds Joseph. Joseph appears disoriented and unwell — Ruvik's influence within STEM is affecting him.
- Leslie is also present briefly but disappears after Ruvik appears.
- **Story Context**: It is revealed that Ruvik seeks a body to escape STEM with. Leslie, due to his mental instability, is a compatible host.

#### The Lower District — Haunted Ambush
After the cutscene, a large group of Haunted ambushes the plaza.
- **Strategy**: Use the plaza's pillars as cover. Prioritize the three Haunted entering from the south alley — they are the most aggressive.
- Use an Explosive Bolt on the cluster of four Haunted that rush from the east entrance simultaneously.
- **Collectible — Missing Person Poster**: On the stone pillar at the plaza's northwest corner — grab it after clearing the ambush.
- Exit through the north archway when the ambush is repelled.

### Objective: Reach the Safe House

#### The City's Northern Block
Follow Joseph through the northern city blocks. He guides Sebastian but moves slowly.
- **Collectible — Document (Personal Document)**: On the steps of the boarded-up building at the block's midpoint.
- A Haunted with a mounted machine gun turret occupies the rooftop above the block's far end.
  - **Strategy**: Do not run into the open street ahead — the turret covers the full street width. Move along the left-side building facades, staying in the shadow cover of the overhanging ledges.
  - Use a Harpoon Bolt from the covered position to pin the turret gunner to his post, disabling the threat.
- **Key Item — Locker Key (White Statue)**: On the alley wall behind the building the turret gunner occupies. Smash it.
- A cutscene triggers as Sebastian and Joseph reach a fortified building. Chapter 11 ends.

## Chapter 12: The Ride

### Objective: Reach the Central Tower

#### The Moving Platform Sequence
Sebastian finds himself on a large moving cart/platform traveling on rails through a shifting nightmare landscape. The environment scrolls past as the cart moves at a fixed pace.
- **Note**: This is a more action-focused chapter. Sebastian must fight enemies while the platform moves. Use available cover objects on the cart.
- Haunted jump onto the cart from platforms on both sides as it passes them.
  - Prioritize enemies from the right side first — they arrive in larger groups.
  - Use the Handgun for single-target close kills. Save Explosive Bolts for groups of 3 or more.
- **Collectible — Newspaper**: On the top of the supply crate at the rear of the cart. Grab it between enemy waves.
- **Collectible — Map Fragment**: On a post at the midpoint platform — the cart slows slightly here. Quickly grab it as you pass.

#### The Burning Bridge Crossing
The cart tracks lead across a large burning bridge. The bridge is partially collapsed.
- **Environmental Hazard**: Fire jets from the bridge's sides at timed intervals. Watch the pattern and sprint through gaps during the off-interval.
- The cart stops at the bridge's midpoint. Push the stuck mechanism lever to resume movement.
  - While stopped, three Haunted approach from the bridge on foot. Use the Shotgun for rapid point-blank kills before they reach the cart.
- **Collectible — Audio Tape**: On the bridge railing, near the stuck mechanism lever. Grab it while the cart is stopped.

---

### Objective: Defeat the Amalgam Alpha

#### The Amalgam Alpha — Boss Introduction
The cart arrives at a large industrial arena. A massive creature — the Amalgam Alpha, one of Ruvik's earliest failed STEM experiments — occupies the space. It is an enormous, flesh-based entity with multiple humanoid bodies fused together.
- **Phase 1 — Weak Points**:
  - The Amalgam has three glowing red weak points visible on its body: one on the torso, one on the left flank, and one on the back.
  - Hit all three weak points with high-damage weapons (Shotgun, Sniper Rifle) to damage the creature.
  - The Amalgam moves slowly but has a sweeping charge attack. When it charges, sprint to the side and do not sprint backward — its charge covers a straight line.
- **Phase 2 (After First Health Bar)**:
  - The Amalgam begins spinning, making weak points harder to hit. Wait for it to stop after each spin before firing.
  - **Strategy**: Use Freeze Bolts to halt its spinning momentarily. While frozen, aim at the exposed torso weak point and unload Shotgun shells.
- **Phase 3 (Final)**:
  - The Amalgam becomes enraged. Its charge speed increases significantly.
  - Use the Sniper Rifle (if available) for long-range hits on weak points while staying at maximum arena distance.
  - When all weak points are destroyed, the Amalgam collapses.
- Collect the substantial **green gel** and **sniper ammo** dropped after the fight.
- A cutscene triggers as the arena shifts. Chapter 12 ends.

## Chapter 13: Casualties

### Objective: Navigate Back to the Hospital

#### The Distorted Hospital Re-Entry
Sebastian finds himself back in the nightmare version of Beacon Mental Hospital. The environment is more corrupted than ever — rooms are non-Euclidean, geometry repeats, and lights flicker.
- **Collectible — Document**: On the floor of the first repeating corridor, next to a wheelchair.
- Walk forward. Ruvik appears at the corridor's end and vanishes. Do not pursue.
- Two new enemy types appear in this chapter: **Reborn Laura** (a more powerful variant of Laura) and **Vigi** (floating, ghostlike enemies that cannot be killed with normal weapons — only the Flashlight or Flash Bolts will temporarily repel them).

#### The Vigi Encounter
Three Vigi float through the ward corridor ahead.
- **Strategy**: Vigi are invulnerable to bullets. Equip the Flash Bolt (crossbow) and fire one at the center of a Vigi cluster to send them retreating. They will return after a few seconds — use the time they are repelled to sprint through the corridor.
- Do not stop moving in the Vigi section — alternating Flash Bolts with sprinting is the most efficient method.
- **Collectible — Missing Person Poster**: On the wall at the ward's far end, just past the last Vigi patrol zone.

### Objective: Find Leslie

#### The Basement Descent
A staircase at the ward's end leads to the hospital basement — a flooded, dark lower level.
- Use the Shock Bolt on the water before entering — two Haunted are wading in the flooded basement.
- **Collectible — Map Fragment**: On a submerged platform at the basement's center.
- **Collectible — Audio Tape**: On a dry shelf on the basement's far wall, above the water level.
- **Key Item — Locker Key (White Statue)**: On a metal cart in the basement's northeast corner. Smash it.

#### Reborn Laura Encounter
Reborn Laura (faster, more aggressive than the original) appears at the basement exit.
- **Key Mechanic**: Reborn Laura is repelled and damaged by fire. She cannot be hurt by bullets.
- **Strategy**: Use matches to ignite the oil-soaked floor in the corridor ahead of her — she will not cross burning floor sections. This temporarily halts her pursuit and damages her.
- Alternatively, use the **Flamethrower** (if found earlier in the chapter on a shelf) to repel and damage her directly.
- Lead her into the burning section, exit through the far door, and seal it behind you.

### Objective: Stop Ruvik

#### The Central Shaft
A large vertical shaft connects the basement to the hospital's upper floors via a service elevator.
- Interact with the elevator controls. The elevator rises slowly.
- During the ascent, Haunted climb the shaft walls and leap onto the elevator platform.
  - Kill or knock each Haunted off the platform edge before they can grab Sebastian.
  - Use the Shotgun for knock-back effect — each blast will push a Haunted off the platform edge.
- **Collectible — Newspaper**: On the elevator platform floor, near the southeast corner. Pick up before the first Haunted arrive.
- The elevator reaches the top. Exit through the shaft's upper door.

#### The Confrontation with Joseph
A cutscene triggers. Joseph, fully under Ruvik's influence, confronts Sebastian. He attacks.
- **Joseph Encounter**: Joseph is not to be killed. The goal is to subdue him.
  - Use non-lethal approaches: Flash Bolts to blind, Freeze Bolts to immobilize, then approach and hold R1/RB to perform a subduing interaction.
  - Do not use the Shotgun or fire lethal weapon blasts — this forces the encounter into a repeat.
- After subdueing Joseph, a cutscene plays. Leslie is taken by Ruvik. Chapter 13 ends.

## Chapter 14: Ulterior Motives

### Objective: Find Ruvik

#### Kidman's Revelation
A cutscene plays. The chapter opens with Kidman's perspective. It is revealed that Kidman is a Mobius agent tasked with recovering Ruvik's STEM research and eliminating Ruvik himself. She did not enter STEM to save Sebastian.

#### The Real Beacon Hospital Corridors
Sebastian moves through a half-real, half-nightmare version of Beacon's upper floors.
- **Collectible — Document**: On the nursing station desk in the first real-world corridor section.
- **Collectible — Map Fragment**: In the supply room off the main corridor — the door is unlocked.
- New Haunted variant: **The Sadist** (returning from earlier chapters) is positioned at the corridor junction. He is fast and aggressive.

- **Collectible — Personal Document (Kidman's Notes)**: On a metal cabinet in the room adjacent to the Sadist's position. Collect after he is dealt with.

### Objective: Access the STEM Chamber

#### The Ruvik Confrontation Corridor
Sebastian confronts Ruvik directly in a long, distorted corridor.
- Ruvik fires mental blasts at Sebastian. These appear as shockwaves on the floor — dodge sideways using X/A to avoid each wave.

Sebastian reaches a large circular antechamber with the STEM machine's outer housing visible through a glass wall.

- **Collectible — Document (STEM Research Report)**: On the main console. This reveals Mobius's role in funding and exploiting Ruvik's research.
- **Key Item — Locker Key (White Statue)**: On the antechamber floor near the east wall. Smash it.
- Interact with the main console to initiate the STEM machine shutdown sequence. A cutscene triggers — the shutdown fails. Ruvik appears inside the STEM chamber and escapes into Leslie's body. Kidman attempts to stop him but fails. Chapter 14 ends.

## Chapter 15: An Evil Within

### Objective: Stop Ruvik

#### The Final STEM World
Sebastian is pulled back into STEM one final time. The nightmare world is collapsing — environments shift rapidly between locations seen throughout the game. The world is fragmenting.
- Walk forward. The path is largely linear as the environment dissolves around Sebastian.
- **Collectible — Document (Final Research Notes)**: On a desk that materializes briefly in the shifting environment. Grab it quickly before it dissolves.
- Multiple Haunted appear from dissolving walls. Do not stop to fight — push through, using Explosive Bolts to clear clustered groups blocking the path.

#### The Ruined Safe Room
Sebastian finds himself in a distorted, collapsing version of the Safe Room.
- **Final Upgrade Opportunity**: Interact with the Upgrade Chair one last time. Spend all remaining green gel.
  - **Recommended Final Upgrades**: Handgun (Firepower Level 5 cap), Health (maximum), Sniper Rifle (Firepower upgrade if unlocked).
- The Safe Room collapses. Exit through the dissolving door.

---

### Objective: Defeat Ruvik (Final Boss)

#### Ruvik — Phase 1 (Human Form)
Ruvik appears in the final arena — a collapsing amalgamation of all STEM environments. He confronts Sebastian in his human form.
- **Phase 1 Strategy**:
  - Ruvik teleports rapidly around the arena. He fires homing mental bolts that track Sebastian's movement.
  - Do not sprint in straight lines — the homing bolts adjust to straight-line movement. Instead, run in curved arcs to cause the bolts to curve away.
  - Ruvik is briefly stationary when he charges a heavy attack. This is the window to fire. Use the Sniper Rifle for precision hits during these stationary moments.
  - Hit Ruvik with 4–5 Sniper Rifle shots or 8–10 Handgun shots to deplete Phase 1 health.

#### Ruvik — Phase 2 (Amalgam Form)
Ruvik's consciousness merges with the STEM machine's data, creating a new Amalgam entity — a massive, shifting creature made of fused bodies and corrupted data.
- **Phase 2 Strategy**:
  - The Amalgam form has two large glowing red cores on its mass. Both must be destroyed.
  - The creature creates shockwave ground attacks by slamming its fists — the shockwave travels in a line. Jump or sidestep to avoid.
  - Freeze Bolts will briefly halt the creature's movement. Use them when it is about to slam to buy firing time.
  - Target the first core with Sniper Rifle shots (3–4 hits). The core will close. The creature flips, exposing the second core on its new front face.
  - Target the second core. The creature will attempt to grab Sebastian — if grabbed, rapidly mash X/A to pull free. After 3–4 Sniper shots, the second core collapses.
- The Amalgam form falls. A cutscene triggers.

---

### Objective: Escape the STEM World

#### The Final Escape Sequence
The STEM world is fully collapsing. Sebastian must sprint to the exit before it dissolves entirely.
- Sprint forward continuously along the dissolving path. Do not stop for any reason.
- Platforms ahead will vanish — jump (X/A) across gaps as they appear. Timing the jumps too early or too late results in falling.
- At the sequence's end, Sebastian reaches the STEM machine's core output point. A blinding white light engulfs everything.

#### Final Cutscene and Ending
Sebastian wakes in the real world — in Beacon Mental Hospital's lobby, now a crime scene. Days have passed. Kidman is present and debriefs with Mobius agents outside.
- Sebastian walks through the hospital exit into daylight. He is alive.
- A post-credits sequence plays: a figure resembling Ruvik in Leslie's body is seen walking away from the hospital in the distance.
- Credits roll. The main campaign is complete.

### New Game Plus and Completion Notes
> **New Game+**: Upon completing the game, a New Game+ mode unlocks. This carries over all of Sebastian's upgrades, collected weapons, and green gel into a new run. Collectibles and Locker Keys not found in the first run can be collected in NG+.
> **Chapter Select**: Available from the main menu after any chapter is completed. Progress (gel, collectibles) is retained. Replay individual chapters to collect missed items without starting a new full game.
> **Classic Mode**: A separate unlockable difficulty with no aiming reticle and limited saves (using matches as save tokens).
